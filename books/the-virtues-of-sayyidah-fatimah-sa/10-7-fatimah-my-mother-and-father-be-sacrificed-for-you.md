7) “Fatimah! My mother and father be sacrificed for you”
========================================================

> 18. عن ابن عمر رضي الله عنهما: أن النبي صلى الله عليه وسلم كان إذا
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
سافر كان آخر الناس عهدا به فاطمة، و إذا قدم من سفر كان أول الناس به
عهدا فاطمة رضي الله عنها. فقال لها رسول الله صلى الله عليه وسلم: فداك
أبي و أمي.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Abdullah bin Umar (ra) narrates that whenever the Holy Prophet (saw)
went on a journey, the last person he would speak to from his (saw)
family before setting off would be Fatimah (sa). When he (saw) returned
from a journey, the first person the Holy Prophet (saw) would come to
would be Fatimah (sa) and the Messenger of Allah (saw) would say to her
(sa), “(Oh Fatimah!) My mother and father be sacrificed for you.”

> 19. عن عمر بن الخطاب رضي الله عنه: أن النبي صلى الله عليه وسلم قال
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لفاطمة: فداك أبي و أمي.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Umar bin Khattab (ra) (also) narrates that the Holy Prophet (saw) used
to say to Fatimah (sa), “(Oh Fatimah!) My mother and father be
sacrificed for you.”
