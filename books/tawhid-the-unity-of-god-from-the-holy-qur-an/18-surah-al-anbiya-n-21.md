Surah Al-Anbiya, n.21
=====================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَمِ اتَّخَذُوا آلِهَةً مِنَ الْأَرْضِ هُمْ يُنْشِرُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Or have they taken gods from the earth who raise (the dead).
(21:21)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لَوْ كَانَ فِيهِمَا آلِهَةٌ إِلَّا اللَّهُ لَفَسَدَتَا ۚ فَسُبْحَانَ
اللَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَرْشِ عَمَّا يَصِفُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***If there had been in them any gods except Allah, they would both have
certainly been in a state of disorder; therefore glory be to Allah, the
Lord of the dominion***[^1]***, above what they attribute (to Him).
(21:22)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لَا يُسْأَلُ عَمَّا يَفْعَلُ وَهُمْ يُسْأَلُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***He cannot be questioned concerning what He does and they shall be
questioned. (21:23)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَمِ اتَّخَذُوا مِنْ دُونِهِ آلِهَةً ۖ قُلْ هَاتُوا بُرْهَانَكُمْ ۖ
هَٰذَا ذِكْرُ مَنْ مَعِيَ وَذِكْرُ مَنْ قَبْلِي ۗ بَلْ أَكْثَرُهُمْ
لَا يَعْلَمُونَ الْحَقَّ ۖ فَهُمْ مُعْرِضُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Or, have they taken gods besides Him? Say: Bring your proof; this is
the reminder of those with me and the reminder of those before***[^2]
***me. Nay! most of them do not know the truth, so they turn
aside.***[^3]***(21:24)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمَا أَرْسَلْنَا مِنْ قَبْلِكَ مِنْ رَسُولٍ إِلَّا نُوحِي إِلَيْهِ
أَنَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا أَنَا فَاعْبُدُونِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***And We did not send before you any messenger but We revealed to him
that there is no god but Me, therefore serve Me (21:25)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَقَالُوا اتَّخَذَ الرَّحْمَٰنُ وَلَدًا ۗ سُبْحَانَهُ ۚ بَلْ عِبَادٌ
مُكْرَمُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***And they say: The Beneficent Allah has taken to Himself a
son***[^4]***! Glory be to Him. Nay! they***[^5] ***are honored
servants*** ***(21:26)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لَا يَسْبِقُونَهُ بِالْقَوْلِ وَهُمْ بِأَمْرِهِ يَعْمَلُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***They do not precede Him in speech and (only) according to His
commandment do they act. (21:27)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَعْلَمُ مَا بَيْنَ أَيْدِيهِمْ وَمَا خَلْفَهُمْ وَلَا يَشْفَعُونَ
إِلَّا لِمَنِ ارْتَضَىٰ وَهُمْ مِنْ خَشْيَتِهِ مُشْفِقُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***He knows what is before them and what is behind them, and they do not
intercede except for him whom He approves and for fear of Him they
tremble. (21:28)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمَنْ يَقُلْ مِنْهُمْ إِنِّي إِلَٰهٌ مِنْ دُونِهِ فَذَٰلِكَ نَجْزِيهِ
جَهَنَّمَ ۚ كَذَٰلِكَ نَجْزِي الظَّالِمِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***And whoever of them should say: Surely I am a god besides Him, such a
one do We recompense with hell; thus do, We recompense the unjust.
(21:29)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَوَلَمْ يَرَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا أَنَّ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ
كَانَتَا رَتْقًا فَفَتَقْنَاهُمَا ۖ وَجَعَلْنَا مِنَ الْمَاءِ كُلَّ
شَيْءٍ حَيٍّ ۖ أَفَلَا يُؤْمِنُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Do not those who disbelieve see that the heavens and the earth were
closed up, but We have opened them; and We have made of water everything
living, will they not then believe?(21:30)***

[^1]: The supreme Authority

[^2]: Indicates Unity of God and universal aspect of Islam

[^3]: Verses from here to v. 27 refer to angel and holy men being taken
as the issues of God

[^4]: Taken to – or adopted – has begun beget - issuing

[^5]: The prophets and the angels whom they worshipped as gods or sons
and daughters of God
