Dedication
==========

“In the Name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful”

This book is dedicated to our beloved Holy Prophet Muhammad son of
Abdullah (S), Seal of the Prophets, his holy progeny - Ahl al-Bayt
(a.s.) - and all devoted scholars and intellectuals who endeavor in the
path of grandeur and dignity of Islam; Islamic universal awakening,
brotherhood, solidarity and to present the peaceful divine message of
monotheism, peace, harmony, coexistence, equality, freedom, justice,
love, and human dignity to rescue the mankind through enlightenment at
this critical juncture of our contemporary world in the twenty-first
century
