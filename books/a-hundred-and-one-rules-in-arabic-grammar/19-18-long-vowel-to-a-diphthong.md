18. Long vowel to a Diphthong.
==============================

If any of the two long vowel**و** **,** **ا** ** ** is proceeded by the
short vowel**–َ** ** ** , the long vowel changes its character to a
diphthong:

uu ---\>  ou    **دَور** **،** **دور**

ii   ---\>  ei   **دَين  ** ** ** **،** **دين**
