Surah Al-Maidah, n.5
====================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لَقَدْ كَفَرَ الَّذِينَ قَالُوا إِنَّ اللَّهَ هُوَ الْمَسِيحُ ابْنُ
مَرْيَمَ ۚ قُلْ فَمَنْ يَمْلِكُ مِنَ اللَّهِ شَيْئًا إِنْ أَرَادَ أَنْ
يُهْلِكَ الْمَسِيحَ ابْنَ مَرْيَمَ وَأُمَّهُ وَمَنْ فِي الْأَرْضِ
جَمِيعًا ۗ وَلِلَّهِ مُلْكُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ وَمَا
بَيْنَهُمَا ۚ يَخْلُقُ مَا يَشَاءُ ۚ وَاللَّهُ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ
قَدِيرٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Certainly they disbelieve who say: Surely, Allah-- He is the Messiah,
son of Marium. Say: Who then could control anything as against Allah
when He wished to destroy the Messiah son of Marium and his mother and
all those on the earth? And Allah's is the kingdom of the heavens and
the earth and what is between them; He creates what He pleases; and
Allah has power over all things (5:17)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لَقَدْ كَفَرَ الَّذِينَ قَالُوا إِنَّ اللَّهَ هُوَ الْمَسِيحُ ابْنُ
مَرْيَمَ ۖ وَقَالَ الْمَسِيحُ يَا بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ اعْبُدُوا اللَّهَ
رَبِّي وَرَبَّكُمْ ۖ إِنَّهُ مَنْ يُشْرِكْ بِاللَّهِ فَقَدْ حَرَّمَ
اللَّهُ عَلَيْهِ الْجَنَّةَ وَمَأْوَاهُ النَّارُ ۖ وَمَا
لِلظَّالِمِينَ مِنْ أَنْصَارٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Certainly they disbelieve who say: Surely Allah***[^1]***, He is the
Messiah, son of Marium; and the Messiah said: O Children of Israel!
serve Allah, my Lord and your Lord. Surely whoever associates (others)
with Allah, then Allah has forbidden to him the garden, and his abode is
the fire; and there shall be no helpers for the unjust***[^2]
***(5:72)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لَقَدْ كَفَرَ الَّذِينَ قَالُوا إِنَّ اللَّهَ ثَالِثُ ثَلَاثَةٍ ۘ
وَمَا مِنْ إِلَٰهٍ إِلَّا إِلَٰهٌ وَاحِدٌ ۚ وَإِنْ لَمْ يَنْتَهُوا
عَمَّا يَقُولُونَ لَيَمَسَّنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا مِنْهُمْ عَذَابٌ
أَلِيمٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Certainly they disbelieve who say: Surely Allah is the third (person)
of the three; and there is no god but the one Allah, and if they desist
not from what they say, a painful chastisement shall befall those among
them who disbelieve***[^3] ***(5:73)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَفَلَا يَتُوبُونَ إِلَى اللَّهِ وَيَسْتَغْفِرُونَهُ ۚ وَاللَّهُ
غَفُورٌ رَحِيمٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Will they not then turn to Allah and ask His forgiveness? And Allah
is Forgiving, Merciful (5:74)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَا الْمَسِيحُ ابْنُ مَرْيَمَ إِلَّا رَسُولٌ قَدْ خَلَتْ مِنْ قَبْلِهِ
الرُّسُلُ وَأُمُّهُ صِدِّيقَةٌ ۖ كَانَا يَأْكُلَانِ الطَّعَامَ ۗ
انْظُرْ كَيْفَ نُبَيِّنُ لَهُمُ الْآيَاتِ ثُمَّ انْظُرْ أَنَّىٰ
يُؤْفَكُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***The Messiah, son of Marium is but a messenger; messengers before him
have indeed passed away; and his mother was a truthful woman; they both
used to eat food. See how We make the communications clear to them, then
behold, how they are turned away (5:75)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قُلْ أَتَعْبُدُونَ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ مَا لَا يَمْلِكُ لَكُمْ ضَرًّا
وَلَا نَفْعًا ۚ وَاللَّهُ هُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Say: Do you serve besides Allah that which does not control for you
any harm, or any profit? And Allah-- He is the Hearing, the Knowing
(5:76)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قُلْ يَا أَهْلَ الْكِتَابِ لَا تَغْلُوا فِي دِينِكُمْ غَيْرَ الْحَقِّ
وَلَا تَتَّبِعُوا أَهْوَاءَ قَوْمٍ قَدْ ضَلُّوا مِنْ قَبْلُ
وَأَضَلُّوا كَثِيرًا وَضَلُّوا عَنْ سَوَاءِ السَّبِيلِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Say: O followers of the Book! be not unduly immoderate***[^4] ***in
your religion, and do not follow the low desires of people who went
astray before and led many astray and went astray from the right***[^5]
***path (5:77)***

[^1]: God incarnate

[^2]: Indicates that the just ones will have intercessors

[^3]: This is in refutation of Trinity

[^4]: Step not out of the laws – beyond the truth

[^5]: Levelled
