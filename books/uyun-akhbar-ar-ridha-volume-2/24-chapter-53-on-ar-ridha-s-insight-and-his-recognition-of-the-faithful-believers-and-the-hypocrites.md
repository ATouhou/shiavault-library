Chapter 53: On Ar-Ridha’’s Insight and His Recognition of the Faithful Believers and the Hypocrites
===================================================================================================

53-1 (The author of the book narrated) my father - may God be pleased
with him - narrated that Sa’d ibn Abdullah quoted on the authority of
Abdullah ibn Aamir ibn Sa’d, on the authority of Abdul Rahman ibn Abi
Najran, “Abul Hassan Ar-Ridha’ (a.s.) wrote a letter to some of his
companions which he read to me. It said, ‘We know (all about) men when
we see them. We recognize their true faithfulness or hypocrisy.’”
