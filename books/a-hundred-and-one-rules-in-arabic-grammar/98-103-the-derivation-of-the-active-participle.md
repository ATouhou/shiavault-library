103. The Derivation of the Active Participle.
=============================================

The Active Participle derivation pattern for verb Form I is
usually**فاعِل** . For the other forms (II-X), the pattern is to prefix
(**مـُ** ) to the stem. The stem vowel on the second radical is always
Kasra. An exception to that is for pattern IX, which has to be FatHa.
