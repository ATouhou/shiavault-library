The Whispered Prayer of the Knowers
===================================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم
  </p>
</blockquote>

*In the Name of ALLAH, the All-compassionate*

*the All-merciful*

BIS-MIL-LAAHIR-RAH'-MAANIR-RAH'EEM

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللّهُمّ صَلّ عَلَى مُحَمّدٍ وَآلِ مُحَمّد
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Allah, send blessings on Muhammad and on the family of Muhammad,*
ALLAHUMMA S'ALE A'LAA MUHAMMAD WA AALE MUHAMMAD

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إلهِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God,*

ILAAHEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قَصُرَتِ الأَلْسُنُ عَنْ بُلُوغِ ثَنآئِكَ، كَما يَلِيقُ بِجَلالِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Our tongues fall short and we are unable to praise you enough to the
level of your majestic Glory*

QAS'URATIL-AL-SUNU A'M-BULOOGHI THANAAA-IKA KAMA YALEEQU BIJALAALIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَعَجَزَتِ الْعُقُولُ عَنْ إدْراكِ كُنْهِ جَمالِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And our intellect is unable to grasp the core of your beauty,*

WA A'JAZATIL-U'QOOLU A'N ID-RAAKI KUN-HEE JAMAALIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَانْحَسَرَتِ الأَبْصارُ دُونَ النَّظَرِ إلى سُبُحاتِ وَجْهِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And our eyes fail to gaze upon the glory of your face,*

WA AN-H'ASARATIL-AB-S'AARU DOONAN-NAZ'ARI ILAA SUBUH'AATI WAJ-HIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَلَمْ تَجْعَلْ لِلْخَلْقِ طَرِيقاً إلى مَعْرِفَتِكَ إلاَّ بِالْعَجْزِ
عَنْ مَعْرِفَتِكَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and you have not assigned to your creation, a way to know you, except
the inability to know you*

WA LAM TAJ-A'L-LIL-KHAL-QI T'AREEQAN ILAA MAA'-RIFATIKA IL-LAA
BIL-A'J-ZI A'M-MAA'-RIFATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إلهِي فَاجْعَلْنا مِنَ الَّذِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God, include us among those*

ILAAHEE FAJ-A'L-NAA MINAL-LAD'EENA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
تَرَسَّخَتْ أَشْجارُ الشَّوقِ إلَيْكَ فِي حَدآئِقِ صُدُورِهِمْ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Who have the trees of your yearning firmly rooted within their breasts*

TARAS-SAKHAT ASH-JAARUSH-SHAW-QI ILAY-KA FEE H'ADAAA-IQI S'UDOORIHIM-

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَخَذَتْ لَوْعَةُ مَحَبَّتِكَ بِمَجامِعِ قُلُوبِهِمْ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and whose hearts have been seized by the strong feeling of your love!*

WA AKHAD'AT LAW-A'TU MAH'AB-BATIKA BIMAJAAMII' QULOOBIHIM-

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَهُمْ إلَى أَوْكارِ الأَفْكارِ يَأْوُونَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*those who seek shelter in your nests of pondering and meditation,*

FAHUM ILAAA AW-KAARIL-AF-KAARI YAA-WOON

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَفِي رِياضِ الْقُرْبِ وَالْمُكاشَفَةِ يَرْتَعُونَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And they feed upon your gardens of nearness and revelation,*

WA FEE REEAAZ''IL-QUR-BI WAL-MUKAASHAFATI YAR-TAU'WNA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمِنْ حِياضِ الْمَحَبَّةِ بِكَأْسِ الْمُلاطَفَةِ يَكْرَعُونَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And they drink from the pools of your love with the cup of your gentle
favors upon them*

WA MIN H'EEAAZ''IL-MAH'AB-BATI BIKAA-SIL-MULAAT'AFATI YAK-RAU'WN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَشَرايعَ الْمُصافاةِ يَرِدُونَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and they enter into the watering-places of your warm affection.*

WA SHARAAA-IA'L-MUS'AAFAATI YARIDOON

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قَدْ كُشِفَ الْغِطآءُ عَنْ أَبْصَارِهِمْ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*The veil has been lifted from their eyes,*

QAD KUSHIFAL-GHIT'AAA-U A'N AB-S'AARIHIM

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَانْجَلَتْ ظُلْمَةُ الرَّيْبِ عَنْ عَقآئِدِهِمْ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And the darkness of uncertainty has been dispelled from their beliefs
and thoughts,*

WA ANJALAT Z'UL-MATUR-RAY-BI A'N A'QAAA-IDIHIM WA Z''AMAAA-IRIHIM-

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَانْتَفَتْ مُخالَجَةُ الشَّكِّ عَنْ قُلُوبِهِمْ وَسَرآئِرِهِمْ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And the clashes of doubt has been canceled from their hearts and secret
thoughts,*

WA ANTAFAT MUKHAALAJATUSH-SHAK-KI A'N QULOOBIHIM WA SARAAA-IRIHIM-

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَانْشَرَحَتْ بِتَحْقِيقِ الْمَعْرِفَةِ صُدُورُهُمْ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And their breasts have expanded through the attainment of true
knowledge,*

WA ANSHARAH'AT BITAH'-QEEQIL-MAA'-RIFATI S'UDOORUHUM-

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَعَلَتْ لِسَبْقِ السَّعادَةِ فِي الزَّهادَةِ هِمَمُهُمْ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And their aspirations have ascended through the rejection of the
(worldly) fortunes*

 WA A'LAT LISAB-QIS-SAA'ADATI FIZ-ZAHAADATI HIMAMUHUM*-*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَعَذُبَ فِي مَعِينِ الْمُعامَلَةِ شِرْبُهُمْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And their drinking is sweet, derived from the spring of dedication to
doing good*

WA A'D'UBA FEE MAE'ENIL-MUA'AMALATI SHIR-BUHUM

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَطابَ فِي مَجْلِسِ الأُنْسِ سِرُّهُمْ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And their secret thoughts are pleasant , which they received from the
sitting-place of your intimacy,*

WA T'AABA FEE MAJ-LISIL-UNSI SIR-RUHUM-

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَمِنَ فِي مَوْطِنِ الْمَخافَةِ سِرْبُهُمْ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And their minds are secure from all evil and terror*

WA AMINA FEE MAW-T'INIL-MAKHAAFATI SIR-BUHUM

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَاطْمَأَنَّتْ بِالرُّجُوْعِ إلى رَبِّ الأَرْبابِ أَنْفُسُهُمْ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And their souls have achieved tranquility through the return to the
Lord of lords,*

WA AT'-MAAN-NAT BIR-RUJOOI' ILAA RAB-BIL-AR-BAABI ANFUSUHUM*-*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَتَيَقَّنَتْ بِالْفَوْزِ وَالْفَلاحِ أَرْواحُهُمْ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*their spirits have reached certitude through triumph and prosperity,*

WA TAYAQ-QANAT BIL-FAW-ZI WAL-FALAAHEE AR-WAAH'UHUM-

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَقَرَّتْ بِالنَّظَرِ إلى مَحْبُوبِهِمْ أَعْيُنُهُمْ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*their eyes are content through gazing upon their Beloved,*

WA QAR-RAT BIN-NAZ'ARI ILAA MAH'-BOOBIHIM AA'-YUNUHUM-

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَاسْتَقَرَّ بِإدْراكِ السُّؤْلِ وَنَيْلِ الْمَأْمُولِ قَرَارُهُمْ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*their settling place has been established through reaching what they
had searched for and attaining their desire*

WAS-TAQAR-RA BIID-RAAKIS-SOO-LI WA NAY-LIL-MAA-MOOLI QARAARUHUM-

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَرَبِحَتْ فِي بَيْعِ الدُّنْيَا بِالآخِرَةِ تِجارَتُهُمْ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and their trade has profited through the sale (giving up) of the
desires of this world for the next*

WA RABIH'AT FEE BAY-I'D-DUNYAA BIL- AAAKHIRATI TIJAARATUHUM

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إلهِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God,*

ILAAHEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ما أَلَذَّ خَواطِرَ الإِلْهامِ بِذِكْرِكَ عَلَى الْقُلُوبِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*how pleasant it is for hearts to be inspired by thoughts of your
remembrance,*

MAAA ALAD'-D'A KHAWAAT'IRAL-IL-HAAMI BID'IK-RIKA A'LAAL-QULOOB

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَما أَحْلَى الْمَسِيرَ إلَيْكَ بِالأَوْهامِ فِي مَسالِكِ الْغُيُوبِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*how sweet it is to travel to you, through the imagination of the roads
of the unseen worlds,*

WA MAAA AH'-LA AL-MASEERA ILAY-KA BIL-AW-HAAMI FEE MASAALIKIL-GHUYOOB

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَما أَطْيَبَ طَعْمَ حُبِّكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And how pleasant is the taste of your love,*

WA MAAA AT'-YABA T'AA'-MA H'UB-BIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَما أَعْذَبَ شِرْبَ قُرْبِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And how delightful is the drink of your nearness!*

WA MAAA AA'-D'ABA SHIR-BA QUR-BIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَأَعِذْنا مِنْ طَرْدِكَ وَإبْعادِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*So give us protection from being cast out and being sent far away from
you*

FAAI'D'-NA MIN T'AR-DIKA WA IB-A'ADIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَاجْعَلْنا مِنْ أَخَصِّ عارِفِيكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and place us amongst the best of those who know you*

WAJ-A'L-NAA MINAKHAS'-S'I A'ARIFEEK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَصْلَحِ عِبادِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And the most righteous of your servants,*

WA AS'-LAHEE I'BAADIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَصْدَقِ طآئِعِيكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And the most truthful of the ones who obey you,*

WA AS'-DAQI T'AAA-IE'EK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَخْلَصِ عُبَّادِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And the most sincere of your worshipers!*

WA AKH-LAS'I U'B-BAADIKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يا عَظِيمُ، يا جَلِيلُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O All-mighty, O Majestic,*

YAA A'Z'EEMU YAA JALEEL

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يا كَرِيمُ، يا مُنِيلُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Generous, O Rewarder*

YAA KAREEMU YAA MUNEEL

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِرَحْمَتِكَ وَمَنِّكَ يا أَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِينَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*By your mercy and kindness, O Most Merciful of the merciful!*

BIRAH'-MATIKA WA MAN-NIKA YAAA AR-H'AMAR-RAAH'IMEEN
