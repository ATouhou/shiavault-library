THE ZIYARAH OF IMAM AL-HASAN AL-ASKARI
======================================

Translated by: Badr Shahin

Shaykh al-Tusi has reported through a considerable series of narrators
that Imam al-Hasan al-Askari (a.s.) said, “My grave will be in
Surra-man-Ra’a (currently Samarra). It will be shelter for both the
parties.” Explaining this saying, Shaykh al-Majlisi says that ‘both the
parties’ means the Shi’ah and the Sunnah. This means that the virtue of
the Imam (a.s.) includes both his adherents and his enemies. Similarly,
the holy tomb of Imam al-Kazim (a.s.) and Imam al-Jawad (a.s.) is
shelter for the people of Baghdad.

Sayyid Ibn Tawus says that once you intend to visit the holy tomb of
Imam al-Hasan al-Askari (a.s.), you may do the following in the
beginning:

Once you arrive in Samarra, you may wash yourself (traditionally, this
bathing is called ‘the bath of Ziyarah), put on the cleanest of your
clothes, walk with gravity and tranquility to the tomb, and when you
reach at the gate of the holy mausoleum, you may say the following as a
form of seeking permission to enter:

</tr>

<td align="left"><p>يَا أَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِينَ.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td align="left"><p>O Allah: surely, Iblis, the rebel, the accursed,</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>ALLAAHUMMA WA INNA IBLEESAL-MUTAMARRIDAL-LA’EEN(A)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>اللّهُمّ وَإِنّ إِبْلِيسَ الْمُتَمَرّدَ اللّعِينَ</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td align="left"><p>Asked You to grant his respite so that he would mislead Your creatures, and You granted him so,</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>QADIS-TANZ’ARAKA LI-IGHWAA`I KHALQIKA FA-ANZ’ARTAH(OO)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>قَدِ اسْتَنْظَرَكَ لاِغْوَاءِ خَلْقِكَ فَأَنْظَرْتَهُ،</p></td>

<tr class="even">

<td align="left"><p>وَاسْتَمْهَلَكَ لاِضْلالِ عَبِيدِكَ فَأَمْهَلْتَهُ</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">

<td align="left"><p>BISAABIQI ‘ILMIKA FEEH(I)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>بِسَابِقِ عِلْمِكَ فِيهِ</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">

<td align="left"><p>WAQAD ‘ASHSHASHA WA KATHURAT JUNOODUH(OO)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَقَدْ عَشّشَ، وَكَثُرَتْ جُنُودُهُ،</p></td>
</tr>

<td align="left"><p>WAZDAH’AMAT JUYOOSHUH(OO)</p></td>

</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td align="left"><p>And his propagandists have widespread in all the corners of this earth,</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>WANTASHARAT DU’AATUHOO FEE AQT’AARIL-ARD’(I)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَانْتَشَرَتْ دُعَاتُهُ فِي أَقْطَارِ الارْضِ،</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td align="left"><p>And they have mislead Your servants,</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>FA-AD’ALLOO ‘IBAADAK(A)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>فَأَضَلّوَا عِبَادَكَ،</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td align="left"><p>And deformed Your religion,</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>WA-AFSADOO DEENAK(A)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَأَفْسَدُوَا دِينَكَ،</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">

</tr>
<tr class="even">

<td align="left"><p>وَجَعَلُوَا عِبَادَكَ شِيَعًا مُتَفَرّقِينَ،</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td align="left"><p>And rebellious parties.</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>WA AH’ZAABAN MUTAMARRIDEEN</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وأَحْزَابًا مُتَمَرّدِينَ،</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">

<td align="left"><p>WAQAD WA’ADTA NAQD’A BUNYAANIH(EE)</p></td>

</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td align="left"><p>And You would tear out his matters,</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>WA TAMZEEQA SHA`NIH(EE)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَتَمْزِيقَ شَأْنِهِ،</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">

<td align="left"><p>FA AHLIK AWLAADAHOO WA JUYOOSHAH(OO)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>فَأَهْلِكْ أَوْلادَهُ وَجُيُوشَهُ،</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td align="left"><p>And purify Your lands from his inventions and fabrications,</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>WA T’AHHIR BILAADAKA MINIKH-TIRAA’AATIHEE WAKH-TILAAFAATIH(EE)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَطَهّرْ بِلادَكَ مِنِ اخْتِرَاعَاتِهِ وَاخْتِلافَاتِهِ،</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">

<td align="left"><p>WA ARIH’ ‘IBAADAKA MIN MADHAAHIBIHEE WA QIYAASAATIH(EE)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَأَرِحْ عِبَادَكَ مِنْ مَذَاهِبِهِ وَقِيَاسَاتِهِ،</p></td>
</tr>

</tr>

<td align="left"><p>WABSUT’ ‘ADLAK(A)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَابْسُطْ عَدْلَكَ،</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">

<td align="left"><p>WA AZ’-HIR DEENAK(A)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَأَظْهِرْ دِينَكَ،</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">

<td align="left"><p>WA QAWWI AWLIYAA`AK(A)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَقَوّ أَوْلِيَاءَكَ،</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td align="left"><p>And weaken Your enemies,</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>WA AWHIN A’DAA`AK(A)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَأَوْهِنْ أَعْدَاءَكَ،</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">

</tr>
<tr class="odd">

<td align="left"><p>WA KHALLID-HUM FIL-JAH’EEM(I)</p></td>

</tr>

<td align="left"><p>WAJ-‘AL LA’AA`INAKAL-MUSTAWDA’ATA FEE MANAAH’ISIL-KHILQATI WA MASHAAWEEHIL-FIT’RATI DAA`IRATAN ‘ALAYHIM</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَاجْعَلْ لَعَائِنَكَ الْمُسْتَوْدَعَةَ فِي مَنَاحِسِ الْخِلْقَةِ وَمَشَاوِيهِ الْفِطْرَةِ دَائِرَةً عَلَيْهِمْ</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td align="left"><p>And control over them,</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>WA MUWAKKALATAN BIHIM</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَمُوَكّلَةً بِهِمْ</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td align="left"><p>And strike them every morning and evening</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>WA JAARIYATAN FEEHIM KULLA S’ABAAH’IN WA MASAA`(IN)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَجَارِيَةً فِيهِمْ كُلّ صَبَاحٍ وَمَسَاءٍ</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td align="left"><p>And every single minute.</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>WA GHUDUWWIN WA RAWAAH’(IN)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَغُدُوّ وَرَوَاحٍ.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">

<td align="left"><p>RABBANAA AATINAA FID-DUNYAA H’ASANA(TAN)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>رَبّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدّنْيَا حَسَنَةً</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td align="left"><p>And goodness in the world to come,</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>WA FIL-AAKHIRATI H’ASANA(TAN)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَفِي الآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td align="left"><p>And save us, out of Your mercy, from the torment of Fire.</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>WA QINAA BIRAH’MATIKA ‘ADHAABAN-NAAR(I)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>وَقِنَا بِرَحْمَتِكَ عَذَابَ النَّارِ</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td align="left"><p>You are surely the most Merciful of all those who show mercy.</p></td>
<td align="left"><p>YAA ARH’AMAR-RAAH’IMEEN(A)</p></td>
<td align="left"><p> يَا أَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِينَ.</p></td>
