30) His Supplication for Help in Repaying Debts
===============================================

> (30) وَ كَانَ مِنْ دُعَائِهِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلَامُ فِي الْمَعُونَةِ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَلَى قَضَاءِ الدَّيْنِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 1. اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ هَبْ لِيَ الْعَافِيَةَ مِنْ دَيْنٍ تُخْلِقُ بِهِ وَجْهِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يَحَارُ فِيهِ ذِهْنِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يَتَشَعَّبُ لَهُ فِكْرِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يَطُولُ بِمُمَارَسَتِهِ شُغْلِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 2. وَ أَعُوذُ بِكَ يَا رَبِّ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مِنْ هَمِّ الدَّيْنِ وَ فِكْرِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ شُغْلِ الدَّيْنِ وَ سَهَرِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَصَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ، وَ أَعِذْنِي مِنْهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَسْتَجِيرُ بِكَ ، يَا رَبِّ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مِنْ ذِلَّتِهِ فِي الْحَيَاةِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ تَبِعَتِهِ بَعْدَ الْوَفَاةِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَصَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَجِرْني مِنْهُ بِوُسْعٍ فَاضِلٍ أَوْ كَفَافٍ وَاصِلٍ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 3. اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ احْجُبْنِي عَنِ السَّرَفِ وَ الِازْدِيَادِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ قَوِّمْنِي بِالْبَذْلِ وَ الِاقْتِصَادِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ عَلِّمْنِي حُسْنَ التَّقْدِيرِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اقْبِضْنِي بِلُطْفِكَ عَنِ التَّبْذِيرِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَجْرِ مِنْ أَسْبَابِ الْحَلَالِ أَرْزَاقِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ وَجِّهْ فِي أَبْوَابِ الْبِرِّ إِنْفَاقِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ ازْوِ عَنِّي مِنَ الْمَالِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَا يُحْدِثُ لِي مَخِيلَةً أَوْ تَأَدِّياً إِلَى بَغْيٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَوْ مَا أَتَعَقَّبُ مِنْهُ طُغْيَاناً .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 4. اللَّهُمَّ حَبِّبْ إِلَيَّ صُحْبَةَ الْفُقَرَاءِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَعِنِّي عَلَى صُحْبَتِهِمْ بِحُسْنِ الصَّبْرِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 5. وَ مَا زَوَيْتَ عَنِّي مِنْ مَتَاعِ الدُّنْيَا الْفَانِيَةِ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَاذْخَرْهُ لِي فِي خَزَائِنِكَ الْبَاقِيَةِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 6. وَ اجْعَلْ مَا خَوَّلْتَنِي مِنْ حُطَامِهَا ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ عَجَّلْتَ لِي مِنْ مَتَاعِهَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بُلْغَةً إِلَى جِوَارِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ وُصْلَةً إِلَى قُرْبِكَ وَ ذَرِيعَةً إِلَى جَنَّتِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِنَّكَ ذُو الْفَضْلِ الْعَظِيمِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَنْتَ الْجَوَادُ الْكَرِيمُ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

1- O God, bless Muhammad and his Household  
 and release me from a debt  
 which makes me lose face,  
 confuses my mind,  
 disrupts my thinking,  
 and prolongs my occupation with attending to it!

2- I seek refuge in Thee, my Lord,  
 from worry and thought about debt,  
 from the distraction and sleeplessness of debt;  
 so bless Muhammad and his Household and give me refuge from it!  
 I seek sanctuary in Thee, my Lord, from  
 debt's abasement in life  
 and its ill effects after death,  
 so bless Muhammad and his Household  
 and give me sanctuary from it through  
 a bountiful plenty  
 or a continually arriving sufficiency!

3- O God,  
 bless Muhammad and his Household  
 prevent me from extravagance and excess,  
 put me on the course of generous spending and moderation,  
 teach me excellent distribution,  
 hold me back through Thy gentleness from squandering,  
 allow me to attain my provisions through lawful means,  
 direct my spending toward the gateways of devotion,  
 and take away from me any possession  
 which will bring forth pride in me,  
 lead to insolence,  
 or drag me in its heels to rebellion!

4- O God,  
 make me love the companionship of the poor  
 and help me be their companion with excellent patience!

5- Whenever Thou takest away from me  
 the goods of this perishing world,  
 store them for me in Thy abiding treasuries!

6- Make this world's broken pieces which Thou hast conferred upon me  
 and its goods which Thou hast quickly granted to me  
 a way to reach Thy neighbourhood,  
 a link to Thy nearness, and a means to Thy Garden!  
 Verily Thou art Possessor of bounty abounding,  
 and Thou art the Munificent, the Generous.
