28. Destroying Prayers  
========================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قَالَ رَسُولُ اللٌّه ِ(ص): لاَ تُضَيِّعُوا صَلاَتَكُمْ فَإِنَّ مَنْ
ضَيَّعَ صَلاَتَهُ حُشِرَ مَعَ قَارُونَ وَ هَامَانَ وَ فِرْعَوْنَ‏.
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
 
  </p>
</blockquote>

The Messenger of Allah (peace be upon him and his progeny) has said: “Do
not destroy your prayers for verily one who destroys his prayers shall
be resurrected in the company of Qarun, Haman and Fir\`awn.”** **  
 Biharul Anwar, Volume 82, Page 202
