It is an Ism that is Manṣūb (denotating) that on which the action
occurs, like when you say:

ضربتُزيدا ً                           (I hitZayd **)**

**ركبتُ** الفرسَ                             (I rodethe horse )

It is of two types: Ḍāhir and Muđmar.

The Ḍāhir has already been mentioned.

The Muđmar is two groups: Muttaṣil (attached personal pronouns) and
Munfaṣil (detached personal pronouns).

The Muttaṣil is twelve (in number), and they are:

ضربني [^65]                    [He hitme (masc./fem.) **]**

**ضربَ** نا                                   [He hitus (masc./fem.
dual/pl.) ]

ضربكَ                                  [He hityou (masc. sing.) ]

ضربكِ                                 [He hityou (fem. sing.) ]

ضربكما [^66]                 [He hityou (masc./fem. dual) ]

ضربكم        [^67]                       [He hityou (masc. pl.) ]

ضربكنّ [^68]                  [He hityou (fem. pl.) ]

ضربه                                  (He hithim )

ضربها                                  (He hither )

ضربهما                     [He hitthem (masc./fem. dual) ]

ضربهم                     [He hitthem (masc. pl.) ]

ضربهنّ                     [He hitthem (fem. pl.) ]
