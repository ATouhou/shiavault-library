15) His Supplication when Sick
==============================

> ( 15 ) وَ كَانَ مِنْ دُعَائِهِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلَامُ إِذَا مَرِضَ أَوْ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
نَزَلَ بِهِ كَرْبٌ أَوْ بَلِيَّةٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

His Supplication when Sick or Visited by Distress or an Affliction

1- O God, to Thee belongs praise  
 for the good health of my body  
 which lets me move about,  
 and to Thee belongs praise,  
 for the ailments  
 which Thou causest to arise in my flesh!

2- For I know not, my God,  
 which of the two states deserves more my thanking Thee  
 and which of the two times is more worthy for my praise of Thee:

3- the time of health,  
 within which Thou makest me delight in the agreeable things of Thy
provision,  
 through which Thou givest me the joy to seek  
 the means to Thy good pleasure and bounty,  
 and by which Thou strengthenest me  
 for the acts of obedience  
 which Thou hast given me success to accomplish;

4- or the time of illness  
 through which Thou puttest me to the test  
 and bestowest upon me favours:  
 lightening of the offenses  
 that weigh down my back,  
 purification of the evil deeds  
 into which I have plunged,  
 incitement to reach for repentance,  
 reminder of the erasure of misdeeds through ancient favour;

5- and, through all that, what the two writers write for me:  
 blameless acts,  
 which no heart had thought,  
 no tongue had uttered,  
 and no limb had undertaken,  
 rather, as Thy bestowal of bounty upon me  
 and the beneficence of Thy benefaction toward me.[^1]

6- O God,  
 bless Muhammad and his Household,  
 make me love what Thou hast approved for me,  
 make easy for me what Thou hast sent down upon me,  
 purify me of the defilement of what I have sent ahead,  
 erase the evil of what I have done beforehand,  
 let me find the sweetness of well-being,  
 let me taste the coolness of safety,  
 and appoint for me  
 a way out from my illness to Thy pardon,  
 transformation of my infirmity into Thy forbearance,  
 escape from my distress to Thy refreshment,  
 and safety from this hardship in Thy relief!

7- Thou art gratuitously bountiful in beneficence,  
 ever gracious in kindness,  
 the Generous, the Giver,  
 Possessor of majesty and munificence!

> 1. اللَّهُمَّ لَكَ الْحَمْدُ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَلَى مَا لَمْ أَزَلْ أَتَصَرَّفُ فِيهِ مِنْ سَلَامَةِ بَدَنِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَكَ الْحَمْدُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَلَى مَا أَحْدَثْتَ بِي مِنْ عِلَّةٍ فِي جَسَدِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 2. فَمَا أَدْرِي ، يَا إِلَهِي ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَيُّ الْحَالَيْنِ أَحَقُّ بِالشُّكْرِ لَكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَيُّ الْوَقْتَيْنِ أَوْلَى بِالْحَمْدِ لَكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 3. أَ وَقْتُ الصِّحَّةِ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الَّتِي هَنَّأْتَنِي فِيهَا طَيِّبَاتِ رِزْقِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ نَشَّطْتَنِي بِهَا لِابْتِغَاءِ مَرْضَاتِكَ وَ فَضْلِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ قَوَّيْتَنِي مَعَهَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَلَى مَا وَفَّقْتَنِي لَهُ مِنْ طَاعَتِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 4. أَمْ وَقْتُ الْعِلَّةِ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الَّتِي مَحَّصْتَنِي بِهَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ النِّعَمِ الَّتِي أَتْحَفْتَنِي بِهَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
تَخْفِيفاً لِمَا ثَقُلَ بِهِ عَلَيَّ ظَهْرِي مِنَ الْخَطِيئَاتِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تَطْهِيراً لِمَا انْغَمَسْتُ فِيهِ مِنَ السَّيِّئَاتِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تَنْبِيهاً لِتَنَاوُلِ التَّوْبَةِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تَذْكِيراً لِمَحْوِ الْحَوْبَةِ بِقَدِيمِ النِّعْمَةِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 5. وَ فِي خِلَالِ ذَلِكَ مَا كَتَبَ لِيَ الْكَاتِبَانِ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مِنْ زَكِيِّ الْأَعْمَالِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَا لَا قَلْبٌ فَكَّرَ فِيهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَا لِسَانٌ نَطَقَ بِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَا جَارِحَةٌ تَكَلَّفَتْهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بَلْ إِفْضَالًا مِنْكَ عَلَيَّ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ إِحْسَاناً مِنْ صَنِيعِكَ إِلَيَّ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 6. اللَّهُمَّ فَصَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ حَبِّبْ إِلَيَّ مَا رَضِيتَ لِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يَسِّرْ لِي مَا أَحْلَلْتَ بِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ طَهِّرْنِي مِنْ دَنَسِ مَا أَسْلَفْتُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ امْحُ عَنِّي شَرَّ مَا قَدَّمْتُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَوْجِدْنِي حَلَاوَةَ الْعَافِيَةِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَذِقْنِي بَرْدَ السَّلَامَةِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اجْعَلْ مَخْرَجِي عَنْ عِلَّتِي إِلَى عَفْوِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مُتَحَوَّلِي عَنْ صَرْعَتِي إِلَى تَجَاوُزِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ خَلَاصِي مِنْ كَرْبِي إِلَى رَوْحِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ سَلَامَتِي مِنْ هَذِهِ الشِّدَّةِ إِلَى فَرَجِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 7. إِنَّكَ الْمُتَفَضِّلُ بِالْإِحْسَانِ ، الْمُتَطَوِّلُ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِالِامْتِنَانِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْوَهَّابُ الْكَرِيمُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ذُو الْجَلَالِ وَ الْإِكْرَامِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

[^1]: A believer who cannot perform the obligatory acts of worship
because of illness is credited with them in any case. The commentator
cites a number of hadith to this effect, e.g.: 'When the believer
becomes sick, God says to the angel charged with him: "Write for him
what you used to write when he was healthy".'
