Amassing [Wealth]
=================

**Amassing [wealth]-التكاثر**

1. Amassing that which does not last for you and for which you will not
remain is from the greatest ignorance.

> 1ـ تَكَثُّرُكَ (تَكَبُّرُكَ) بِما لايَبْقى لَكَ ولاتَبْقى لَهُ مِنْ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أعْظَمِ الجَهْلِ.
  </p>
</blockquote>
