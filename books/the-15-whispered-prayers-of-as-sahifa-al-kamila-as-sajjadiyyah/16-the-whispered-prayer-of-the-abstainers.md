The Whispered Prayer of the Abstainers
======================================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم
  </p>
</blockquote>

*In the Name of ALLAH, the All-compassionate*

*the All-merciful*

BIS-MIL-LAAHIR-RAH'-MAANIR-RAH'EEM

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللّهُمّ صَلّ عَلَى مُحَمّدٍ وَآلِ مُحَمّد
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Allah, send blessings on Muhammad and on the family of Muhammad,*
ALLAHUMMA S'ALE A'LAA MUHAMMAD WA AALE MUHAMMAD

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
داراً حَفَرَتْ لَنا حُفَرَ مَكْرِها . إلهِي أَسْكَنْتَنا
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God, you have settled us in a dwelling (dunya) which has dug for us
in it pits of deception,*

ILAAHEEE AS-KANTANAA DAARANA H'AFARAT LANAA H'UFARA MAK-RIHAA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَعَلَّقَتْنا بِأَيْدِي الْمَنايا فِي حَبائِلِ غَدْرِها،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*We have been trapped in this world (Dunya), which deceives us and we
cannot escape until we are removed by death*

WA A'L-LAQ-TANAA BIY-DEEL-MANAAYAA FEE H'ABAAA-ILI GHAD-RIHAA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَإلَيْكَ نَلْتَجِئُ مِنْ مَكآئِدِ خُدَعِها،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*So In you we seek shelter from the tricks and plans of this world
(Dunya),*

FAALAY-KA NAL-TAJI-U MIM-MAKAAA-IDI KHUDAI'HAA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَبِكَ نَعْتَصِمُ مِنَ الاغْتِرارِ بِزَخارِفِ زِيْنَتِهَا، فَإنَّهَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And to you we hold fast, for fear that we may be deceived by the
glitter of its (dunya’s) ornaments!*

WA BIKA NAA'-TAS'IMU MINALIGH-TIRAAR BIZAKHAARIFI ZEENATIHAA FA IN-NAHA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْمُهْلِكَةُ طُلاَّبَهَا،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*It (Dunya)destroys the ones who seeks it*

AL-MUH-LIKATU T'UL-LAABAHAA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْمُتْلِفَةُ حُلاَّلَهَا،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*It ruins the ones who try to settle in it*

AL-MUT-LIFATU H'UL-LAALAHAA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْمَحْشُوَّةُ بِالافاتِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*it is overflowing with problems*

AL-MAH'-SHOO-WATU BIL-AAAFAATI

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْمَشْحُونَةُ بِالنَّكَباتِ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and loaded with calamities.*

AL-MASH-H'OONATU BIN-NAKABAAT

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إلهِي فَزَهِّدْنا فِيها،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God, inspire us to renounce it*

ILAAHEE FAZAH-HID-NA FEEHAA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَسَلِّمْنا مِنْها بِتَوْفِيقِكَ وَعِصْمَتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And keep us safe from it by giving success to our good work and by your
protecting us from sin.*

WA SAL-LIM-NAA MIN-HAA BITAW-FEEQIKA WA I'S'-MATIKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَانْزَعْ عَنَّا جَلابِيبَ مُخالَفَتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Strip away, from us the robes of sins*

WA ANZAA' A'N-NAA JALAABEEBA MUKHAALAFATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَتَوَلَّ أُمُورَنا بِحُسْنِ كِفايَتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And you take over our affairs through your sufficient management,*

WA TAWAL-LA UMOORANAA BIH'US-NI KIFAAYATIKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَوْفِرْ مَزِيدَنا مِنْ سَعَةِ رَحْمَتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And give us more of your unlimited mercy,*

WA AW-FIR MAZEEDANAA MIN SAA'TI RAH'-MATIKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَجْمِلْ صِلاتِنا مِنْ فَيْضِ مَواهِبِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And gift us your beautiful gifts from the overflowing fountain of your
grants,*

WA AJ-MIL S'ILAATINAA MIN FAY-Z''I MAWAAHIBIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَاغْرِسْ فِي أَفْئِدَتِنا أَشْجارَ مَحَبَّتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*plant in our hearts the trees of your love,*

WAGH-RIS FEEE AF-IDATINAAA ASH-JAARA MAH'AB-BATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَتْمِمْ لَنا أَنْوارَ مَعْرِفَتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And complete for us the light of your knowledge,*

WA AT-MIM LANAAA ANWAARA MAA'-RIFATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَذِقْنا حَلاوَةَ عَفْوِكَ، وَلَذَّةَ مَغْفِرَتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And let us taste the sweetness of your pardon and the pleasure of your
forgiveness,*

WA AD'IQ-NAA H'ALAAWATA A'F-WIKA WA LAD'-D'ATA MAGH-FIRATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَقْرِرْ أَعْيُنَنا يَوْمَ لِقآئِكَ بِرُؤْيَتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*bring joy to our eyes by permitting us to see you on the day of meeting
(Judgement)*

WA AQ-RIR AA'-YUNANAA YAW-MA LIQAAA-IKA BIROO-YATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَخْرِجْ حُبَّ الدُّنْيا مِنْ قُلُوبِنا
  </p>
</blockquote>

*remove the love of this world from our hearts,*

WA AKH-RIJ H'UB-BAD-DUNYAA MIN QULOOBINAA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
كَما فَعَلْتَ بِالصَّالِحِينَ مِنْ صَفْوَتِكَ، وَالأَبْرارِ مِنْ
خاصَّتِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*just as you have done for the righteous and selected friends, and for
the pious ones, those of whom you have selected*

KAMA FAA'L-TA BIS'-S'AALIH'EENA MIN S'AF-WATIKA WAL-AB-RAARI MIN
KHAAAS'-S'ATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِرَحْمَتِكَ يا أَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Most Merciful of the merciful,*

YAAA AR-H'AMAR-RAAH'IMEEN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا أَكْرَمَ الأَكْرَمِينَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Most Generous of the most generous!*

WA YAAA AK-RAMAL-AK-RAMEEN
