54) His Supplication for the Removal of Worries
===============================================

> (54) وَ كَانَ مِنْ دُعَائِهِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلَامُ فِي اسْتِكْشَافِ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْهُمُومِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 1. يَا فَارِجَ الْهَمِّ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ كَاشِفَ الْغَمِّ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَا رَحْمَانَ الدُّنْيَا وَ الْآخِرَةِ وَ رَحِيمَهُمَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ افْرُجْ هَمِّي ، وَ اكْشِفْ غَمِّي .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 2. يَا وَاحِدُ يَا أَحَدُ يَا صَمَدُ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَا مَنْ لَمْ يَلِدْ وَ لَمْ يُولَدْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَمْ يَكُنْ لَهُ كُفُواً أَحَدٌ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اعْصِمْنِي وَ طَهِّرْنِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اذْهَبْ بِبَلِيَّتِي .
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اقْرَأْ آيَةَ الْكُرْسِيِّ وَ الْمُعَوِّذَتَيْنِ وَ قُلْ هُوَ
اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ ، وَ قُلْ :
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 3. اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
سُؤَالَ مَنِ اشْتَدَّتْ فَاقَتُهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ ضَعُفَتْ قُوَّتُهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ كَثُرَتْ ذُنُوبُهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
سُؤَالَ مَنْ لَا يَجِدُ لِفَاقَتِهِ مُغِيثاً ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَا لِضَعْفِهِ مُقَوِّياً ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَا لِذَنْبِهِ غَافِراً غَيْرَكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَا ذَا الْجَلَالِ وَ الْإِكْرَامِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَسْأَلُكَ عَمَلًا تُحِبُّ بِهِ مَنْ عَمِلَ بِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يَقِيناً تَنْفَعُ بِهِ مَنِ اسْتَيْقَنَ بِهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
حَقَّ الْيَقِينَ فِي نَفَاذِ أَمْرِكَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 4. اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اقْبِضْ عَلَى الصِّدْقِ نَفْسِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اقْطَعْ مِنَ الدُّنْيَا حَاجَتِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اجْعَلْ فِيمَا عِنْدَكَ رَغْبَتِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
شَوْقاً إِلَى لِقَائِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ هَبْ لِي صِدْقَ التَّوَكُّلِ عَلَيْكَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 5. أَسْأَلُكَ مِنْ خَيْرِ كِتَابٍ قَدْ خَلَا ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ شَرِّ كِتَابٍ قَدْ خَلَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَسْأَلُكَ خَوْفَ الْعَابِدِينَ لَكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ عِبَادَةَ الْخَاشِعِينَ لَكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يَقِينَ الْمُتَوَكِّلِينَ عَلَيْكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تَوَكُّلَ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ عَلَيْكَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 6. اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ رَغْبَتِي فِي مَسْأَلَتِي

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مِثْلَ رَغْبَةِ أَوْلِيَائِكَ فِي مَسَائِلِهِمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ رَهْبَتِي مِثْلَ رَهْبَةِ أَوْلِيَائِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اسْتَعْمِلْنِي فِي مَرْضَاتِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَمَلًا لَا أَتْرُكُ مَعَهُ شَيْئاً مِنْ دِينِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَخَافَةَ أَحَدٍ مِنْ خَلْقِكَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 7. اللَّهُمَّ هَذِهِ حَاجَتِي فَأَعْظِمْ فِيهَا رَغْبَتِي ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَظْهِرْ فِيهَا عُذْرِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَقِّنِّي فِيهَا حُجَّتِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ عَافِ فِيهَا جَسَدِي .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 8. اللَّهُمَّ مَنْ أَصْبَحَ لَهُ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ثِقَةٌ أَوْ رَجَاءٌ غَيْرُكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَقَدْ أَصْبَحْتُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَنْتَ ثِقَتِي وَ رَجَائِي فِي الْأُمُورِ كُلِّهَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَاقْضِ لِي بِخَيْرِهَا عَاقِبَةً ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ نَجِّنِي مِنْ مَضَلَّاتِ الْفِتَنِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِرَحْمَتِكَ يَا أَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِينَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 9. وَ صَلَّى اللَّهُ عَلَى سَيِّدِنَا

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مُحَمَّدٍ رَسُولِ اللَّهِ الْمُصْطَفَى
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ عَلَى آلِهِ الطَّاهِرِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

1- O Reliever of worry!  
 O Remover of grief!  
 O Merciful in this world and the next  
 and Compassionate in both!  
 Bless Muhammad and his Household,  
 relieve my worry,  
 and remove my grief!

2- O One, O *Unique*, O *Eternal Refuge*!  
 O He *Who has not begotten,*  
*nor has been begotten,*  
*and equal to Him is not any one*![^1]  
 Preserve me,  
 purify me,  
 and take away my affliction!

Here you should recite the throne verse (2:255), the two Suras of taking
refuge (113-114), and unity (112). Then say:

3- O God, I ask Thee  
 with the asking of him whose neediness is intense,  
 whose strength is frail,  
 whose sins are many,  
 the asking of one who finds no helper in his neediness,  
 no strengthener in his frailty,  
 no forgiver of his sin other than Thee,  
 O Possessor of majesty and munificence!  
 I ask of Thee a work through which Thou wilt love him who works it  
 and a certainty by which Thou wilt profit him who is certain  
 with the truth of certainty concerning the execution of Thy command!

4- O God,  
 bless Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad,  
 take my soul while it is firm in sincerity,  
 cut off my need for this world,  
 make my desire for what is with Thee  
 become a yearning to meet Thee,  
 and give me true confidence in Thee!

5- I ask of Thee the good of the writ that has been made  
 and I seek refuge with Thee from the evil of the writ that has been
made.[^2]  
 I ask of Thee  
 the fear of The worshipers,  
 the worship of those humbly fearful of Thee,  
 the certainty of those who have confidence in Thee,  
 and the confidence of those who have faith in Thee.

6- O God, make my desire in my asking  
 like the desire of Thy friends in their asking,  
 and my fear  
 like the fear of Thy friends!  
 Employ me in Thy good pleasure through works  
 in which I will not leave aside anything of Thy religion  
 fearing any of Thy creatures!

7- O God,  
 this is my need,  
 so make my desire for it great,  
 within it make manifest my excuse,  
 through it instil me with my argument,  
 and by means of it make well my body!

8- O God,  
 some rise in the morning  
 having trust or hope in other than Thee.  
 I rise in the morning,  
 and Thou art my trust and my hope in all affairs,  
 so decree for me those which are best in outcome  
 and deliver me from misguiding trials,  
 O Most Merciful of the merciful!

9- And God bless our chief,  
 Muhammad the Messenger of God, the chosen,  
 and his Household, the pure!

[^1]: Sura 112

[^2]: Allusion to such verses as 8:68: Had it not been for a prior writ
from God, there had afflicted you, for what you took, a dreadful
chastisement.
