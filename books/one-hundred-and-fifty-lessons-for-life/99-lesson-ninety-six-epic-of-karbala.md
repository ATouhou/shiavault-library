Lesson Ninety Six: Epic Of Karbala
==================================

Imam Husayn (a.s.) said:

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لا وَاللّهِ لا أُعْطِيهِمْ بِيَدِيْ إعْطَاءَ الدَّليلِ وَ لا أَفِرُّ
فِرارَ الْعَبِيْد... إنِّىْ لا أَرَىَ الْمَوْتَ إلاّ سَعادَةً و
الحَياةَ مَعَ الظّالِمِينَ إلاّ بَرَما
  </p>
</blockquote>

Translation
-----------

I swear by God that I shall never join hands with them like those
humbled, nor escape like the slaves. I consider death as nothing but
prosperity, and life with the unjust as nothing but a source of blame
and misfortune. [^1]

Brief Description
-----------------

Karbala is the great and everlasting epic of man’s history.

Ashura is an unforgettable day in the history of mankind and nations who
want to live honorably and die honorably. The above two sentences from a
speech of Imam Husayn (a.s.) are two clear signs of this reality.

[^1]: Naqtalel Hussein, page 246 & 256. Al-Irshad, vol2, page 97. Aalamu
Alwara, page 242. Al-Manaqib, vol 4, page 86.
