The Whispered Prayer of those who Hold Fast
===========================================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم
  </p>
</blockquote>

*In the Name of ALLAH, the All-compassionate*

*the All-merciful*

BIS-MIL-LAAHIR-RAH'-MAANIR-RAH'EEM

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللّهُمّ صَلّ عَلَى مُحَمّدٍ وَآلِ مُحَمّد
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Allah, send blessings on Muhammad and on the family of Muhammad,*
ALLAHUMMA S'ALE A'LAA MUHAMMAD WA AALE MUHAMMAD

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَللَّهُمَّ يا مَلاَذَ اللائِذِينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O ALLAH, O Shelter of the ones who seek shelter!*

AL-LAHUM-MA YAA MALAAD'AL-LAAA-ID'EEN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا مَعاذَ الْعآئِذِينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O sanctuary of the ones who seek refuge!*

WA YAA MAA'AD'AL-A'AA-ID'EENA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا مُنْجِيَ الْهالِكِينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O savior of the ones who are perishing!*

WA YAA MUNJEEAL-HAALIKEEN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا عاصِمَ الْبآئِسِينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Protector of the ones who are in a miserable condition*

WA YAA A'AS'IMAL-BAAA-ISEEN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا راحِمَ الْمَساكِينِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Merciful toward the ones who are in need!*

WA YAA RAAH'IMAL-MASAAKEEN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا مُجِيبَ الْمُضْطَرِّينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Responder to the ones who are in distress!*

WA YAA MUJEEBAL-MUZ''-T'AR-REEN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا كَنْزَ الْمُفْتَقِرِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Treasure of the ones who have nothing!*

WA YAA KANZAL-MUF-TAQIREEN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا جابِـرَ الْمُنْكَسِرِينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Mender of the ones who are broken!*

WA YAA JAABIRAL-MUNKASIREEN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا مَأْوَى الْمُنْقَطِعِينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O refuge of the ones who are separated!*

WA YAA MAA-WAAL-MUNQAT'IE'EN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا ناصِرَ الْمُسْتَضْعَفِينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Helper of the ones who are oppressed!*

WA YAA NAAS'IRAL-MUS-TAZ''-A'FEEN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا مُجِيرَ الْخآئِفِينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Granter of sanctuary to the fearful!*

WA YAA MUJEERAL-KHAAA-IFEEN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا مُغِيثَ الْمَكْرُوبِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Aider of the ones who are in trouble!*

*WA YAA MUGHEETHAL-MAK-ROOBEEN*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا حِصْنَ اللاَّجِينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Fortress of the refugees!*

WA YAA H'IS'-NAL-LAAJEEN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إنْ لَمْ أَعُذْ بِعِزَّتِكَ فَبِمَنْ أَعُوذُ ؟
  </p>
</blockquote>

*If I do not seek refuge in your absolute might, in whom shall I seek
refuge?*

IL-LAM AU'D' BII'Z-ZATIKA FABIMAN AU'WD'

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَإنْ لَمْ أَلُذْ بِقُدْرَتِكَ فَبِمَنْ أَلُوذُ ؟
  </p>
</blockquote>

*If I do not seek shelter in your all compassing power, in whom shall I
seek shelter?*

WA IL-LAM ALUD' BIQUD-RATIKA FABIMAN ALOOD'

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَقَدْ أَلْجَأَتْنِي الذُّنُـوبُ إلَى التَّشَبُّثِ بِأَذْيالِ
عَفْوِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My sins have made me seek protection by holding onto the skirts of your
pardon,*

WA QAD AL-JAAT-NEED'-D'UNOOBU ILAAT-TASHAB-BUTHI BID'-YAALI A'F-WIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَحْوَجَتْنِي الْخَطايا إلَى اسْتِفْتاحِ أَبْـوابِ صَفْحِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My offenses have compelled me to beg the opening of the doors of your
forgiveness,*

WA AH'-WAJAT-NEEL-KHAT'AAYAAA ILAA AS-TIF-TAAHEE AB-WAABI S'AF-H'IK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَدَعَتْنِي الإِسآءَةُ إلَى الإِنـاخَةِ بِفِنآءِ عِزِّكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My bad actions have summoned me to dismount in the courtyard of your
might,*

WA DAA'T-NEEL-ISAAA-ATU ILAAL-INAAKHATI BIFINAAA-I I'Z-ZIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَحَمَلَتْنِي الْمَخافَةُ مِنْ نِقْمَتِكَ عَلَى التَّمَسُّكِ
بِعُرْوَةِ عَطْفِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My fear of receiving your punishment has pushed me to cling to the
handhold of your tenderness!*

WA H'AMALAT-NEEL-MAKHAAFATU MIN-NAQIMATIKA A'LAAT-TAMAS-SUKI BIU'R-WATI
A'T'-FIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَما حَقُّ مَنِ اعْتَصَمَ بِحَبْلِكَ أَنْ يُخْذَلَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*It is not right for him who holds fast to your rope to be abandoned,*

WA MAAA AH'AQ-QU MANI AA'-TAS'AMA BIH'AB-LIKA AY-YUKH-D'AL

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَلا يَلِيقُ بِمَنِ اسْتَجَـارَ بِعِزِّكَ أَنْ يُسْلَمَ أَوْ يُهْمَلَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*nor proper for him who seeks the sanctuary of your might to be forsaken
or disregarded.*

WA LAA YALEEQU BIMANI AS-TAJAARA BII'Z-ZIKA AY-YUS-LAMA AW YUH-MALA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إلهِي فَلا تُخْلِنا مِنْ حِمايَتِكَ، وَلاَ تُعْرِنَا مِنْ رِعَايَتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God, do not withdraw your support and do not take away your guarding
of me*

ILAAHEE FALAA TUKH-LINAA MIN H'IMAAYATIK WA LAA TUA'-RINAA
MIR-RIA'AYATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَذُدْنا عَنْ مَـوارِدِ الْهَلَكَةِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*protect us from the roads that lead to ruin,*

WA D'UD-NAA A'M-MAWAARIDIL-HALAKAH

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَإنَّا بِعَيْنِكَ وَفِي كَنَفِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*for we are in your eye and under your wing!*

FA IN-NAA BIAY-NIKA WA FEE KANAFIKA WA LAK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَلَكَ أَسْأَلُـكَ بِأَهْـل خاصَّتِكَ مِنْ مَلائِكَتِكَ ،
وَالصَّالِحِينَ مِنْ بَرِيَّتِكَ، أَنْ تَجْعَلَ عَلَيْنا واقِيَةً
  </p>
</blockquote>

*I ask you by those whom you have chosen, be they your angels or the
righteous ones, to assign over us a protector*

AS-ALUKA BIH-LI KHAAAS'-S'ATIKA MIM-MALAAA-IKATIKA WAS'-S'AALIH'EENA
MIM-BAREE-YATIKA AN TAJ-A'LA A'LAY-NAA WAAQEEAH

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
تُنْجِينا مِنَ الْهَلَكاتِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Through which to save us from destruction,*

TUNJEENAA MINAL-HALAKAT

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَتُجَنِّبُنا مِنَ الآفاتِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And to turn us away from calamities,*

WA TUJAN-NIBUNAA MINAL-AAAFAAT

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَتُكِنُّنا مِنْ دَواهِي الْمُصِيباتِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And to and hide us from the striking of great afflictions,*

WA TUKIN-NUNAA MIN DAWAAHEEL-MUS'EEBAAT

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَنْ تُنْزِلَ عَلَيْنا مِنْ سِكَيْنَتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And to send down upon us tranquility,*

WA AN TUNZILA A'LAY-NAA MIN SAKEENATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَنْ تُغَشِّيَ وُجُوهَنا بِأَنْوارِ مَحَبَّتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And to wrap our faces in the lights of your love*

WA AN TUGHASH-SHEEA WUJOOHANAA BINWAARI MAH'AB-BATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَنْ تُؤْوِيَنا إلى شَدِيدِ رُكْنِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And to give us a place of safety in your strong support,*

WA AN TOO-WIYANAAA ILAA SHADEEDI RUK-NIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَنْ تَحْوِيَنا فِي أَكْنافِ عِصْمَتِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And to gather us under the wings of your preservation!*

WA AN TAH'-WIYANAA FEEE AK-NAAFI I'S'-MATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِرأْفَتِكَ وَرَحْمَتِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*By your clemency and your mercy, !*

BIRAA-FATIKA WA RAH'-MATIKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يا أَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِينَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Most Merciful of the merciful*

YAAA AR-H'AMAR-RAAH'IMEEN
