11) His Supplication for Good Outcomes
======================================

> (11) وَ كَانَ مِنْ دُعَائِهِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلَامُ بِخَوَاتِمِ الْخَيْرِ

> 1. يا مَنْ ذِكْرُهُ شَرَفٌ لِلذَّاكِرِينَ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يَا مَنْ شُكْرُهُ فَوْزٌ لِلشَّاكِرِينَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يَا مَنْ طَاعَتُهُ نَجَاةٌ لِلْمُطِيعِينَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اشْغَلْ قُلُوبَنَا بِذِكْرِكَ عَنْ كُلِّ ذِكْرٍ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَلْسِنَتَنَا بِشُكْرِكَ عَنْ كُلِّ شُكْرٍ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ جَوَارِحَنَا بِطَاعَتِكَ عَنْ كُلِّ طَاعَةٍ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 2. فَإِنْ قَدَّرْتَ لَنَا فَرَاغاً مِنْ شُغْلٍ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَاجْعَلْهُ فَرَاغَ سَلَامَةٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لَا تُدْرِكُنَا فِيهِ تَبِعَةٌ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَا تَلْحَقُنَا فِيهِ سَأْمَةٌ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
حَتَّى يَنْصَرِفَ عَنَّا كُتَّابُ السَّيِّئَاتِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِصَحِيفَةٍ خَالِيَةٍ مِنْ ذِكْرِ سَيِّئَاتِنَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يَتَوَلَّى كُتَّابُ الْحَسَنَاتِ عَنَّا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَسْرُورِينَ بِمَا كَتَبُوا مِنْ حَسَنَاتِنَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 3. وَ إِذَا انْقَضَتْ أَيَّامُ حَيَاتِنَا ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تَصَرَّمَتْ مُدَدُ أَعْمَارِنَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اسْتَحْضَرَتْنَا دَعْوَتُكَ الَّتِي لَا بُدَّ مِنْهَا وَ مِنْ
إِجَابَتِهَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَصَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اجْعَلْ خِتَامَ مَا تُحْصِي عَلَيْنَا كَتَبَةُ أَعْمَالِنَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
تَوْبَةً مَقْبُولَةً
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لَا تُوقِفُنَا بَعْدَهَا عَلَى ذَنْبٍ اجْتَرَحْنَاهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَا مَعْصِيَةٍ اقْتَرَفْنَاهَا .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 4. وَ لَا تَكْشِفْ عَنَّا سِتْراً سَتَرْتَهُ عَلَى رُءُوسِ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْأَشْهَادِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَوْمَ تَبْلُو أَخْبَارَ عِبَادِكَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 5. إِنَّكَ رَحِيمٌ بِمَنْ دَعَاكَ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مُسْتَجِيبٌ لِمَنْ نَادَاكَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

1- O He remembering whom brings honour  
 to those who remember!  
 O He thanking whom brings triumph  
 to those who give thanks!  
 O He obeying whom brings deliverance  
 to those who obey!  
 Bless Muhammad and his Household,  
 and divert our hearts through remembering Thee  
 from every act of remembrance,  
 our tongues through thanking Thee  
 from every act of thanksgiving,  
 our limbs through obeying Thee  
 from every act of obedience!

2- If Thou hast ordained for us idleness from these occupations,  
 make it an idleness of safety, within which no ill consequence visits
us or weariness overtakes us!  
 Then the writers of evil deeds may depart from us with a page empty of
the mention of our evil deeds,  
 and the writers of good deeds may turn away from us happy with the good
deeds of ours which they have written.

3- And when the days of our life have passed by,  
 the terms of our lifetimes have elapsed,  
 and Thy call, which must come and be answered, summons us forth,  
 then bless Muhammad and his Household  
 and make the outcome of what the writers of our works count against
us  
 an accepted repentance,  
 which afterwards gave us no rest  
 in a sin that we committed  
 or an act of disobedience that we performed!

4- Remove not from us any covering  
 with which Thou hast covered over the heads of the witnesses  
 on the day when the records of Thy servants are tried!

5- Verily Thou art compassionate to him who supplicates Thee,  
 the responder to him who calls upon Thee!
