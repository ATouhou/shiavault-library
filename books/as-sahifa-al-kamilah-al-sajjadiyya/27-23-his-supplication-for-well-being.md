23) His Supplication for Well-Being
===================================

> (23) وَ كَانَ مِنْ دُعَائِهِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلَامُ إِذَا سَأَلَ اللَّهَ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْعَافِيَةَ وَ شُكْرَهَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

His Supplication when he Asked God for Well-Being and Thanked Him for it

> 1. اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَلْبِسْنِي عَافِيَتَكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ جَلِّلْنِي عَافِيَتَكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ حَصِّنِّي بِعَافِيَتِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَكْرِمْنِي بِعَافِيَتِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَغْنِنِي بِعَافِيَتِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تَصَدَّقْ عَلَيَّ بِعَافِيَتِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ هَبْ لِي عَافِيَتَكَ وَ أَفْرِشْنِي عَافِيَتَكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَصْلِحْ لِي عَافِيَتَكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَا تُفَرِّقْ بَيْنِي وَ بَيْنَ عَافِيَتِكَ فِي الدُّنْيَا وَ
الْآخِرَةِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 2. اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ عَافِنِي عَافِيَةً كَافِيَةً شَافِيَةً عَالِيَةً نَامِيَةً ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَافِيَةً تُوَلِّدُ فِي بَدَنِي الْعَافِيَةَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَافِيَةَ الدُّنْيَا وَ الْآخِرَةِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 3. وَ امْنُنْ عَلَيَّ بِالصِّحَّةِ وَ الْأَمْنِ وَ السَّلَامَةِ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فِي دِينِي وَ بَدَنِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ الْبَصِيرَةِ فِي قَلْبِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ النَّفَاذِ فِي أُمُورِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ الْخَشْيَةِ لَكَ ، وَ الْخَوْفِ مِنْكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ الْقُوَّةِ عَلَى مَا أَمَرْتَنِي بِهِ مِنْ طَاعَتِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ الِاجْتِنَابِ لِمَا نَهَيْتَنِي عَنْهُ مِنْ مَعْصِيَتِكَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 4.اللَّهُمَّ وَ امْنُنْ عَلَيَّ بِالْحَجِّ وَ الْعُمْرَةِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ زِيَارَةِ قَبْرِ رَسُولِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
صَلَواتُكَ عَلَيْهِ وَ رَحْمَتُكَ وَ بَرَكَاتُكَ عَلَيْهِ وَ عَلَى
آلِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ آلِ رَسُولِكَ عَلَيْهِمُ السَّلَامُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَبَداً مَا أَبْقَيْتَنِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فِي عَامِي هَذَا وَ فِي كُلِّ عَامٍ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اجْعَلْ ذَلِكَ مَقْبُولًا مَشْكُوراً ، مَذْكُوراً لَدَيْكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَذْخُوراً عِنْدَكَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 5. وَ أَنْطِقْ بِحَمْدِكَ وَ شُكْرِكَ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ ذِكْرِكَ وَ حُسْنِ الثَّنَاءِ عَلَيْكَ لِسَانِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اشْرَحْ لِمَرَاشِدِ دِينِكَ قَلْبِي .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 6. وَ أَعِذْنِي وَ ذُرِّيَّتِي مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ شَرِّ السَّامَّةِ وَ الْهَامَّةِ و الْعَامَّةِ وَ اللَّامَّةِ
،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ شَيْطَانٍ مَرِيدٍ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ سُلْطَانٍ عَنِيدٍ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ مُتْرَفٍ حَفِيدٍ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ ضَعِيفٍ وَ شَدِيدٍ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ شَرِيفٍ وَ وَضِيعٍ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ صَغِيرٍ وَ كَبِيرٍ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ قَرِيبٍ وَ بَعِيدٍ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ مَنْ نَصَبَ لِرَسُولِكَ وَ لِأَهْلِ بَيْتِهِ
حَرْباً مِنَ الْجِنِّ وَ الْإِنْسِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ دَابَّةٍ أَنْتَ آخِذٌ بِنَاصِيَتِهَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِنَّكَ عَلَى صِرَاطٍ مُسْتَقِيمٍ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 7. اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مَنْ أَرَادَنِي بِسُوءٍ فَاصْرِفْهُ عَنِّي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ ادْحَرْ عَنِّي مَكْرَهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ ادْرَأْ عَنِّي شَرَّهُ ، وَ رُدَّ كَيْدَهُ فِي نَحْرِهِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 8. وَ اجْعَلْ بَيْنَ يَدَيْهِ سُدّاً حَتَّى تُعْمِيَ عَنِّي بَصَرَهُ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تُصِمَّ عَنْ ذِكْرِي سَمْعَهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تُقْفِلَ دُونَ إِخْطَارِي قَلْبَهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تُخْرِسَ عَنِّي لِسَانَهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تَقْمَعَ رَأْسَهُ ، وَ تُذِلَّ عِزَّهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تَكْسُرَ جَبَرُوتَهُ ، وَ تُذِلَّ رَقَبَتَهُ ، وَ تَفْسَخَ كِبْرَهُ
،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تُؤْمِنَنِي مِنْ جَمِيعِ ضَرِّهِ وَ شَرِّهِ وَ غَمْزِهِ وَ هَمْزِهِ
وَ لَمْزِهِ وَ حَسَدِهِ وَ عَدَاوَتِهِ وَ حَبَائِلِهِ وَ مَصَايِدِهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ رَجِلِهِ وَ خَيْلِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِنَّكَ عَزِيزٌ قَدِيرٌ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

1- O God,  
 bless Muhammad and his Household,  
 clothe me in Thy well-being,  
 wrap me in Thy well-being,  
 fortify me through Thy well-being,  
 honour me with Thy well-being,  
 free me from need through Thy well-being,  
 donate to me Thy well-being,  
 bestow upon me Thy well-being,  
 spread out for me Thy well-being,  
 set Thy well-being right for me,  
 and separate me not from Thy well-being  
 in this world and the next!

2- O God,  
 bless Muhammad and his Household  
 and make me well with a well-being sufficient, healing, sublime,
growing,  
 a well-being that will give birth to well-being in my body,  
 a well-being in this world and the next!

3- Oblige me through  
 health, security, and safety  
 in my religion and body,  
 insight in my heart,  
 penetration in my affairs,  
 dread of Thee, fear of Thee,  
 strength for the obedience  
 which Thou hast commanded for me,  
 and avoidance of the disobedience  
 which Thou hast prohibited for me!

4- O God,  
 oblige me through  
 the *hajj*,  
 the *umra*,[^1]  
 and visiting the graves of Thy Messenger  
 (Thy blessings, mercy, and benedictions upon him and upon his
Household)  
 and the Household of Thy Messenger (upon them be peace)  
 for as long as Thou causest me to live,  
 in this year of mine and in every year,  
 and make that accepted, thanked, and mentioned before Thee  
 and stored away with Thee!

5- Make my tongue utter Thy praise, Thy thanksgiving,  
 Thy remembrance, and Thy excellent laudation,  
 and expand my heart  
 toward the right goals of Thy religion!

6- Give me and my progeny refuge from  
 the accursed Satan,  
 the evil of venomous vermin, threatening pests,  
 swarming crowds, and evil eyes,  
 the evil of *every rebel satan*,[^2]  
 the evil of every refractory sovereign,  
 the evil of everyone living in ease and served,  
 the evil of everyone weak or strong,  
 the evil of everyone born high or low,  
 the evil of everyone small or great,  
 the evil of everyone near or far,  
 the evil of everyone, jinn or man, who declares war on Thy Messenger
and his Household,

and the evil of every *crawling creature*  
 that Thou hast *taken by the forelock*!  
*Surely* Thou art *on a straight path*.[^3]

7- O God,  
 bless Muhammad and his Household  
 and if someone desires ill for me  
 turn him away from me,  
 drive away from me his deception,  
 avert from me his evil, send his trickery back to his own throat,

8- and place before him a barricade,  
 so that Thou mayest blind his eyes toward me,  
 deafen his ears toward my mention,  
 lock his heart toward recalling me,  
 silence his tongue against me,  
 restrain his head, abase his exaltation,  
 break his arrogance, abase his neck, disjoint his pride,

and make me secure from all his injury,  
 his evil, his slander,  
 his backbiting, his faultfinding,  
 his envy, his enmity,  
 his snares, his traps,  
 his foot soldiers, and his cavalry!  
 Surely Thou art Mighty, Powerful!

[^1]: That is, the lesser pilgrimage to Mecca, made outside of the
season of the hajj.

[^2]: 22:3

[^3]: 11:56
