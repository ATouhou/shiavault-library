Letters of Imam ‘Ali Amir Al-Mu’minin, Peace Be On Him
======================================================

Selections from the writings of our master Amir al-Mu’minin, ‘Ali Ibn
Abi Talib and his letters to his enemies and the governors of his
provinces, including selections of his letters of appointment to his
administrative officers and his injunctions to members of his family and
his companions

  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
باب مختار من كتب مولانا أمير المؤمنين علي (عليه السلام)
  </p>
</blockquote>

الى عماله و وصاياه لأهله و أصحابه .
  </p>
</blockquote>
