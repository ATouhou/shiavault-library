Introduction
============

***In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

The Fourfold Characteristics Of The Prophets
--------------------------------------------

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَنِ الصَّادِقِ عَلَيهِ السَّلامُ قَالَ: إِنَّ الصَّبرَ وَالصِّدْقَ
وَالحِلمَ وَحُسْنَ الخُلقِ مِن أَخْلاقِ الأَنبِياءِ عَلَيهِمُ
السَّلامُ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

It has been narrated on the authority of Imam Sadeq peace be upon him:
Patience, truthfulness, forbearance and good temper are some of the
characteristics of the Prophets.

Visitation
----------

It has been reported on the authority of the holy Prophet of Islam : The
best habit of Prophets and the righteous ones is visitation of one
another for the sake of God.

Cheerfulness And Shaking Hand
-----------------------------

Jaber Ibn Abdollah Anseri has quoted the holy Prophet as saying: A sign
of Prophets and the righteous ones was that when they met, cheerfulness
was seen from their faces and they shook hands.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَنْ جابِرِ بنِ عَبدِ اللّهِ الأَنْصاري، عَن النَّبيّ صلَّى اللّه
عَلَيهِ وَآلهِ: مِنْ أَخْلاقِ النّبيّينَ وَالصِّدِّيقِينَ:
الْبَشَاشَةُ إِذا تراءُوا، وَالمُصَافَحَةُ إِذا تَلاقَوا.
  </p>
</blockquote>

The Prophets’ Dislike Of Affectation And Undue Trouble
------------------------------------------------------

The holy Prophet has said: "We Prophets, the Trustworthy and the pious
ones do not like affectation and undue trouble".

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قَالَ النَّبيُّ صَلَّى اللّهُ عَلَيْهِ وَآلهِ : نَحْنُ مَعاشِرَ
الأَنْبِياءِ وَ الأُمناءِ وَ الأَتْقِياء بُرَآءُ مِنَ التَّكَلّفِ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Prophets And Moral Virtues
--------------------------

Imam Sadeq has said: God Almighty has made Prophets distinguished for
their moral virtues. whoever has such virtues must be thankful to God
and whoever lacks them has to beseech God for them. Imam Sadeq was
asked: What are moral virtues? The Imam said: Piety, moderation in
spending patience, thankfulness, forbearance, modesty, generosity,
courage, zeal, truthfulness, benefaction, trustworthiness, certainty,
good temper, and fairness".

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قالَ الصّادقُ عَلَيهِ السَّلامُ : إِنَّ اللّه خصَّ الأَنْبِياءَ
عَلَيْهِمُ السَّلامُ بِمَكارِمِ الأَخْلاق فَمَنْ كانَتْ فيهِ
فَليَحْمَدِ اللّه عَلى ذلكَ وَمَنْ لمْ تَكُنْ فيهِ فَلْيَتَضَرَّعْ
إِلى اللّهِ وَلِيَسْأَلهُ إِيَّاها، قيلَ لَهُ: وَما هِيَ؟ قَالَ
عَلَيهِ السَّلامُ: الوَرَعُ وَالْقَناعَةُ وَالصَّبْرُ وَالشُّكْرُ
وَالحِلْمُ وَالحَياءُ وَالسَّخاءُ وَالشَّجاعَةُ وَالغَيْرَةُ وَصِدْقُ
الحديث وَالبرُّ وَأَداءُ الأَمانَةِ وَالْيَقِينُ وَحُسنُ الخُلُقِ
وَالْمُرُوءة.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Prophet’s Salient Characteristics
---------------------------------

Imam Sadeq has said: The Prophets, four salient characteristics are: 1-
Benefaction:2- Generosity:3- Patience during disaster and unpleasant
events:4- Act in relation to a believer’s right.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قَالَ الصَّادِقُ عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ: أَرْبَعَةٌ مِنْ أَخْلاقِ
الأَنْبِياءِ عَلَيْهِمُ السَّلامُ: البِرُّ وَالسَّخاءُ وَالصَّبرُ
عَلَى النّائِبةِ وَالقِيامُ بِحَقِّ الْمُؤمِنِ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

To Perfume The Whisker
----------------------

It has been reported on the authority of Imam Sadeq : "To perfume the
whisker is the habit of Prophets and respect for the honorable
recorders."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَنِ أَبي عَبدِ اللّهِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ قالَ: الطّيبُ في الشَّاربِ
مِن أَخْلاقِ النَّبِيِّينَ وَكَرامَةُ الكاتِبينَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Cleanliness
-----------

It has been reported on the authority of Imam Reza : "Cleanliness is one
of the characteristics of the Prophets."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَنِ الرِّضا عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ: مِنْ أَخْلاقِ الأَنبِياءِ
التَّنَظُّفُ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

To Have Walking Stick
---------------------

The holy Prophet has said to have a walking stick is a sign of a
believer and the habit of the Prophets. In another hadith, we read:
"Leaning against a walking stick is a habit of the Prophets."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قالَ رَسُولُ اللّهُ عَلَيْهِ وآلهِ: حَمْلُ الْعَصا عَلامَةُ الْمُؤمِنِ
وَسُنَّةُ الأَنْبِيَاءِ و في رِوايَةٍ أُخْرى: التَّوكُّؤُ عَلَى الْعصا
مِن أَخلاقِ الأَنْبِياء.
  </p>
</blockquote>

The Fivefold Characteristics Of The Prophets
--------------------------------------------

Imam Reza has said: "Roosters have five characteristics of the Prophets:
1- Knowing the prayer timing 2- Zeal 3- Generosity 4- courage 5- Love of
one’s wife

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قال الرِّضا عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ: في الدّيكِ الأَبْيَضِ خَمْسُ خِصالٍ
مِنْ خِصالِ الأَنْبِيَاءِ معْرِفَتُهُ بِأَوْقاتِ الصَّلاةِ،
وَالغَيْرَةُ وَالسَّخاءُ وَالشُّجاعَةُ وَكَثْرَةُ الطَّرُوقَةِ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Being Kind To Ladies
--------------------

Imam Sadeq has said: "Being Kind to ladies is among the Prophets
habits."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قالَ أَبو عَبْدِ اللَّهِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ: مِنْ أَخْلاقِ
الأَنْبِياءِ حُبُّ النِّساءِ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

The Way The Prophets Slept
--------------------------

Imam Ali said: "Prophets sleep on their back, their eyes do not sleep,
for they are looking forward for receiving revelation."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قالَ أَميرُ المُؤمِنينَ عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ: الأَنْبياءُ تَنَامُ عَلى
أَقْفِيَتِها، مُسْتَلْقِيَةً وأَعْيُنُهُمْ لا تَنَامُ مُتوَقِّعَةً
لِوَحْيِ اللّهِ عَزَّ وَجَلَّ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

The Prophet’s Vigil
-------------------

It has been reported on the authority of Imam Ali : "Staying awake to
pray leads to physical health, God’s pleasure and Mercy as well as
restoration to the Prophet’s moral virtues."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَنْ عَلِيٍّ عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ: قَالَ: قِيامُ اللَّيْلِ مُصِحَّةٌ
لِلبَدَنِ وَمَرْضَاةٌ لِلرَّبِّ عَزَّ وَجَلَّ وَتَعَرُّضٌ لِلرَّحْمَةِ
وَتَمَسُّكٌ بِأَخْلاقِ النَّبِيّينَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

Keeping Away From A Sinful Look
-------------------------------

Imam Sadeq quoting Prophet Moses in the story of Prophet Shuaib’s
daughter as saying: "Walk behind me and lead the way, for we — the
Prophets — do not look at women’s rumps."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَنْ جَعْفَرِ بْنِ مُحَمّدٍ عَلَيْهما السَّلامُ : وَقالَ مُوسى : كُوني
خَلفي وَعَرِّفيني الطَّريقَ فَإِنّا [ الأَنبياء ] قَوْمٌ لا نَنْظُرُ
إِلى أَدْبَارِ النِّساءِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

The Prophet’s Forgiveness
-------------------------

It has been reported on the authority of Imam Sadeq : To be powerful and
to forgive is one of the habits of the Prophets and the pious ones."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَنِ الصّادِقِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ قالَ أَميرُ الْمُؤْمِنينَ عَلَيْهِ
السَّلامُ: العَفْوُ عِنْدَ الْقُدْرَةِ مِنْ سُنَنِ الْمُرْسَلينَ
وَالْمُتَّقينَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Avoiding Flattery
-----------------

Imam Ali has said: "Flattery is not the habit of the Prophets."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قال أَميرُ الْمُؤْمِنينَ عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ : لَيْسَ المَلَقُ مِنْ
خُلُقِ الأَنْبِيَاءِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

The Prophets’ Way Of Speaking
-----------------------------

The holy Prophet has said: "We — the Prophets — have been commissioned
to speak to people to the extent of their understanding."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قالَ رَسُولُ اللّهِ صَلَّى اللّهُ عَلَيْهِ وَآله : إِنّا مَعاشِرَ
الأَنبِياءِ أُمِرْنا أَنْ نُكلِّمَ النّاسَ عَلى قَدْرِ عُقُولِهِمْ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

The Prophet’s Heritage
----------------------

The holy Prophet has said: "Surely the Prophets have bequeathed neither
Dirham nor Dinar but they have left a bequest of Knowledge."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قَالَ رَسُولُ اللّهِ صَلَّى اللّهُ عَلَيْهِ وَآلِهِ : إِنَّ
الأَنْبِياءَ لَمْ يُوَرِّثُوا دِينَارًا وَلا دِرْهَمًا ولكِن وَرَّثُوا
العِلمَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

The Prophet’s Supper
--------------------

Imam Ali has said: "the Prophets would have dinner after performing
their night prayer. Therefore, do not forsake dinner, for forsaking it
will affect your health."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قال أَميرُ الْمُؤْمِنينَ عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ : عِشاءُ النَّبِيّينَ
بَعدَ العَتمةِ فلا تَدَعُوهُ فإِنَّ تَرْكَ العِشاءِ خَرَابُ البَدَنِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

Farming And Shepherdism
-----------------------

Muhammad Ibn Attiah has reported on the authority of Imam Sadeq : "God
Almighty likes farming and shepherdism for His messengers so that they
will not dislike rainfall."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَن مُحَمَّدِ بنِ عَطيةِ قالَ : سَمِعتُ أَبا عبدِ اللّهِ عليهِ
السَّلامُ يَقُولُ : إِنَّ اللّه عزَّ وَجلَّ أَحَبَّ لأَنبِيائه
عَلَيهمُ السَّلامُ مِنَ الأَعمالِ الْحَرْثَ وَالرَّعيَ لِئلا يَكرَهوا
شَيْئًا مِنْ قِطرِ السَّماءِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

Brushing One’s Teeth
--------------------

It has been reported on the authority of Imam Sadeq: "To brush one’s
teeth is the habit of the Prophets."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَن أَبي عَبدِ اللَّهِ عليهِ السَّلامُ قالَ : السِّواكُ مِن سُنَنِ
المُرسَلينَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

Truthfulness And Trustworthiness
--------------------------------

It has been reported on the authority of Imam Sadeq : "God Almighty has
not ordained a messenger save with truthfulness and trustworthiness
towards both benefactor and wrongdoer."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَن أَبي عبدِ اللّه عَليهِ السَّلامُ قالَ : إِنَّ اللّه عَزَّ وَجَلَّ
لَم يَبعث نَبِيًّا إِلا بِصدقِ الحَديث وَاداءِ الأَمانةِ إِلى البَرِّ
وَالفاجِرِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

Where The Prophets Cannot Endure
--------------------------------

It has been reported on the authority of Imam Sadeq : "God Almighty
afflicted Prophet Job (Ayyub) without having committed any sin to the
extent that people reproached him and surely the Prophets cannot endure
reproach."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَن أَبي عَبدِ اللّهِ عَليهِ السَّلامُ قالَ : إِنَّ اللّهَ تَباركَ
وَتَعالى ابْتَلَى أَيُّوبَ عَليهِ السَّلامُ بِلا ذَنْبٍ . فَصَبرَ
حَتّى عُيِّرَ وَإِنَّ الأَنبياءَ لا يَصبِرونَ عَلى التَّعييرِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

The Prophets And Perfume
------------------------

It has been reported on the authority of Imam Reza: "Wearing perfume is
the habit of the Prophets."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قالَ عَليُّ بنُ مُوسى الرِّضا عَليهِ السَّلامُ : الطّيبُ مِن أَخلاقِ
الأَنبياء عَلَيهمُ السَّلامُ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

The Prophets And Tribulation
----------------------------

It has been reported on the authority of Prophet Jacob (Yaqoob) :
"Tribulation afflicts the Prophets and those who resemble us more
immediately than any one else."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَن يَعقُوب النَّبيّ عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ : إِنّا مَعْشَرَ الأَنْبِيَاءَ
أَسْرَعُ شَيءٍ البلاءُ إِلينا ، ثُمَّ الأَمثل فَالأَمْثَل النّاسَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

Prophets And Immaculacy
-----------------------

Imam Sadeq has reported on the authority of his father, Imam Baqer : The
Prophets are immaculate; they are innocent and pure; they do not commit
any sin nor are unjust. They do not even commit mortal or venial sin."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَن جَعفَرِ بنِ مُحَمَّدٍ عَليه السَّلامُ عَنْ أَبِيهِ عَلَيْهِ
السَّلامُ إِنَّ الأَنْبِيَاءَ لا يَذنُبُونَ ،لأَنَّهُم مَعصُومُونَ
مُطهَّرُونَ لا يُذْنِبُونَ وَلا يزيغُونَ وَلا يَرتكِبُونَ ذَنْبًا
صَغِيرًا وَلا كَبِيرًا .
  </p>
</blockquote>

Longevity In The Light Of Obedience To God
------------------------------------------

Imam Ali has said: "Longevity in the light of obedience to God is one of
the characteristics of Prophets."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قال أَميرُ الْمُؤْمِنينَ عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ : طُولُ العُمرِ مَعَ
الطاعةِ مِن خُلُقِ الأَنْبِيَاءِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

Perfume, Espouse, And Toothbrush
--------------------------------

It has been reported on the authority of Imam Sadeq : "Prophets are
endowed with three virtues: Wearing Perfume, love of espouse, and
brushing their teeth."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَن أَبي عَبْدِ اللَّهِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ قالَ : ثَلاثٌ أُعطيهِنَّ
الأَنْبِيَاءَ عَلَيْهِمُ السَّلامُ : العِطرُ ، وَالأَزْوَاجُ ،
والسّواكُ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

Prophets’ Generosity
--------------------

It has been reported on the authority of Imam Moosa Ibn Ja’afar : "All
Prophets and their successors were generous."

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَن أَبي الحَسنِ مُوسى عَليهِ السَّلامُ قالَ : ما بَعَثَ اللَّهُ
نَبِيًّا وَلا وَصِيًّا إِلا سَخِيًّا .
  </p>
</blockquote>

Prophets’ Weapon
----------------

It has been reported on the authority of Imam Reza who said to his
disciples: Resort to the Prophets’ weapon. They asked: What is the
weapon of Prophets? The Imam answered: Doa.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَنِ الرِّضَا عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ أَنَّهُ كَانَ يَقُولُ لأَصْحَابِهِ :
عَليكُم بِسِلاحِ الأَنْبِيَاءِ ، فَقِيلَ : وَما سِلاحُ الأَنْبِيَاءِ ؟
قَالَ : الدُّعَاءُ .
  </p>
</blockquote>
