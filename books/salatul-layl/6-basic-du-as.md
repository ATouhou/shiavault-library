Basic Du’as
===========

Du’a before beginning Salatul Layl
----------------------------------

<blockquote dir="rtl">

  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللّهُمَّ اِنِّي اَتَوَجَّهُ اِلَيْكَ بِنَبِيِّكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>

  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">

<blockquote dir="rtl">

فِي الدُّنْيَا وَ الآخِرَةِ
  </p>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنَ الْمُقَرَّبِيْنَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللّهُمَّ ارْحَمْنِي بِهِمْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

### English translation of Du’a before beginning Salatul Layl

In the name of Allah the Beneficent, the Merciful  
 O Allah bless Muhammad and his family  
 O Allah I turn to You through Your Prophet  
 the Prophet of mercy  
 and (through) his family  
 and I place them in front of my needs  
 so make me  
 through them,  
 respected, in the world  
 and in the hereafter  
 and from the near ones  
 O Allah have mercy on me  
 through them

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لاَ تُعَذِّبْنِي بِهِمْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اهْدِنِي بِهِمْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لاَ تُضِلَّنِي بِهِمْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ ارْزُقْنِي بِهِمْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لاَ تَحْرِمْنِي بِهِمْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اقْضِ لِي حَوَائِجَ الدُّنْيَا وَ الآَخِرَةِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اِنَّكَ عَلى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيْرٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ بِكُلِّ شَيْءٍ عَلِيْمٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

and do not punish me  
 through them  
 and guide me  
 through them  
 and do not lead me astray  
 through them  
 and grant me sustenance  
 through them  
 and do not deny me  
 through them  
 and fulfill my needs  
 of the world and the hereafter  
 Surely You have power over all things  
 and (You) are aware of all things

----------------------------------------

بِسْمِ اللّهِ الرَّحْمنِ الرَّحِيْمِ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>

<blockquote dir="rtl">

### English translation of Du’a after 8 raka’ats of Nafilah of Layl

In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful  
 O Allah bless Muhammad and his family

O Allah You are the Ever Living, the Eternal  
 the Elevated, the Mighty  
 the Creator, the Sustainer  
 the One who gives life  
 and the One who causes to die  
 the Beginner, the Unique

For You is nobility  
 and for You is generosity  
 and for You is grace  
 and for You is the command

You are One  
 there is no partner for You

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَا خَالِقُ يَا رَازِقُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَا مُحْيِي يَا مُمِيْتُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَا بَدِيْعُ يَا رَفِيْعُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَسْأَلُكَ اَنْ تُصَلِّيَ عَلى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اَنْ تَرْحَمَ ذُلِّ بَيْنَ يَدَيْكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تَضَرُّعِي اِلَيْكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَحْشَتِي مِنَ النَّاسِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اُنْسِي بِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

O Creator, O Sustainer  
 O the One who gives life  
 and the One who causes to die  
 O the Unique, the Sublime  
 I ask you to bless Muhammad and his family  
 and to have mercy on my lowliness  
 before You  
 and my beseeching You  
 and my loneliness from people  
 and my intimacy with You

Du’a after Salat al-Shaf’a
--------------------------

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِسْمِ اللّهِ الرَّحْمنِ الرَّحِيْمِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اِلهِي تَعَرَّضَ لَكَ فِي هذَا اللَّيْلِ الْمُتَعَرِّضُوْنَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ قَصَدَكَ فِيْهِ الْقَاصِدُوْنَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

  <p>
وَ اَمَّلَ فَضْلَكَ وَ مَعْرُوْفَكَ الطَّالِبُوْنَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَكَ فِي هذَا اللَّيْلِ نَفَحَاتٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

  <p>

  <p>

  </p>
</blockquote>

### English translation of Du’a after Salat al-Shaf’a

In the name of Allah the Beneficent the Merciful  
 O Allah bless Muhammad and his family

O Allah, on this night  
 the seekers have sought You  
 and the aspirers have aspired for You  
 and the searchers have hoped  
 for Your blessings and grace

For You have in this night, hidden bounties  
 and rewards and presents and gifts  
 which You bestow upon whom you please  
 from Your servants  
 and You deny them to one who has not reached Your regard

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ هَا اَنَا ذَا عُبَيْدُكَ الْفَقِيْرُ اِلَيْكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلْمُؤَمِّلُ فَضْلَكَ وَ مَعْرُوْفَكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَاِنْ كُنْتَ يَا مَوْلاَيَ تَفَضَّلْتَ فِي هذِهِ اللَّيْلَةِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَلى اَحَدٍ مِنْ خَلْقِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ عُدْتَ عَلَيْهِ بِعَائِدَةٍ مِنْ عَطْفِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَصَلِّ عَلى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلطَّيِّبِيْنَ الطَّاهِرِيْنَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلْخَيِّرِيْنَ اَلْفَاضِلِيْنَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ جُدْ عَلَيَّ بِطَوْلِكَ وَ مَعْرُوْفِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَا رَبَّ الْعَالَمِيْنَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

Here I am,  
 Your abject servant  
 in need of You  
 hopeful of Your blessings and grace  
 So, O my Master  
 if You have bestowed blessings  
 on this night  
 on anyone of Your creatures  
 and turned to him with affection  
 then bless Muhammad and his family  
 the pure, the purified,  
 the virtuous ones  
 the excellent ones  
 and favor me with Your grace and bounty  
 O Lord of the worlds

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ صَلَّى اللّهُ عَلى مُحَمَّدٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
خَاتَمِ النَّبِيِّيْنَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ آلِهِ الطَّاهِرِيْنَ وَ سَلَّمَ تَسْلِيْمًا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اِنَّ اللّهَ حَمِيْدٌ مَجِيْدٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللّهُمَّ اِنِّي اَدْعُوْكَ كَمَا اَمَرْتَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَاسْتَجِبْ لِي كَمَا وَعَدْتَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اِنَّكَ لاَ تُخْلِفُ الْمِيْعَادَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

And send blessings on Muhammad  
 the seal of the Prophets  
 and his family, the Purified  
 and salute them with  
 a perfect salutation  
 Surely Allah is praiseworthy, Glorious  
 O Allah I have supplicated to You  
 as You have commanded  
 then answer me as You have promised  
 Surely You do not break Your promises.

Du’a after Salatul Witr
-----------------------

This du’a is called Du’a al-Hazeen, the sorrowful du’a. It should be
recited softly and sorrowfully

.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِسْمِ اللّهِ الرَّحْمنِ الرَّحِيْمِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اُنَاجِيْكَ يَا مَوْجُوْدًا فِي كُلِّ مَكَانٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لَعَلَّكَ تَسْمَعُ نِدَائِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَقَدْ عَظُمَ جُرْمِي وَ قَلَّ حَيَائِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَوْلاَيَ يَا مَوْلاَيَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَيُّ اْلاَهْوَالِ اَتَذَكَّرُ وَ اَيُّهَا اَنْسى
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَوْ لَمْ يَكُنْ اِلاَّ الْمَوْتُ لَكَفى
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
كَيْفَ وَ مَا بَعْدَ الْمَوْتِ اَعْظَمُ وَ اَدْهى
  </p>
</blockquote>

### English translation of Du’a after Salatul Witr

In the name of Allah, The Beneficent, the Merciful  
 O Allah bless Muhammad and his family  
 I whisper unto You  
 O one who is present in every place  
 so that You may hear my call  
 for surely my sin is excessive  
 and my shame is less  
 My Master, O my Master  
 which of the terrifying states shall I remember  
 and which of them shall I forget  
 for if there was nothing except death  
 it would be enough  
 then what about after death  
 greater and much worse?

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَوْلاَيَ يَا مَوْلاَيَ حَتَّى مَتى وَ اِلى مَتى
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَقُوْلُ لَكَ الْعُتْبى مَرَّةً بَعْدَ اُخْرى
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ثُمَّ لاَ تَجِدُ عِنْدِي صِدْقًا وَ لاَ وَفَاءً
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَيَا غَوْثَاهُ ثُمَّ وَا غَوْثَاهُ بِكَ يَا اَللّهُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مِنْ هَوىً قَدْ غَلَبَنِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ عَدُوٍّ قَدِ اسْتَكْلَبَ عَلَيَّ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ دُنْياً قَدْ تَزَيَّنَتْ لِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ نَفْسٍ اَمَّارَةٍ بِالسُّوْءِ اِلاَّ مَا رَحِمَ رَبِّي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَوْلاَيَ يَا مَوْلاَيَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اِنْ كُنْتَ رَحِمْتَ مِثْلِي فَارْحَمْنِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

My master O my Master  
 up to when and till when  
 will I say, I am to blame,  
 again and again,  
 but then You do not find any truth or loyalty in me?

I call for help  
 and I call for help, O Allah  
 from desires which have overpowered me  
 and from the enemy which has pounced on me  
 and from the world which attracts me  
 and from ***the soul that leads towards evil except that on which my
Lord has mercy (12:53)***

My master O my master  
 if You have had mercy on the likes of me  
 then have mercy on me

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اِنْ كُنْتَ قَبِلْتَ مِثْلِي فَاقْبَلْنِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَا قَابِلَ السَّحَرَةِ اقْبَلْنِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَا مَنْ لَمْ اَزَلْ اَتَعَرَّفُ مِنْهُ الْحُسْنى
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَا مَنْ يُغَذِّيْنِي بِالنِّعَمِ صَبَاحًا وَ مَسَاءً
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اِرْحَمْنِي يَوْمَ آتِيْكَ فَرْدًا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
شَاخِصًا اِلَيْكَ بَصَرِي مُقَلَّدًا عَمَلِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قَدْ تَبَرَّأَ جَمِيْعُ الْخَلْقِ مِنِّي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
نَعَمْ وَ اَبِي وَ اُمِّي وَ مَنْ كَانَ لَهُ كَدِّي وَ سَعْيِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَاِنْ لَمْ تَرْحَمْنِي فَمَنْ يَرْحَمُنِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مَنْ يُؤْنِسُ فِي الْقَبْرِ وَحْشَتِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

and if You have accepted from the likes of me  
 then accept from me  
 O One who accepts the early morning prayer  
 accept me,  
 O One who, I still know only good from Him  
 O One who nourishes me with blessings  
 morning and evening  
 have mercy on me when I come to You  
 alone,  
 my glance fixed on You  
 my actions carried on my neck  
 When all of creation  
 will withdraw away from me  
 yes, even my father and mother  
 and those for whom I worked and struggled  
 then if You will not have mercy on me  
 who will have mercy on me  
 who will give me solace  
 from the loneliness of the grave

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مَنْ يُنْطِقُ لِسَانِي اِذَا خَلَوْتُ بِعَمَلِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ سَأَلْتَنِي عَمَّا اَنْتَ اَعْلَمُ بِهِ مِنِّي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَاِنْ قُلْتُ نَعَمْ فَاَيْنَ الْمَهْرَبُ مِنْ عَدْلِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اِنْ قُلْتُ لَمْ اَفْعَلْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قُلْتَ اَلَمْ اَكُنِ الشَّاهِدَ عَلَيْكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
