Surah al-Ikhlas, n. 112
=======================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
سْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

**In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful.**

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Say: He, Allah, is One. (112:1)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللَّهُ الصَّمَدُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Allah is He on Whom all depend***[^1]***. (112:2)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لَمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُولَدْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***He begets not, nor is He begotten. (112:3)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَلَمْ يَكُنْ لَهُ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***And none is like Him. (112:4)***

[^1]: Perfect – the compact – independent - Needless
