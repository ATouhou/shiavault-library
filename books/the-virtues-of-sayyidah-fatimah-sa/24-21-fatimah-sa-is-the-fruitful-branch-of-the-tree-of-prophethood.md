21) Fatimah (sa) is the fruitful branch of the tree of Prophethood
==================================================================

> 59. عن المسور رضي الله عنه قال: قال رسول الله صلى الله عليه وسلم:
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فاطمة شجنة منى يبسطنى ما بسطها و يقبضنى ما قبضها.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Miswar (ra) narrates that the Messenger of Allah (saw) said, “Fatimah is
my fruitful branch, I am pleased with what pleases her and I am troubled
by whatever troubles her.”

> 60. عن ابن عباس رضي الله عنهما رفعه: أنا شجرة، و فاطمة حملها، و علي
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لقاحها، و الحسن و الحسين ثمرها، و المحبون أهل البيت ورقها من الجنة
حقـاً حقـاً.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Abdullah bin Abbas (ra) narrates this marfu tradition in which the Holy
Prophet (saw) said, “I am a tree, Fatimah is its branch, Ali is its
flower, Hasan and Husain are its fruit and the lovers of the ‘people of
the house’ are its leaves. All of them will be in Paradise. This is the
truth, this is the truth.”
