8. The Ethos Of Lot
===================

Introduction
------------

Prophet Lot son of Haram, the son of Tarih is Abraham’s nephew. His name
has been mentioned in 14 Quranic chapters. He used to live in a village
called Sodome in Jordan. His people were destroyed with a torment as
mentioned in hagiology.

He And The Divine Knowledge
---------------------------

***"And (as for) Lut, We gave him wisdom and knowledge, " (21:74)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَلُوطًا آتَيْنَاهُ حُكْمًا وَعِلْمًا.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Calling People To Worship God
-----------------------------

***"When their brother Lut said to them: Will you not guard (against
evil)? Surely I am a faithful Apostle to you; therefore guard against
(the punishment of) Allah and obey me." (26:161- 163)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قَالَ لَهُمْ أَخُوهُمْ لُوطٌ أَلَا تَتَّقُونَ. إِنِّي لَكُمْ رَسُولٌ
أَمِينٌ. فَاتَّقُوا اللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُونِ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Communicating The Message Of God
--------------------------------

***"And I do not ask you any reward for it; my reward is only with the
Lord of the worlds." (26:164)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمَا أَسْأَلُكُمْ عَلَيْهِ مِنْ أَجْرٍ ۖ إِنْ أَجْرِيَ إِلَّا عَلَىٰ
رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

Lot’s Invocation
----------------

***"My Lord! Deliver me and my followers from what they do. So We
delivered him and his followers all." (26:169-170)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
رَبِّ نَجِّنِي وَأَهْلِي مِمَّا يَعْمَلُونَ. فَنَجَّيْنَاهُ وَأَهْلَهُ
أَجْمَعِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

Forbidding Evil
---------------

***And (We sent) Lut when he said to his people: Most surely you are
guilty of an indecency which none of the nations has ever done before
you; What! Do you come to the males and commit evil deeds in your
assemblies? But nothing was the answer of his people except that they
said: Bring on us Allah’s punishment, if you are one of the truthful. He
said: My Lord! Help me against the mischievous people." (29:28- 30)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَلُوطًا إِذْ قَالَ لِقَوْمِهِ إِنَّكُمْ لَتَأْتُونَ الْفَاحِشَةَ مَا
سَبَقَكُمْ بِهَا مِنْ أَحَدٍ مِنَ الْعَالَمِينَ. أَئِنَّكُمْ
لَتَأْتُونَ الرِّجَالَ وَتَقْطَعُونَ السَّبِيلَ وَتَأْتُونَ فِي
نَادِيكُمُ الْمُنْكَرَفَمَا كَانَ جَوَابَ قَوْمِهِ إِلَّا أَنْ قَالُوا
ائْتِنَا بِعَذَابِ اللَّهِ إِنْ كُنْتَ مِنَ الصَّادِقِينَ. قَالَ رَبِّ
انْصُرْنِي عَلَى الْقَوْمِ الْمُفْسِدِينَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

His Threefold Virtues
---------------------

Imam Baqer has said: Lut was magnanimous and generous and whenever
guests arrived, he would warmly receive them.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قَالَ أَبُو جَعْفَرٍ عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ:
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ كَانَ لُوطُ عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ رَجُلا سَخِيًّا كَرِيمًا يُقْرِئُ
الضَّيْفَ إِذا نَزَلَ بِهِ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Migration In The Cause Of Religion
----------------------------------

Lot left Iraq for Damascus along with his uncle, Abraham. He forsook his
homeland and his people to safeguard his religion.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَقَدْ هَاجَرَ مَعَ عَمِهِ إِبْراهيمَ مِنْ أَرْضِ الْعِراقِ إِلى
بِلادِ الشّامِ تَارِكًا وَطَنَهُ وَأَهْلِه وَقَبِيلَتَهُ لِدِينِهِ
وَابْتَعَدَ عَنْ قَوْمِه وَأَهْلِه.
  </p>
</blockquote>
