26) His Supplication for his Neighbours and Friends
===================================================

> (26) وَ كَانَ مِنْ دُعَائِهِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلَامُ لِجِيرَانِهِ وَ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَوْلِيَائِهِ إِذَا ذَكَرَهُمْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

His Supplication for his Neighbours and Friends when he Mentioned them

> 1. اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تَوَلَّنِي فِي جِيرَانِي وَ مَوَالِيَّ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْعَارِفِينَ بِحَقِّنَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ الْمُنَابِذِينَ لِأَعْدَائِنَا بِأَفْضَلِ وَلَايَتِكَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 2. وَ وَفِّقْهُمْ لِإِقَامَةِ سُنَّتِكَ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ الْأَخْذِ بِمَحَاسِنِ أَدَبِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فِي إِرْفَاقِ ضَعِيفِهِمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ سَدِّ خَلَّتِهِمْ ، وَ عِيَادَةِ مَرِيضِهِمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ هِدَايَةِ مُسْتَرْشِدِهِمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مُنَاصَحَةِ مُسْتَشِيرِهِمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تَعَهُّدِ قَادِمِهِمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ كِتْمَانِ أَسْرَارِهِمْ ، وَ سَتْرِ عَوْرَاتِهِمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ نُصْرَةِ مَظْلُومِهِمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ حُسْنِ مُوَاسَاتِهِمْ بِالْمَاعُونِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ الْعَوْدِ عَلَيْهِمْ بِالْجِدَةِ وَ الْإِفْضَالِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ إِعْطَاءِ مَا يَجِبُ لَهُمْ قَبْلَ السُّؤَالِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 3. وَ اجْعَلْنِي اللَّهُمَّ أَجْزِي بِالْإِحْسَانِ مُسِيئَهُمْ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أُعْرِضُ بِالتَّجَاوُزِ عَنْ ظَالِمِهِمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَسْتَعْمِلُ حُسْنَ الظَّنِّ فِي كَافَّتِهِمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَتَوَلَّى بِالْبِرِّ عَامَّتَهُمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَغُضُّ بَصَرِي عَنْهُمْ عِفَّةً ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أُلِينُ جَانِبِي لَهُمْ تَوَاضُعاً ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَرِقُّ عَلَى أَهْلِ الْبَلَاءِ مِنْهُمْ رَحْمَةً ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أُسِرُّ لَهُمْ بِالْغَيْبِ مَوَدَّةً ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أُحِبُّ بَقَاءَ النِّعْمَةِ عِنْدَهُمْ نُصْحاً ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أُوجِبُ لَهُمْ مَا أُوجِبُ لِحَامَّتِي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَرْعَى لَهُمْ مَا أَرْعَى لِخَاصَّتِي .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 4. اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ ارْزُقْنِي مِثْلَ ذَلِكَ مِنْهُمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اجْعَلْ لِي أَوْفَى الْحُظُوظِ فِيمَا عِنْدَهُمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ زِدْهُمْ بَصِيرَةً فِي حَقِّي ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مَعْرِفَةً بِفَضْلِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
حَتَّى يَسْعَدُوا بِي وَ أَسْعَدَ بِهِمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
آمِينَ رَبَّ الْعَالَمِينَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

1- O God, bless Muhammad and his Household  
 and attend to me with Thy best attending  
 in my neighbours and friends  
 who recognize our right[^1]  
 and war against our enemies!

2- Give [my neighbours and friends] success in  
 performing Thy prescriptions  
 and taking on the beauties of Thy courtesy through  
 acting gently with their weak,  
 remedying their lacks, visiting their sick,  
 guiding their seeker of right guidance,  
 giving good counsel to their seeker of advice,  
 attending to the one among them who returns from travel,  
 hiding their secrets, covering over their shameful things,  
 helping their wronged,  
 sharing kindly with them in goods,  
 turning toward them with wealth and bestowal of bounty,  
 and giving what is due to them before they ask!

3- Let me, O God, repay their evildoer  
 with good-doing,  
 turn away from their wrongdoer with forbearance.  
 have a good opinion of every one of them,  
 attend to all of them with devotion,  
 lower my eyes before them in continence,  
 make mild my side toward them  
 in humility,  
 be tender toward the afflicted among them  
 in mercy,  
 make them happy in absence through affection,  
 love that they continue to receive favour  
 through good will,  
 grant them what I grant my next of kin,  
 and observe for them  
 what I observe for my special friends!

4- O God,  
 bless Muhammad and his Household,  
 provide me the like of that from them,  
 appoint for me the fullest share of what is with them,  
 increase them in insight toward my right  
 and knowledge of my excellence  
 so that they will be fortunate through me and I fortunate through
them!  
 Amen, Lord of the worlds!

[^1]: That is, those who recognize the Imamate of Zayn al-'Abidin and
the other Imams. Cf. 47.64.
