Surah Al-Imran, n.3
===================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Allah, (there is) no god but He, the Everliving***[^1]***, the
Self-subsisting by Whom all things subsist***[^2] ***(3:2)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِنَّ اللَّهَ لَا يَخْفَىٰ عَلَيْهِ شَيْءٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَلَا فِي
السَّمَاءِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Allah - surely nothing is hidden from Him in the earth or in the
heaven (3:5)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
هُوَ الَّذِي يُصَوِّرُكُمْ فِي الْأَرْحَامِ كَيْفَ يَشَاءُ ۚ لَا
إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْعَزِيزُ الْحَكِيمُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***He it is Who shapes you in the wombs as He likes; there is no god but
He, the Mighty, the Wise (3:6)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
شَهِدَ اللَّهُ أَنَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ وَالْمَلَائِكَةُ
وَأُولُو الْعِلْمِ قَائِمًا بِالْقِسْطِ ۚ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ
الْعَزِيزُ الْحَكِيمُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Allah bears witness that there is no god but He, and (so do) the
angels and those possessed of knowledge, maintaining His creation with
justice***[^3]***; there is no god but He, the Mighty, the Wise
(3:18)***

[^1]: Self-Living 2:255

[^2]: Sustainer of all things – or by Whom all things are sustained
2:255

[^3]: Not inclined to any extreme
