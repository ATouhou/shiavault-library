Jannatul Baquee
===============

This is the general cemetery in existence since the time of the Prophet
of Islam. Here many Ashab, Momineen and Shohada al-Ohad have been
buried. Also the graves of the following members of the Ahlul Bayt can
be found there.

Janabe Fatima Zehra A.S.
------------------------

According to some historians she is buried here.
