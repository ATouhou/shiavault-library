12.  Economic Welfare
=====================

• In the government of Imam Mahdi (a.t.f.s.), people will have generous
and bighearted rulers. Imam (a.t.f.s.) will distribute wealth to the
public in an unprecedented manner.[^1]

• Imam Mahdi (a.t.f.s.) will dole out incomputable wealth.[^2]

• Imam Mahdi (a.t.f.s.) will dispense wealth equally among the
people.[^3]

• The grants and bestowals of Imam az-Zamaan (a.t.f.s.) will be
extremely adorable.[^4]

• Economic welfare will attain such heights That none eligible for
Zakaat will be found.[^5]

• Due to economic prosperity and ideal state of safety and security,
none will indulge in hoarding.[^6]

• The kinds and types of bounties enjoyed by the people during the
government of Imam az-Zamaan (a.t.f.s.) will be unmatched.[^7]
