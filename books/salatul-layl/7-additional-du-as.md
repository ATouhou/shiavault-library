Additional  Du’as
=================

Du’a before Salatul Layl
------------------------

This is a beautiful du’a which Imam Zaynul ‘Abidin (a) used to recite
before he began Salatul Layl.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِسْمِ اللّهِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِيْمِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اِلهِي غَارَتْ نُجُوْمُ سَمَائِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ نَامَتْ عُيُوْنُ اَنَامِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ هَدَاَتْ اَصْوَاتُ عِبَادِكَ وَ اَنْعَامِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ غَلَّقَتِ الْمُلُوْكُ عَلَيْهَا اَبْوَابَهَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ طَافَ عَلَيْهَا حُرَّاسُهَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ احْتَجَبُوا عَمَّنْ يَسْئَلُهُمْ حَاجَةً
  </p>
</blockquote>

### English translation of Du’a before Salatul Layl

In the name of Allah the Beneficent, the Merciful  
 O Allah bless Muhammad and his family  
 My God, the stars of Your sky have sunk  
 and the eyes of Your people are sleeping  
 and the voices of Your servants  
 and Your animals  
 have become silent  
 and the kings have locked their doors  
 and its guards are moving around it  
 and they are covered  
 from he who wishes to ask them for a need

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَوْ يَنْتَجِعُ مِنْهُمْ فَائِدَةً
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اَنْتَ اِلهِي حَيٌّ قَيُّوْمٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لاَ تَاْخُذُكَ سِنَةٌ وَ لاَ نَوْمٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لاَ يَشْغَلُكَ شَيْئٌ عَنْ شَيْئٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَبْوَابُ سَمَائِكَ لِمَنْ دَعَاكَ مُفَتَّحَاتٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ خَزَائِنُكَ غَيْرُ مُغَلَّقَاتٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اَبْوَابُ رَحْمَتِكَ غَيْرُ مَحْجُوْبَاتٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ فَوَائِدُكَ لِمَنْ سَأَلَكَ غَيْرُ مَحْظُوْرَاتٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بَلْ هِيَ مَبْذُوْلاَتٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَنْتَ اِلهِي اَلْكَرِيْمُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

or to get a benefit from them

But You my God, are ***Ever Living, Subsistent*****,** ***slumber or
sleep does not overtake you*** **(2:255)**  
 and one thing does not occupy You from another  
 the doors of Your sky are open  
 for he who asks You  
 and Your treasures are never locked  
 and the doors of Your mercy are never covered  
 and Your benefits are never forbidden  
 for he who asks You  
 rather they are given generously  
 You are my God, the Generous

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الَّذِي لاَ تَرُدُّ سَائِلاً مِنَ الْمُؤْمِنِيْنَ سَأَلَكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لاَ تَحْتَجِبُ عَنْ اَحَدٍ مِنْهُمْ اَرَادَكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لاَ وَ عِزَّتِكَ وَ جَلاَلِكَ وَ لاَ تُخْتَزَلُ حَوَائِجَهُمْ دُوْنَكَ
وَ لاَ يَقْضِيْهَا اَحَدٌ غَيْرُكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللّهُمَّ وَ قَدْ تَرَانِي وَ وُقُوْفِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ ذُلَّ مَقَامِي بَيْنَ يَدَيْكَ وَ تَعْلَمُ سَرِيْرَتِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تَطَّلِعُ عَلى مَا فِي قَلْبِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مَا يَصْلِحُ بِهِ اَمْرُ آخِرَتِي وَ دُنْيَايَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللّهُمَّ اِنَّ ذِكْرَ الْمَوْتِ وَ اَهْوَالِ الْمُطَّلِعِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَالْوُقُوْفَ بَيْنَ يَدَيْكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

who does not turn away a beseecher  
 who asks from You  
 and You do not conceal Yourself  
 from anyone of the believers  
 who intends You  
 No, by Your Honor and Your Majesty  
 no one removes their needs  
 other than You  
 and no one fulfills their needs apart from You

O Allah You see me,  
 and my standing (before You)  
 and my humble position in front of You  
 and You know my secrets  
 and are aware of what is in my heart  
 and of what will improve my affairs  
 of the Hereafter and the world

O Allah, the remembrance of death  
 and the frightening conditions of the observer  
 and standing before You

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
نَغَّصَنِي مَطْعَمِي وَ مَشْرَبِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اَغَصَّنِي بِرِيْقِي اَقْلَقَنِي عَنْ وِسَادِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مَنَعَنِي رُقَادِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
كَيْفَ يَنَامُ مَنْ يَخَافُ مَلَكَ الْمَوْتِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فِي طَوَارِقِ اللَّيْلِ وَ طَوَارِقِ النَّهَارِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بَلْ كَيْفَ يَنَامُ الْعَاقِلُ وَ مَلَكُ الْمَوْتِ لاَ يَنَامُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لاَ بِاللَّيْلِ وَ لاَ بِالنَّهَارِ وَ يَطْلُبُ رُوْحَهُ بِالْبَيَاتِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ فِي آنَاءِ السَّاعَاتِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَسْأََلُكَ الرَّوْحَ وَ الرَّاحَةَ عِنْدَ الْمَوْتِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ الْعَفْوَ عَنِّي حِيْنَ اَلْقَاكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

makes me stay away  
 from my food and drink  
 and makes me choke  
 keeps me away from my pillow  
 and prevents me from sleeping  
 How can one sleep  
 who fears the angel of death?  
 in the calamities of the night  
 and the calamities of the day  
 rather how can an intelligent person sleep  
 while the angel of death does not sleep  
 neither at night nor in the day  
 and seeks his soul in the state of sleep  
 and in the middle of the hours  
 I beseech You to give me ease and comfort  
 at the time of death  
 and forgiveness when I meet You

Du’a for Qunut of Salatul Layl
------------------------------

It is recommended to lengthen the Qunut in the 8 raka’ats of salatul
Layl, whenever possible. The following Du’a for Qunut in Salatul Layl is
by Imam Ja’far as-Sadiq (a). It is allowed to use a book in qunut of
mustahab prayers.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِسْمِ اللّهِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِيْمِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اِلهِي كَيْفَ اَدْعُوْكَ وَ قَدْ عَصَيْتُكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ كَيْفَ لاَ اَدْعُوْكَ وَ قَدْ عَرَفْتُ حُبَّكَ فِي قَلْبِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اِنْ كُنْتُ عَاصِيًا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَدَدْتُ اِلَيْكَ يَدًا بِالذُّنُوْبِ مَمْلُوْءَةً
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ عَيْنًا بِالرَّجَاءِ مَمْدُوْدَةً
  </p>
</blockquote>

### English translation of Du’a for Qunut of Salatul Layl

In the name of Allah the Beneficent the Merciful  
 O Allah bless Muhammad and his family  
 My God, how can I beseech You  
 when I have disobeyed You  
 and how can I not beseech You  
 when I have known Your love in my heart  
 As I am a sinner  
 I have stretched my hand to You, full of sins,  
 and my eyes with stretched out hope

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَوْلاَيَ اَنْتَ عَظِيْمُ الْعُظَمَاءِ وَ اَنَا اَسِيْرُ الاُسَرَاءِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَنَا الاَسِيْرُ بِذَنْبِي اَلْمُرْتَهَنُ بِجُرْمِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اِلهِي لَئِنْ طَالَبْتَنِي بِذَنْبِي لاُطَالِبَنَّكَ بِكَرَمِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَئِنْ طَالَبْتَنِي بِجَرِيْرَتِي لاُطَالِبَنَّكَ بِعَفْوِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَئِنْ اَمَرْتَ بِي اِلىٰ النَّارِ لاُخْبِرَنَّ اَهْلَهَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَنِّي كُنْتُ اَقُوْلُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لاَ اِلهَ اِلاَّ اللّهُ مُحَمَّدٌ رَسُوْلُ اللّهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللّهُمَّ اِنَّ الطَّاعَةَ تَسُرُّكَ وَ الْمَعْصِيَةَ لاَ تَضُرُّكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَهَبْ لِي مَا يَسُرُّكَ وَ اغْفِرْ لِي مَا لاَ يَضُرُّكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَا اَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِيْنَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

My Master, You are the Greatest of the Great  
 and I am the most wretched of prisoners  
 I am a prisoner of my sins  
 a mortgagee of my crimes  
 My God if You seek me out for my sins  
 I will seek You out for Your generosity

and if You seek me for my crimes  
 I will seek You for Your pardon  
 and if You command me to the Fire  
 I will inform the people of the Fire  
 that I used to say ***there is no god but Allah (47:19)***  
***Muhammad is the Messenger of Allah (49:29)***  
 O Allah obedience pleases You  
 and disobedience does not harm You  
 so grant me what pleases You  
 and forgive me what does not harm You  
 O most Merciful

Du’a after every 2 raka’ats of Nafilah of Layl
----------------------------------------------

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِسْمِ اللّهِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِيْمِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللّهُمَّ اِنِّي اَسْئَلُكَ وَ لَمْ يُسْئَلْ مِثْلُكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَنْتَ مَوْضِعُ مَسْئَلَةِ السَّائِلِيْنَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مُنْتَهى رَغْبَةِ الرَّاغِبِيْنَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَدْعُوْكَ وَلَمْ يُدْعَ مِثْلُكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اَرْغَبُ اِلَيْكَ وَ لَمْ يُرْغَبْ اِلى مِثْلِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَنْتَ مُجِيْبُ دَعْوَةِ الْمُضْطَرِّيْنَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اَرْحَمُ الرَّاحِمِيْنَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَسْأَلُكَ بِاَفْضَلِ الْمَسَائِلِ وَ اَنْجَحِهَا وَ اَعْظَمِهَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

### English translation of Du’a after every 2 raka’ats of Nafilah of Layl

In the name of Allah  
 the Beneficent, the Merciful  
 O Allah bless Muhammad and his family

O Allah I ask from You  
 and there is none like You who is asked  
 You are the Object of the seeking of the seekers  
 and the ultimate aim of the desire of the desirers

I call to You  
 for there is none like You who is called  
 and I wish from You  
 for there is none like You who is wished from  
 You are the One who answers the call of the distressed, and the most
Merciful  
 I ask You,  
 by the best of requests  
 and the most successful of them  
 and the most mighty of them

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَا اَللّهُ يَا رَحْمٰنُ يَا رَحِيْمُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ بِاَسْمَائِكَ الْحُسْنى
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اَمْثَالِكَ الْعُلْيَا وَ نِعْمَتِكَ الَّتِي لاَ تُحْصى
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ بِاَكْرَمِ اَسْمَائِكَ وَ اَحَبِّهَا اِلَيْكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اَقْرَبِهَا مِنْكَ وَسِيْلَةً
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اَشْرَفِهَا عِنْدَكَ مَنْزِلَةً
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اَجْزَلِهَا لَدَيْكَ ثَوَابًا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اَسْرَعِهَا فِي الاُمُوْرِ اِجَابَةً
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ بِاسْمِكَ الْمَكْنُوْنِ الاَكْبَرِ الاَعَزِّ الاَجَلِّ الاَعْظَمِ
الاَكْرَمِ الَّذِي تُحِبُّهُ وَ تَهْوَاهُ وَ تَرْضَى بِهِ عَمَّنْ
دَعَاكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

O Allah O Beneficent O Merciful  
 by Your beautiful names  
 and Your elevated examples  
 and Your favors which cannot be counted  
 and by the most noble of Your names  
 and the ones You love most  
 and the ones closest to You in means  
 and the one most honored with You in status  
 and most plentiful with You in reward  
 and ones fastest in getting an answer in the matters  
 and by Your hidden name  
 the Greatest, the most Honorable  
 the most Glorious  
 the Mighty, the Generous  
 the one which You like  
 and the one You want  
 and the one that makes You pleased  
 with the one who calls You

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَاسْتَجَبْتَ لَهُ دُعَائَهُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ حَقٌّ عَلَيْكَ اَنْ لاَ تَحْرِمَ سَائِلَكَ وَ لاَ تَرُدَّهُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ بِكُلِّ اسْمٍ هُوَ لَكَ فِي التَّوْرَاتِ وَ الاِنْجِيْلِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ الزَّبُوْرِ وَ الْفُرْقَانِ الْعَظِيْمِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ بِكُلِّ اسْمٍ دَعَاكَ بِهِ حَمَلَةُ عَرْشِكَ وَ مَلاَئِكَتُكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اَنْبِيَاؤُكَ وَ رُسُلُكَ وَ اَهْلُ طَاعَتِكَ مِنْ خَلْقِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَنْ تُصَلِّيَ عَلى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اَنْ تُعَجِّلَ فَرَجَ وَلِيِّكَ وَ تُعَجِّلَ خِزْيَ اَعْدَائِهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اَنْ تَفْعَلَ بِي . . . (حَاجَات)
  </p>
</blockquote>

so You answer his prayer  
 and it is right that You do not deny  
 the one who asks from you  
 and do not reject him  
 and by every name that is with You  
 in Tawrat and Injil  
 and Zabur  
 and the Noble Qur’an  
 and by every name that  
 the carriers of Your throne  
 and the angels  
 and Your Prophets  
 and Your Messengers  
 and the obedient ones from Your creation  
 have called You with  
 (I ask) that You bless Muhammad and his family  
 and that You hasten the appearance of your vicegerent (Wali)  
 and that You hasten the humiliation of his enemies
