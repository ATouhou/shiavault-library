The Whispered Prayer of the Fearful
===================================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم
  </p>
</blockquote>

*In the Name of ALLAH, the All-compassionate the All-merciful,*

 BIS-MIL-LAAHIR-RAH'-MAANIR-RAH'EEM

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللّهُمّ صَلّ عَلَى مُحَمّدٍ وَآلِ مُحَمّد
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Allah, send blessings on Muhammad and on the family of Muhammad,*

ALLAHUMMA S'ALE A'LAA MUHAMMAD WA AALE MUHAMMAD

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إلهِي أَتَراكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God, should I believe that you will punish me*

ILAAHEEE ATURAAK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بَعْدَ الإِيْمانِ بِكَ تُعَذِّبُنِي،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*After you see my faith in you?,*

BAA'-DAL-EEMAANI BIKA TUA'D'-D'IBUNEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَمْ بَعْدَ حُبِّي إيَّاكَ تُبَعِّدُنِي،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Or that you will drive me far away from you, after you*

*see my love for you?,*

AM BAA'-DA H'UB-BEEE EE-YAAKA TUBAA'I'DUNEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَمْ مَعَ رَجآئِي برحمَتِكَ وَصَفْحِكَ تَحْرِمُنِي،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Or that you will deprive me from yourself, while I have hope for your
mercy and forgiveness?*

AM MAA' RAJAAA-EE LIRAH'-MATIKA WA S'AF-H'IKA TAH'-RIMUNEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَمْ مَعَ اسْتِجارَتِي بِعَفْوِكَ تُسْلِمُنِي؟
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Or that you will abandon me while I seek sanctuary in your pardon?*

AM MAA' AS-TIJAARATEE BIA'F-WIKA TUSLIMUNEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
حاشا لِوَجْهِكَ الْكَرِيمِ أَنْ تُخَيِّبَنِي،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Never! It is impossible !for you are too generous to disappoint me!*

H'AASHAA LIWAJ-HIKAL-KAREEMI AN TUKHAY-IBANEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لَيْتَ شِعْرِي، أَلِلشَّقآءِ وَلَدَتْنِي أُمِّي،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*If I knew That my mother would give birth to me so that I would end up
in such misery!*

LAY-TA SHIA'-REE

ALILSH-SHAQAAA-I WALADAT-NEEE UM-MEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَمْ لِلْعَنآءِ رَبَّتْنِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Or she would raise me up for me to bear suffering!*

AM LIL-A'NAAA-I RAB-BAT-NEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَلَيْتَهَا لَمْ تَلِدْنِي وَلَمْ تُرَبِّنِي،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Then I wish that she had not given me birth or had not nurtured me!*

FALAY-TAHAA LAM TALID-NEE WA LAM TURAB-BINEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَمِنْ أَهْلِ السَّعادَةِ جَعَلْتَنِي؟ وَلَيْتَنِي عَلِمْتُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Or on the other hand, if I knew That you had appointed as me one of the
people to receive eternal happiness?*

AMIN AH-LIS-SAA'DATI JAA'L-TANEE WA LAY-TANEE A'LIM-TU

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَبِقُرْبِكَ وَجَوارِكَ خَصَصْتَنِي؟
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Or that you had chosen me to be one of those close to you ?*

WA BIQUR-BIKA WA JIWAARIKA KHAS'AS'-TANEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَتَقَرَّ بِذلِكَ عَيْنِي، وَتَطْمَئِنَّ لَهُ نَفْسِي.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*If I knew that , then my eyes would be filled with joy, and my soul
would reach tranquility.*

FATAQAR-RA BID'ALIKA A'Y-NEE WA TAT'-MA-IN-NA LAHOO NAF-SEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إلهِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God,*

ILAAHEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
هَلْ تُسَوِّدُ وُجُوهً خَرَّتْ ساجِدَةً لِعَظَمَتِكَ؟
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Are you going to take away the light from the faces that prostrated to
you in front of your might?*

HAL TUSAW-WIDU WUJOOHANA KHAR-RAT SAAJIDATAL-LIA'Z'AMATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَوْ تُخْرِسُ أَلْسِنَةً نَطَقَتْ بِالثَّنآءِ عَلَى مَجْدِكَ
وَجَلالَتِكَ؟
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Or will you silence those tongues which spoke in praise of your glory
and majesty?*

AW TUKH-RISU AL-SINATAN-NAT'AQAT BITH-THANAAA-I A'LAA MAJ-DIKA WA
JALAALATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَوْ تَطْبَعُ عَلَى قُلُوب انْطَوَتْ عَلى مَحَبَّتِكَ؟
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Or will you seal those hearts which contain love for you?*

AW TAT'-BAU' A'LAA QULOOBIN ANT'AWAT A'LAA MAH'AB-BATIKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَوْ تُصِمُّ أَسْماعَاً تَلَذَّذَتْ بِسَماعِ ذِكْرِكَ فِي إرادَتِكَ؟
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Or will you seal the ears which took pleasure in hearing the
recitations of your remembrance*

*according to your will?*

AW TUS'IM-MU AS-MAAA'NA TALAD'-D'AD'AT BISAMAAI' D'IK-RIKA FEEE
IRAADATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَوْ تَغُلُّ أَكُفَّاً رَفَعَتْهَا الآمالُ إلَيْكَ رَجآءَ رَأْفَتِكَ؟
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Or will you chain the hands which were raised up in prayer, expecting
hope in your clemency?*

AW TAGHUL-LU AKUF-FAR-RAFAA'T-HAAL-AAAMAALU ILAY-KA RAJAAA-A RAA-FATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَوْ تُعاقِبُ أَبْداناً عَمِلَتْ بِطاعَتِكَ حَتَّى نَحِلَتْ فِي
مُجاهَدَتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Or will you punish those bodies that worked to obey you until it grew
thin in struggling for you?*

AW TUA'AQIBU AB-DAANANA A'MILAT BIT'AAA'TIKA H'AT-TAA NAH'ILAT FEE
MUJAAHADATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَوْ تُعَذِّبُ أَرْجُلاً سَعَتْ فِي عِبادَتِكَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Or will you punish the legs which ran to worship you?*

AW TUA'D'-D'IBU AR-JULAN SAA'T FEE I'BAADATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إلهِي لا تُغْلِقْ عَلى مُوَحِّدِيكَ أَبْوابَ رَحْمَتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God, do not close the gates of mercy to those who believe in you and
your oneness*

ILAAHEE LAA TUGH-LIQ A'LAA MUWAH'-H'IDEEKA AB-WAABA RAH'-MATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَلا تَحْجُبْ مُشْتاقِيكَ عَنِ النَّظَرِ إلَى جَمِيلِ رُؤْيَتِكَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and veil not those who desire you and looking upon the vision of your
beauty!*

WA LAA TH'-JUB MUSH-TAAQEEKA A'NIN-NAZ'ARI ILAA JAMEELI ROO-YATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إلهِي نَفْسٌ أَعْزَزْتَها بِتَوْحِيدِكَ، كَيْفَ تُذِلُّها بِمَهانَةِ
هِجْرانِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God, a soul which you have honored, by it’s being able to recognize
your oneness—then will you humiliate this soul by letting it go far from
you?*

ILAAHEE NAF-SUN AA'-ZAZ-TAHAA BITAWH'EEDIKA KAY-FA TUD'IL-LUHAA
BIMAHAANATI HIJ-RAANIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَضَمِيرٌ انْعَقَدَ عَلى مَوَدَّتِكَ كَيْفَ تُحْرِقُهُ بِحَرَارَةِ
نِيرانِكَ؟
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God, will you burn, in the heat of your fires, a heart which has
been woven in love for you,?*

WA Z''AMEERUN-IN-A'QADA A'LAA MAWAD-DATIKA KAY-FA TUH'-RIQUHOO
BIH'ARAARATI NEERAANIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَجِرْنِي مِنْ أَلِيمِ غَضَبِكَ وَعَظِيمِ سَخَطِكَ، إلهِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God ,protect me from your painful wrath and mighty anger!*

ILAAHEE AJIR-NEE MIN ALEEMI GHAZ''ABIKA WA A'Z'EEMI SAKHAT'IKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يا حَنَّانُ يا مَنَّانُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O! All-loving, O All-kind!*

YAA H'AN-NAANU YAA MAN-NAANU

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يا رَحِيمُ يا رَحْمنُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O!* *Compassionate, O Merciful!*

YAA RAH'EEMU YAA RAH'-MAAN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يا جَبَّارُ يا قَهَّارُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O powerful Compeller, O Subduer!*

YAA JAB-BAARU YAA QAH-HAARU

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يا غَفَّارُ يا سَتَّارُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O All-forgiver, O All-coverer (of sins)!*

YAA GHAF-FAARU YAA SAT-TAARU

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
نَجِّنِي بِرَحْمَتِكَ مِنْ عَذابِ النَّارِ، وَفَضِيحَةِ الْعارِ، إذَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Save me, through your, mercy from the punishment of the Fire and the
disgrace of shame (day of judgment) when*

NAJ-JINEE BIRAH'-MATIKA MIN A'D'AABIN-NAARI WA FAZ''EEH'ATIL-A'ARI ID'AA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
امْتازَ الأَخْيارُ مِنَ الأَشْرارِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*the good are set apart from the evil,*

AM-TAAZAL-AKH-YAARU MINAL-ASH-RAAR

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَحالَتِ الأَحْوالُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And the Conditions are changed,*

WA H'AALATIL-AH'-WAAL

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَهالَتِ الأَهْوالُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And the fears that terrify,*

WA HAALATIL-AH-WAAL

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَقَرُبَ الْمُحْسِنُونَ، وَبَعُدَ الْمُسِيؤُنَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*The good-doers are brought near and the evildoers taken far*

WA QARUBAL-MUH'-SINOON WA BAU'DAL-MUSEEON

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَوُفِّيَتْ كُلُّ نَفْس ما كَسَبَتْ وَهُمْ لا يُظْلَمُونَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and every soul paid what it has earned, and they shall not be wronged!*

 WA WUF-FEEAT KUL-LU NAF-SIM-MAA KASABAT WA HUM LAA YUZ'-LAMOON
