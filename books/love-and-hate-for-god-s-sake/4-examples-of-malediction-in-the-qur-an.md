Examples of Malediction in the Qur’an
=====================================

By referring to the verses of the Noble Qur’an, we can deduce that God,
the Most High, has expressed [His] malediction upon certain individuals
and also upon certain groups of people – the following are some
examples:

1. *Satan:*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ إِنَّ عَلَيْكَ لَعْنَتِي إِليٰ يَوْمِ الدِّينِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***“…and indeed*** ***My*** ***malediction*** ***will be on you until
the Day of Retribution.”***[^1]

2. *Belligerent disbelievers:*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِنَّ اللهَ لَعَنَ الْكَافِرينَ وَأَعَدَّ لَهُمْ سَعِيراً
  </p>
</blockquote>

***“Indeed God has*** ***applied malediction*** ***upon*** ***the
faithless and prepared for them a blaze…”***[^2]

3. *Certain individuals from amongst the Tribes of Israel [Offspring of
Prophet Jacob] –* those key individuals who did not believe in the
prophets which God sent to them and remained as non-believers:

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لُعِنَ الَّذينَ كَفَرُوا مِنْ بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ عَلىٰ لِسَانِ
دَاوُدَ وَعِيسَىٰ بْنِ مَرْيَمَ ذٰلِكَ بِمَا عَسَيْتُمْ وَ كَانُوا
يَعْتَدُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***“The faithless among the Children of Israel*** ***were maledicted***
***on the tongue of David and Jesus, son of Mary. That is because they
would disobey [the commandments] and used to commit
transgression.”***[^3]

4. *Oppressors:*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَلاَ لَعْنَةُ اللهِ عَلىٰ الظّالِـمِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***“Look! The*** ***curse*** ***(removal of the mercy) of God is upon
the wrongdoers”***[^4]

5. *Liars:*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَالْـخَامِسَةُ أَنَّ لَعْنَةَ اللهِ عَلَيهِ إِنْ كَانَ مِنَ
الْكَاذِبِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***“…and a fifth [oath] that God’s*** ***malediction*** ***shall be upon
him if he were lying.”***[^5]

6. *Those who hurt Prophet Muhammad* (S):

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ يُؤْذُونَ اللهَ وَ رَسُولَهُ لَعَنَهُمُ اللهُ فِي
الدُّنْيَا وَ الآخِرَةِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***“Indeed those who torment God and His Apostle are*** ***cursed***
***(removed from mercy) by God in the world and in the
Hereafter…”***[^6]

7. *Those who falsely accuse women of illicit sexual improprieties:*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ يَرْمُونَ الْمُحْصَنَاتِ الْغَافِلاَتِ الْمُؤْمِنَاتِ
لُعِنُوا فِي الدُّنْيَا وَ الآخِرَةِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***“Indeed those who accuse honorable and unwary faithful women shall
be*** ***cursed*** ***in this world and the Hereafter…”***[^7]

8. *Those who kill innocent believers:*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمَنْ يَقْتُلْ مُؤمِناً مُتَعَمِّداً فَجَزَاءُهُ جَهَنَّمُ خَالِداً
فِيهَا وَ غَضِبَ اللهُ عَلَيْهِ وَ لَعَنَهُ وَ أَعَدَّ لَهُ عَذَابَاً
عَظِيماً
  </p>
</blockquote>

***“Should anyone kill a believer intentionally, his requital shall be
hell, to remain in it [forever]; God shall be wrathful to him and***
***maledict against him*** ***and He will prepare for him a great
punishment.”***[^8]

9. *Hypocrites:*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَعَدَ اللهُ الْمُنَافِقِينَ وَ الْمُنَافِقَاتِ وَ الْکُفَّارَ نَارَ
جَهَنَّمَ خَالِدِينَ فِيهَا هِيَ حَسْبُهُمْ وَلَعَنَهُمُ اللهُ
وَلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ مُقِيمٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***“God has promised the hypocrites, men and women, and the faithless -
the Fire of Hell, to remain in it [forever]. That suffices them. God has
evoked*** ***malediction upon them, and there is a lasting punishment
for them.”***[^9]

10. *Those who spread corruption and also sever their family ties:*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَهَلْ عَسَيْتُمْ إِنْ تَوَلَّيْتُمْ أَنْ تُفْسِدُوا فِي الأَرْضِ وَ
تُقَطِّعُوا أَرْحَامَکُمْ أُوْلٰئِكَ الَّذِينَ لَعَنَهُمُ اللهُ
فَأَصَمَّهُمْ وَ أَعْمَىٰ أَبْصَارَهُمْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***“May it not be that if you were to wield authority you would cause
corruption in the land and ill-treat your blood relations? They are the
ones upon whom God has evoked*** ***malediction*** ***upon,*** ***so He
made them deaf, and blinded their sight.”***[^10]

[^1]: Al-Qurʾan, Surat Swad (38), verse 78

[^2]: Al-Qurʾan, Suratul Ahzab (33), verse 64

[^3]: Al-Qurʾan, Suratul Ma’idah (5), verse 78

[^4]: Al-Qurʾan, Surat Hud (11), verse 18

[^5]: Al-Qurʾan, Suratul Nur (24), verse 7

[^6]: Al-Qurʾan, Suratul Ahzab (33), verse 57

[^7]: Al-Qurʾan, Suratul Nur (24), verse 23

[^8]: Al-Qurʾan, Suratul Nisaʾ (4), verse 93

[^9]: Al-Qurʾan, Suratul Tawbah (9), verse 68

[^10]: Al-Qurʾan, Surat Muhammad (47), verse 22-23
