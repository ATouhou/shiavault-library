Acts of Umrah al-Mufradah
=========================

**Article 22-** Acts of Umrah al-Mufradah are seven acts:

1- Ihram from Miqat.

2- Tawaf of the House of Allah (seven rounds).

3- Prayer of Tawaf.

4- Sa’y of Safa and Marwah

5- Taqsir, which Means to Shorten the Hair and the Nail.

6- Tawaf of Nisa’ (women).

7- Prayer of Tawaf of Nisa’ (women).
