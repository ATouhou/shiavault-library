The Whispered Prayer of those Asking for Mediation
==================================================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم
  </p>
</blockquote>

*In the Name of ALLAH, the All-compassionate*

*the All-merciful,* BIS-MIL-LAAHIR-RAH'-MAANIR-RAH'EEM

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللّهُمّ صَلّ عَلَى مُحَمّدٍ وَآلِ مُحَمّد
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Allah, send blessings on Muhammad and on the family of Muhammad,*
ALLAHUMMA S'ALE A'LAA MUHAMMAD WA AALE MUHAMMAD

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الإلهِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God,*

ILAAHEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لَيْسَ لِي وَسِيلَةٌ إلَيْكَ إلاَّ عَواطِفُ رَأفَتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*I have no other assets with you but the tender acts of your
forgiveness,*

LAY-SA LEE WASEELATUN ILAY-KA IL-LAA A'WAAT'IFU RAA-FATIKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَلا لِي ذَرِيعَةٌ إلَيْكَ إلاَّ عَوارِفُ رَحْمَتِكَ، وَشَفاعَةُ
نَبِيِّكَ نَبِيِّ الرَّحْمَةِ، وَمُنْقِذِ الأُمَّةِ مِنَ الْغُمَّةِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*nor any other way to come to you, but with the gentle favors of your
mercy and the intercession of your Prophet, the prophet of mercy, who
rescued the community from confusion.*

WA LAA LEE D'AREEA'TUN ILAY-KA IL-LAA A'WAARIFU RAH'-MATIKA WA
SHAFAAA'TU NABEE-YIKA NABEE-YIR-RAH'-MATI WA MUNQID'IL-UM-MATI
MINAL-GHUM-MAH

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَاجْعَلْهُما لِي سَبَباً إلى نَيْلِ غُفْرانِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*So make these two my connection to attaining your forgiveness*

FAJ-A'L-HUMAA LEE SABABAN ILAA NAY-LI GHUF-RAANIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَصَيِّرْهُمَا لِي وُصْلَةً إلَى الْفَوْزِ بِرِضْوانِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and let them take me to success through your good pleasure!*

WA S'AY-YIR-HUMAA LEE WUS'-LATAN ILAAL-FAW-ZI BIRIZ''-WAANIKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَقَدْ حَلَّ رَجآئِي بِحَرَمِ كَرَمِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My hope has entered in the sacred grounds of your Generosity,*

WA QAD H'AL-LA RAJAAA-EE BIH'ARAMI KARAMIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَحَطَّ طَمَعِي بِفِنآءِ جُودِكَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*my quest for forgiveness, has landed in the courtyard of your
bounties.*

WA H'AT'-T'A T'AMAE'E BIFINAAA-I JOODIKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَحَقِّقْ فِيكَ أَمَلِيْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*So make real my expectations from you,*

FAH'AQ-QIQ FEEKA AMALEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَاخْتِمْ بِالْخَيْرِ عَمَلِي،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*seal my works with good,*

WA AKH-TIM BIL-KHAY-RI A'MALEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَاجْعَلْنِي مِنْ صَفْوَتِكَ الَّذِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and place me amongst your selected friends, those whom you have*

WAJ-A'L-NEE MIN S'AF-WATIKAL-LAD'EENA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَحْلَلْتَهُمْ بُحْبُوحَةَ جَنَّتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*settled in the midst of your Garden,*

AH'-LAL-TAHUM BUH'-BOOH'ATA JAN-NATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَبَوَّأْتَهُمْ دارَ كَرامَتِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And those you have settled in the house of your generosity,*

WA BAW-WAA-TAHUM DAARA KARAAMATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَقْرَرْتَ أَعْيُنَهُمْ بِالنَّظَرِ إلَيْكَ يَوْمَ لِقآئِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And those ,whose eyes you have gladdened by giving them the opportunity
of gazing upon you on the day of meeting,*

WA AQ-RAR-TA AA'-YUNAHUM BIN-NAZ'ARI ILAY-KA YAW-MA LIQAAA-IKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَوْرَثْتَهُمْ مَنازِلَ الصِّدْقِ فِي جِوارِكَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and those, whom you have made heirs to the stations of honesty in your
neighborhood!*

WA AW-RATH-TAHUM MANAAZILAS'-S'ID-QI FEE JAWAARIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يا مَنْ لا يَفِدُ الْوافِدُونَ عَلى أَكْرَمَ مِنْهُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O He, who none is more generous than the one who is reached by those
who reach out*

YAA MAN LAA YAFIDUL-WAAFIDOONA A'LAAA AK-RAMA MIN-HOO

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَلا يَجِدُ الْقاصِدُونَ أَرْحَمَ مِنْهُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and none is more merciful than the one who is found by the ones who are
seeking*

WA LAA YAJIDUL-QAAS'IDOONA AR-H'AMA MIN-H

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يا خَيْرَ مَنْ خَلا بِهِ وَحِيدٌ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O you, who are the best of those with whom the lonely are alone with*

YAA KHAY-RA MAN KHALAA BIHEE WAH'EED

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا أَعْطَفَ مَنْ أَوى إلَيْهِ طَرِيدٌ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O you, who are the most gentle of those with whom the outcasts seek
shelter!*

WA YAAA AA'-T'AFA MAN AAAWAAA ILAY-HEE T'AREEDUN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إلى سَعَةِ عَفْوِكَ مَدَدْتُ يَدِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Towards the vastness of your pardon i extend my hand,*

ILAA SAA'TI A'F-WIKA MADAT-TU YADEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَبِذَيْلِ كَرَمِكَ أَعْلَقْتُ كَفِّي،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*upon the skirt of your generosity I tighten my grasp!*

WA BID'AY-LI KARAMIKA AA'-LAQ-TU KAF-FEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَلا تُولِنِي الْحِرْمانَ، وَلا تُبْلِنِي بِالْخَيْبَةِ وَالْخُسْرانِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Do not deprive me and do not afflict me with disappointment and loss!*

FALA TOOLINEEL-H'IR-MAAN WA LAA TUB-LINEE BIL-KHAY-BATI WAL-KHUS-RAAN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يا سَمِيعَ الدُّعآءِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O the one who listens to Duas!*

YAA SAMEEA'D-DUA'AA-I

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يا أَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Most Merciful of the merciful!*

YAAA AR-H'AMAR-RAAH'IMEEN
