16) The pleasure of Fatimah (sa) is the pleasure of  Allah (SWT)
================================================================

> 43. عن علي رضي الله عنه، قال: قال رسول الله صلى الله عليه وسلم لفاطمة:
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إن الله يغضب لغضبك، و يرضى لرضاك.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Ali (as) narrates that the Holy Prophet (saw) said to Fatimah (sa),
“Indeed Allah becomes angry when you are angry and He is pleased when
you are pleased.”
