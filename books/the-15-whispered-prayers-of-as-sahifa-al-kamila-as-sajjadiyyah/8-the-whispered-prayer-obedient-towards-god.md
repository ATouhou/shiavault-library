The Whispered Prayer obedient towards God
=========================================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم
  </p>
</blockquote>

*In the Name of ALLAH, the All-compassionate*

*the All-merciful,*

 BIS-MIL-LAAHIR-RAH'-MAANIR-RAH'EEM

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللّهُمّ صَلّ عَلَى مُحَمّدٍ وَآلِ مُحَمّد
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Allah, send blessings on Muhammad and on the family of Muhammad,*
ALLAHUMMA S'ALE A'LAA MUHAMMAD WA AALE MUHAMMAD

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللَّهُمَّ أَلْهِمْنا طاعَتَكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O! God lead us to the best ways to obey you*

AL-LAHUM-MA AL-HIM-NAA T’AAA’TAKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَجَنّـِبْنا مَعْصِيَتَكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*turn us away from the path of disobedience to you*

WA JANNIB NAA MAA SEEATAK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَّـِرْ لَنيَسا بُلُوغَ ما نَتَمَنَّىٰ مِنِ ابْتِغاءِ رِضْوانِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*make it easy for us to reach what we are seeking , which is your good
pleasure*

WA YASIR LANA BULOOGHA MAA NATAMANNA MINI ABTIGHAAI RIZWANIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَحْلِلْنا بُحْبُوحَةَ جِنانِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Enable us to settle in the center of your Gardens,*

WA AH’LIL-NAA BUH-BOOH’ATAJINANIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَاقْشَعْ عَنْ بَصائِرِنا سَحابَ الإِرْتِيابِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And disperse from our minds, the clouds of uncertainty,*

WA AQSHAA AM BASAA-IRINA SAHAAB ALIRTEEAB

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَاكْشِفْ عَنْ قُلُوبِنا أَغْشِيَةَ الْمِرْيَةِ وَالْحِجابِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*uncover from our hearts the curtains of doubt,*

WA AK-SHIF AN QULOOBINA AGHSHEATAL MIR YATI WAL HIJAAB

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَزْهِقِ الْباطِلَ عَنْ ضَمائِرِنا،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And make falsehood vanish from our innermost minds and hearts*

WA AZ-HIQIL- BAATILA AN ZAMAAIRINAA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَثْبِتِ الْحَقَّ فِي سَرائِرِنا،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and establish the truth in our secret thoughts,*

WA ATHBITIL HAQQA FEE SARAAIRINA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَإنَّ الشُّكُوكَ وَالظُّنُونَ لَواقِحُ الْفِتَنِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*for doubts and opinions lead to temptations*

FA IN-NASH SHUKOOKA WAZ ZUNOONA LAWAAQIHUL FITAN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمُكَدّ ِرَةٌ لِصَفْوِ الْمَنائِحِ وَالْمِنَنِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And corrupt the purity of gifts and blessings!*

WA MUKAD-DIRATUL-LIS’AF—WIL-MANAAA-IHEE WAL-MINAN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَللَّهُمَّ احْمِلْنا فِي سُفُنِ نَجاتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O! God, carry us in the ships of Your deliverance,*

AL-LAHUM-MA AH’-MIL-NAA FEE SUFUNI NAJAATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمَتّـِعْنا بِلَذِيْذِ مُناجاتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And give us the pleasure where we can enjoy reciting a whispered prayer
to You,*

WA MAT-TIA’-NAA BILAD’EED’I MUNAJAATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَوْرِدْنا حِياضَ حُبّـِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*enable us to enter into the pools of Your love,*

WA AW-RID-NAA H’EEAAZ”A H”UB-BIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَذِقْنا حَلاوَةَ وُدّ ِكَ وَقُرْبِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Let us taste the sweetness of Your love and of being near to you,*

WA AD’IQNAA H’ALAWATA WUD-DIKA WA QURBIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَاجْعَلْ جِهادَنا فِيكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Allow us to struggle in Your love*

WAJ-A’L JIHAADANA FEEK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَهَمَّنا فِي طاعَتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*preoccupy us with obeying you*

WA HAM-MANAA FEE TAA’TIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَخْلِصْ نِيَّاتِنا فِي مُعامَلَتِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And purify our intentions when we are devoting works to You,*

WA AKH-LIS’ NEE-YAATINAA FEE MUA’AMALATIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَإِنَّا بِكَ وَلَكَ وَلا وَسِيلَةَ لَنا إلَيْكَ إلاَّ أَنْتَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*for we exist because of You and we have no one to intercede for us with
You but only you*

 FA IN-NAA BIKA WA LAKWA LAA WASYLATA LANAAA ILAY-KA IL-LAAA ANT

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِلـٰهِي اجْعَلْنِي مِنَ الْمُصْطَفَيْنَ الأَخْيارِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God, place me among the chosen ones, the good,*

ILAAHEE AJ-A’L-NEE MINAL- MUS’-T’AFAY-NAL-AKH-YAAR

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَلْحِقْنِي بِالصَّالِحِينَ الأَبْرارِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Join me to the righteous, the pious,*

WA AL-H’IQ-NEE BIS’- S’AALIH’EENAL-AB-RAAR

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
السَّابِقِينَ إلَىٰ الْمَكْرُماتِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*the first to reach your generous gifts*

AS-SAABIQEENA ILAA ALMAK-RUMAATI

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْمُسارِعِينَ إلَىٰ الْخَيْراتِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*the quick to achieve good acts*

ALMUSAARIE’ENA ILAAY-KHAY-RATI

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْعامِلِينَ لِلْباقِياتِ الصَّالِحاتِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*the workers of righteous deeds whose rewards never end*

AL-A’AMILEEN LIL-BAAQEEAATIS’-S’AALIH’AATI

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
السَّاعِينَ إلىٰ رَفِيعِ الدَّرَجاتِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*the strivers striving to reach the highest degrees!,*

AS-SAAE’ENA ILA RAFEEI’D-DARAJAATI

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إنَّكَ عَلىٰ كُلّ ِ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Indeed you are powerful over everything*

IN-NAKA A’LAA KUL-LI SHAY-IN QADEER

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَبِالإجابَةِ جَدِيرٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and ever willing to respond!*

WA BIL-IJAABATI JADEER 

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِرَحْمَتِكَ يا أَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*By Your mercy, O Most Merciful of the merciful!*

BIRAH’-MATIKA YAAA AR-H’AMAR-RAAH’IMEEN
