43) His Supplication when he Looked at the New Crescent Moon
============================================================

> (43) وَ كَانَ مِنْ دُعَائِهِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلَامُ إِذَا نَظَرَ إِلَى
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْهِلَالِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 1. أَيُّهَا الْخَلْقُ الْمُطِيعُ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الدَّائِبُ السَّرِيعُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْمُتَرَدِّدُ فِي مَنَازِلِ التَّقْدِيرِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْمُتَصَرِّفُ فِي فَلَكِ التَّدْبِيرِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 2. آمَنْتُ بِمَنْ نَوَّرَ بِكَ الظُّلَمَ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَوْضَحَ بِكَ الْبُهَمَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ جَعَلَكَ آيَةً مِنْ آيَاتِ مُلْكِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ عَلَامَةً مِنْ عَلَامَاتِ سُلْطَانِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ امْتَهَنَكَ بِالزِّيَادَةِ وَ النُّقْصَانِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ الطُّلُوعِ وَ الْأُفُولِ ، وَ الْإِنَارَةِ وَ الْكُسُوفِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فِي كُلِّ ذَلِكَ أَنْتَ لَهُ مُطِيعٌ ، وَ إِلَى إِرَادَتِهِ سَرِيعٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 3. سُبْحَانَهُ مَا أَعْجَبَ مَا دَبَّرَ فِي أَمْرِكَ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَلْطَفَ مَا صَنَعَ فِي شَأْنِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
جَعَلَكَ مِفْتَاحَ شَهْرٍ حَادِثٍ لِأَمْرٍ حَادِثٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 4. فَأَسْأَلُ اللَّهَ رَبِّي وَ رَبَّكَ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ خَالِقِي وَ خَالِقَكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مُقَدِّرِي وَ مُقَدِّرَكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مُصَوِّرِي وَ مُصَوِّرَكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَنْ يُصَلِّيَ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَنْ يَجْعَلَكَ هِلَالَ بَرَكَةٍ لَا تَمْحَقُهَا الْأَيَّامُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ طَهَارَةٍ لَا تُدَنِّسُهَا الْآثَامُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 5. هِلَالَ أَمْنٍ مِنَ الْآفَاتِ ، وَ سَلَامَةٍ مِنَ السَّيِّئَاتِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
هِلَالَ سَعْدٍ لَا نَحْسَ فِيهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يُمْنٍ لَا نَكَدَ مَعَهُ ، وَ يُسْرٍ لَا يُمَازِجُهُ عُسْرٌ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ خَيْرٍ لَا يَشُوبُهُ شَرٌّ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
هِلَالَ أَمْنٍ وَ إِيمَانٍ وَ نِعْمَةٍ وَ إِحْسَانٍ وَ سَلَامَةٍ وَ
إِسْلَامٍ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 6. اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اجْعَلْنَا مِنْ أَرْضَى مَنْ طَلَعَ عَلَيْهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَزْكَى مَنْ نَظَرَ إِلَيْهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَسْعَدَ مَنْ تَعَبَّدَ لَكَ فِيهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ وَفِّقْنَا فِيهِ لِلتَّوْبَةِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اعْصِمْنَا فِيهِ مِنَ الْحَوْبَةِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ احْفَظْنَا فِيهِ مِنْ مُبَاشَرَةِ مَعْصِيَتِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 7. وَ أَوْزِعْنَا فِيهِ شُكْرَ نِعْمَتِكَ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَلْبِسْنَا فِيهِ جُنَنَ الْعَافِيَةِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَتْمِمْ عَلَيْنَا بِاسْتِكْمَالِ طَاعَتِكَ فِيهِ الْمِنَّةَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِنَّكَ الْمَنَّانُ الْحَمِيدُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ صَلَّى اللَّهُ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ الطَّيِّبِينَ الطَّاهِرِينَ
.
  </p>
</blockquote>

1- O obedient creature,  
 speedy and untiring,  
 frequenter of the mansions of determination,[^1]  
 moving about in the sphere of governance!

2- I have faith in Him who lights up darknesses through thee,  
 illuminates jet-black shadows by thee,  
 appointed thee one of the signs of His kingdom  
 and one of the marks of His authority,  
 and humbled thee through increase and decrease,  
 rising and setting, illumination and eclipse.  
 In all of this thou art obedient to Him,  
 prompt toward His will.

3- Glory be to Him!  
 How wonderful is what He has arranged in thy situation!  
 How subtle what He has made for thy task!  
 He has made thee the key to a new month  
 for a new situation.

4- So I ask God, my Lord and thy Lord,  
 my Creator and thy Creator,  
 my Determiner and thy Determiner,  
 my Form-giver and thy Form-giver,  
 that He bless Muhammad and his Household  
 and appoint thee a crescent of blessings not effaced by days  
 and of purity not defiled by sins;

5- a crescent of security from blights and of safety from evil deeds;  
 a crescent of auspiciousness containing no misfortune,  
 of prosperity accompanied by no adversity, of ease not mixed with
difficulty,  
 of good unstained by evil;  
 a crescent of security and faith,  
 favour and good-doing, safety and submission!

6- O God,  
 bless Muhammad and his Household,  
 place us among the most satisfied of those over whom the crescent has
risen,  
 the purest of those who have looked upon it,  
 the most fortunate of those who have worshipped Thee under it;  
 give us the success during [the new month] to repent,  
 preserve us within it from misdeeds,  
 guard us therein from pursuing disobedience to Thee,

7- allot to us within it thanksgiving for Thy favour,  
 clothe us during it in the shields of well-being,  
 and complete for us Thy kindness  
 by perfecting therein obedience to Thee!  
 Surely Thou art All-kind, Praiseworthy.  
 And bless Muhammad and his Household, the good, the pure.

[^1]: Allusion to 36:39: And the moon - We have determined it by
mansions, till it returns like an aged palm-bough.
