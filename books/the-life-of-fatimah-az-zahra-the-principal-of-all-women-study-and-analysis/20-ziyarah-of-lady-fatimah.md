Ziyarah of Lady Fatimah
=======================

*Excerpted from the English version of Mafatih al-Jinan*

While standing in the area between the Holy Prophet’s tomb and minbar
(i.e. Garden of Paradise- Rawdhah), you may visit the tomb of Lady
Fatimah al-Zahra’ (s.a.) though there is disagreement about the place of
her tomb. Some say that she was buried in the Rawdhah.

Others say that she was buried at her house. Others say that she was
buried in the Baqi‘ Cemetery. However, the majority of our scholars
agree that she must be visited from the Rawdhah, although to visit her
at these three places is more preferable.

When you stand for the ziyarah of Lady Fatimah al-Zahra’s tomb, you may
say the following:

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يا مُمْتَحَنَةُ امْتَحَنَكِ اللهُ الَّذي خَلَقَكِ قَبْلَ اَنْ
يَخْلُقَكِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Ya mumtahana imtahanaki Allahu alladhi khalaqaki qabla an
yakhluqaki,*
O the carefully examined one: Allah Who created you had tried you
before He created you (for this worldly life),

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فوَجَدَكِ لِمَا امْتَحَنَكِ صابِرَةً
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Fawajadaki lima imtahanaki sabirat,*
and thus He found you successfully enduring in that trial.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَزَعَمْنا اَنّا لَكِ اَوْلِياءُ وَمُصَدِّقُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Wa za’amna anna laki awliyaun wa mussaddiqun,*
We claim being loyalists, believers,

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَصابِرُونَ لِكُلِّ ما اَتانا بِهِ اَبُوكِ صَلَّى اللهُ عَلَيْهِ
وَآلِهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Wa sabiruna likulli ma atana bihi abuki, salla Allahu a’alayhi wa
alih,*
and standing as regards all that which has been conveyed to us by your
father—peace of Allah be upon him and his Household—

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَاَتى بِهِ وَصِيُّهُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Wa ata bihi wasiyuh,*
and all that which his Successor has brought to us.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَاِنّا نَسْأَلُكِ اِنْ كُنّا صَدَّقْناكِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Fa-inna nas’aluki in kunna saddaqnaki,*
We, thus, ask you, if we have really believed you,

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إلاّ اَلْحَقْتِنا بِتَصْديقِنا لَهُما
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Illa alhaqtina bitasdiqina lahuma,*
that you may include us with those who believe in them both,

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لِنُبَشِّرَ اَنْفُسَنا بِاَنّا قَدْ طَهُرْنا بِوَلايَتِكِ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Li-nubashshira anfusana bi-anna qad tahurna bi-wilayatiki.*
so that we may feel happy that we have been purified on account of our
loyalty to you.

It is recommended to add the following:

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ رَسُولِ اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*A salamu ‘alayki ya binta rasuli Allah,*
Peace be upon You; O daughter of the Messenger of Allah.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ نَبِىِّ اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*A salamu ‘alayki ya binta nabiyyi Allah,*
Peace be upon You; O daughter of the Prophet of Allah.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ حَبيبِ اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*A salamu ‘alayki ya binta habibi Allah,*
Peace be upon You; O daughter of the most beloved of Allah.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ خَليلِ اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*A salamu ‘alayki ya binta khalili Allah,*
Peace be upon You; O daughter of the Intimate Servant of Allah.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ صَفىِّ اللهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*A salamu ‘alayki ya binta safiyi Allah,*
Peace be upon You; O daughter of the Choice of Allah.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ اَمينِ اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*A salamu ‘alayki ya binta amini Allah,*
Peace be upon You; O daughter of the Trustee of Allah.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ خَيْرِ خَلْقِ اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*A salamu ‘alayki ya binta khayri khalqi Allah,*
Peace be upon You; O daughter of the best of Allah’s creatures.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ اَفْضَلِ اَنْبِياءِ اللهِ وَرُسُلِهِ
وَمَلائِكَتِهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Asalamu ‘alayki ya binta afdali anbiya-illah wa rusulihi wa
mala’ikatih,*
Peace be upon You; O daughter of the best of Allah’s Prophets,
Messengers and angels.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا بِنْتَ خَيْرِ الْبَرِّيَةِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Asalamu ‘alayki ya binta khayri al-bariyat,*
Peace be upon You; O daughter of the best of created beings.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا سِيِّدَةَ نِساءِ الْعالَمينَ مِنَ الاْوَّلينَ
وَالاْخِرينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Asalamu ‘alayki ya sayidata nisa-il-‘alamina minal-awwalina
wal-akhirin,*
Peace be upon You; O the Doyenne of all women of the world, including
the past and the coming generations.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا زَوْجَةَ وَلِيِّ اللهِ وَخَيْرِ الْخَلْقِ
بَعْدَ رَسُولِ اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Asalamu ‘alayki ya zawjata walliyi Allah wa khayri al-khalqi ba’da
rasýli Allah,*
Peace be upon You; O the lady of the Intimate Servant of Allah and the
best of all created beings after the Messenger of Allah.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا اُمَّ الْحَسَنِ وَالْحُسَيْنِ سَيِّدَىْ شَبابِ
اَهْلِ الْجَنَّةِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Asalamu ‘alayki ya umma al-Hassani wal-Hussein sayyiday chababi ahli
al-janat,*
Peace be upon You; O the mother of alHasan and alHusayn, the two
masters of the youth of Paradise.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ اَيَّتُهَا الصِّدّيقَةُ الشَّهيدَةُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Asalamu ‘alayki ayyatuha assiddiqatu achchahida,*
Peace be upon You; O the veracious, the martyr.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ اَيَّتُهَا الرَّضِيَّةُ الْمَرْضِيَّةُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Asalamu ‘alayki ayyatuha radiyatu al-mardiya,*
Peace be upon You; O the content, the pleased.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسـَّلامُ عـَلَيْكِ اَيَّتـُهَا الْفاضِلـَةُ الزَّكِيـَّةُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*A salamu ‘alayki ayyatuha al-fadilatu zakiya,*
Peace be upon You; O the virtuous, the pure.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسـَّلامُ عـَلَيْكِ اَيَّتـُهَا الْحَوْراءُ الاْنْسِيَّةُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*A salamu ‘alayki ayyatuha al-hawra’u al-insiya,*
Peace be upon You; O the Paradisiacal human being.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسـَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ اَيَّتُهَا التَّقِيَّةُ النَّقِيَّةُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*A salamu ‘alayki ayyatuha taqiyyatu naqiya,*
Peace be upon You; O the pious, the immaculate.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ اَيَّتُهَا الْمحَدَّثَةُ الْعَليمَةُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*A salamu ‘alayki ayyatuha al-muhadathatu al-‘alima,*
Peace be upon You; O the talked by the angels, the knowledgeable.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ اَيَّتُهَا الْمَظْلُومَةُ الْمَغْصُوبَةُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*A salamu ‘alayki ayyatuha al-mathlumatu al-marsoba,*
Peace be upon You; O the oppressed lady whose right was usurped.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ اَيَّتُهَا الْمُضْطَهَدَةُ الْمَقْهُورَةُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*A salamu ‘alayki ayyatuha al-mudtahadatu al-maqhura,*
Peace be upon You; O the persecuted, the maltreated.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكِ يا فاطِمَةُ بِنْتَ رَسُولِ اللهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*A salamu ‘alayki ya Fatimatu binta rasuli Allah,*
Peace be upon You; O Fatimah, daughterof the Messenger of Allah.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَرَحْمَةُ اللهِ وَبَرَكاتُهُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Wa rahmatu Allahi wa barakatuhu,*
So be upon you the mercy and blessings of Allah

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
صَلَّى اللهُ عَلَيْكِ وَعَلى رُوحِكِ وَبَدَنِكِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*salla Allahu ‘alayki wa ‘ala ruhiki wa badaniki,*
May Allah bless you, your soul, and your body.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَشْهَدُ اَنَّكِ مَضَيْتِ عَلى بَيِّنَة مِنْ رَبِّكِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*ashhadu annaki madayti ‘ala bayinatin min rabbiki,*
I bear witness that you have spent your life with full awareness of
your duty towards your Lord;

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَاَنَّ مَنْ سَرَّكِ فَقَدْ سَرَّ رَسُولَ اللهِ صَلَّى اللهُ عَلَيْهِ
وَآلِهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Wa-anna man sarraki faqad sarra rasula Allah, salla Allahu ‘alayhi wa
alih,*
and (I bear witness) that he who pleases you will have pleased the
Messenger of Allah—peace be upon him and his Household

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمَنْ جَفاكِ فَقَدْ جَفا رَسُولَ اللهِ صَلَّى اللهُ عَلَيْهِ وَآلِهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Wa-man jafaki faqad jafa rasula Allah, salla Allahu ‘alayhi wa alih,*
and he who displeases you will have displeased the Messenger of Allah —
peace be upon him and his Household—

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمَنْ آذاكِ فَقَدْ آذى رَسُولَ اللهِ صَلَّى اللهُ عَلَيْهِ وَآلِهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Wa-man athaki faqad atha rasula Allah, salla Allahu ‘alayhhi wa
alih,*
And he who harms you will have harmed the Messenger of Allah
—peace be upon him and his Household—

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمَنْ وَصَلَكِ فَقَدْ وَصَلَ رَسُولَ اللهِ صَلَّى اللهُ عَلَيْهِ
وَآلِهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Wa-man wasalaki faqad wasala rasula Allah, salla Allahu ‘alayhhi wa
ælih,*
and he who respects you will have respected the Messenger of Allah
—peace be upon him and his Household —

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمَنْ قَطَعَكِ فَقَدْ قَطَعَ رَسُولَ اللهِ صَلَّى اللهُ عَلَيْهِ
وَآلِهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Wa-man qata’aki faqad qata’a rasula Allah, salla Allahu ‘alayhhi wa
alih*
and he who disrespects you will have disrespected the Messenger of
Allah—peace be upon him and his Household.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لاَنَّكِ بِضْعَةٌ مِنْهُ وَرُوحُهُ الَّذي بَيْنَ جَنْبَيْهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Li-annaki bid’atun minhu, wa ruhuhu al-lathi bayna janbayh,*
This is because you are an inseparable part of him (i.e. the Holy
Prophet), and you are his soul with which he lives.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اُشْهِدُ اللهَ وَرُسُلَهُ وَمَلائِكَتَهُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Uchhidu Allaha wa rusulahu wa mala’ikatahu,*
I ask Allah, His Messengers, and His angels to be the witnesses

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اَنّي راض عَمَّنْ رَضَيتِ عَنْهُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Anni radin ‘amman radiyti ‘anh,*
that I am verily pleased with him whomever you accept,

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ساخِطٌ عَلى مَنْ سَخِطْتِ عَلَيْهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*sakhitun ‘ala man sakhitti ‘alayh,*
and I am displeased with him whomever you detest,

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مُتَبَرِّىءٌ مِمَّنْ تَبَرَّأْتِ مِنْهُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Mutabari’un miman tabara’ti minh,*
and I disavow him whomever you disavow,

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مُوال لِمَنْ والَيْتِ، مُعاد لِمَنْ عادِيْتِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Muwalin liman walayti, Mu’adin liman ‘adayti,*
and I am loyal to him whomever you support, and I am the enemy of him
whomever you betake as enemy,

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مُبْغِضٌ لِمَنْ اَبْغَضْتِ مُحِبٌّ لِمَنْ اَحْبَبْتِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*mubridun liman abradti, muhibun liman ahbabti,*
and I am hateful to him whomever you hate,

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَكَفى بِاللهِ شَهيداً وَحَسيباً وَجازِياً وَمُثيباً.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Wa kafa bil-Lahi chahidan wa …asiban wa jaziyan wa muthiba.*
and I like him whomever you like. Verily, Allah is Sufficient as
Witness, Reckone Punisher, and Rewarder.

You may then pray Almighty Allah for sending blessings on the Holy
Prophet and Imams.
Within the recommended acts on the third of Jumada al-Akhirah (the
martyrdom day of Lady Fatimah al-Zahra’, s.a.), another form of
ziyarahof Lady Fatimah al-Zahra’ (s.a.) has been cited.

similar to the statements of the aforementioned ziyarah that has been
