Spilling Blood
==============

**Spilling blood سفك الدِّماء-**

1. Spilling blood without just cause invites the descending of divine
wrath and the cessation of blessings.

> 1ـ سَفْكُ الدِّماءِ بِغَيْرِ حَقِّها يَدْعُوا إلى حُلُولِ النِقْمَةِ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وزَوالِ النِّعْمَةِ.
  </p>
</blockquote>
