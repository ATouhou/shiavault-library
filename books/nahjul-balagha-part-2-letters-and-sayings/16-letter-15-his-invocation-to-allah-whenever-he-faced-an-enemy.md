Letter 15: His invocation to Allah whenever he faced an enemy.
==============================================================

*Made by Amir al-mu'minin when he used to face the enemy*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وكان (عليه السلام) يقول إذا لقى العدوّ محارباً:
  </p>
</blockquote>

O My God! Hearts are drawn to You, necks stretch (towards You), eyes are
fixed (on You), steps are in motion (towards You) and bodies have turned
lean. O My God! Hidden animosity has become manifest and the cauldrons
of malice are boiling.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللَّهُمَّ إِلَيْكَ أَفْضَتِ الْقُلُوبُ، وَمُدَّتِ الاْعْنَاقُ،
وَشَخَصَتِ الاْبْصَارُ، وَنُقِلَتِ الاْقْدَامُ، وَأُنْضِيَتِ
الاْبْدَانُ. اللَّهُمَّ قَدْ صَرَّحَ مَكْنُونُ الشَّنَآنِ، وَجَاشَتْ
مَرَاجِلُ الاْضْغَانِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

O My God! We complain to You of the absence of our Prophet, the
numerousness of our enemy and the diffusion of our passions.

*Our Lord! Decide between us and between our people with truth, and You
are the Best of Deciders.* (Qur'an, 7:89)

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّا نَشْكُوا إِلَيكَ غَيْبَةَ نَبِيِّنَا، وَكَثْرَةَ
عَدُوِّنَا، وَتَشَتُّتَ أَهْوَائِنَا.
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
(رَبَّنَا افْتَحْ بَيْنَنَا وَبَيْنَ قَوْمِنَا بِالْحَقِّ وَأَنْتَ
خَيْرُ الْفَاتِحِينَ)
  </p>
</blockquote>
