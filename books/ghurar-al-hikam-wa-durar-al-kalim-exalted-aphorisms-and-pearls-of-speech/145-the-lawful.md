The Lawful
==========

**The Lawful الحلال**

1. You should cling to the lawful, and to treating your family with
kindness, and to remembering Allah in all circumstances.

> 1ـ عَلَيْكَ بِلُزُومِ الحَلالِ، وحُسْنِ البِرِّ بِالعِيالِ،وَ ذِكْرِ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللّهِ في كُلِّ حال.
  </p>
</blockquote>
