Chapter 67: On the Reward of Visiting the shrine of Fatima - the Daughter of Imam Musa ibn Ja’far (a.s.) in Qum
===============================================================================================================

67-1 My father and Muhammad ibn Musa ibn al-Motawakkil - may God be
pleased with them - quoted on the authority of Ali ibn Ibrahim ibn
Hashem, on the authority of his father Sa’d ibn Sa’d to have said, “I
asked Abal Hassan Ar-Ridha’ (a.s.) about visiting the shrine of
Fatima[^1] - the daughter of Imam Musa ibn Ja’far. Imam Ar-Ridha’ (a.s.)
said, “The reward of whomever visits her shrine is Heaven.”

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قَالَ فَقَالَ لِي أَحْسَنْتَ وَشَفَعَ فِيَّ وَأَعْطَانِي ثِيَابَهُ
وَهَا هِيَ وَأَشَارَ إِلَى ثِيَابِ بَدَنِهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ذكر ما وجد على قبر دعبل مكتوباً
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 37- سَمِعتُ أبَا نَصْرٍ مُحَمَّد بن الحَسَن الكَرْخِيِّ الكاتِبُ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَقُولُ رَأيْتُ عَلَى قَبْرِ دِعْبِل بْنِ عَلِيٍّ الخُزاعِيّ
مَكْتوباً:
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>

  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَقُولُ مُخْلِصَاً عَسَاهُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللهُ مَوْلاَهُ وَالرَّسُولُ وَمَنْ
  </p>

<blockquote dir="rtl">

  </p>

زِيَارَةِ فَاطِمَةَ بِنتِ مُوسَى بنِ جَعفَر عَلَيْهِ السَّلامُ فَقَال
