38) His Supplication in Asking Pardon
=====================================

> (38) وَ كَانَ مِنْ دُعَائِهِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلَامُ فِي الِاعْتِذَارِ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مِنْ تَبِعَاتِ الْعِبَادِ وَ مِنَ التَّقْصِيرِ فِي حُقُوقِهِمْ وَ فِي
فَكَاكِ رَقَبَتِهِ مِنَ النَّارِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

His Supplication in Asking Pardon for Misdeeds to God's Servants and for
Falling Short in their Rights and that his Neck be Set Free from the
Fire

1- O God, I ask pardon from Thee for  
 the person wronged in my presence whom I did not help,  
 the favour conferred upon me for which I returned no thanks,  
 the evildoer who asked pardon from me and whom I did not pardon,  
 the needy person who asked from me and whom I preferred not over
myself,  
 the right of a believer who possesses a right incumbent upon me which I
did not fulfil,  
 the fault of a believer which became evident to me and which I did not
conceal,  
 and every sin which presented itself to me and which I failed to avoid.

2- I ask pardon, my God,  
 for all of these and their likes,  
 with an asking of pardon in remorse  
 which may act as an admonisher  
 against similar things ahead of me.

3- So bless Muhammad and his Household  
 and make my remorse  
 for the slips into which I have fallen  
 and my determination to refrain from the evil deeds which present
themselves to me  
 a repentance which will make Thy love for me obligatory  
 O lover of those who repent![^1]

> 1. اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعْتَذِرُ إِلَيْكَ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مِنْ مَظْلُومٍ ظُلِمَ بِحَضْرَتِي فَلَمْ أَنْصُرْهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ مَعْرُوفٍ أُسْدِيَ إِلَيَّ فَلَمْ أَشْكُرْهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ مُسِي‏ءٍ اعْتَذَرَ إِلَيَّ فَلَمْ أَعْذِرْهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ ذِي فَاقَةٍ سَأَلَنِي فَلَمْ أُوثِرْهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ حَقِّ ذِي حَقٍّ لَزِمَنِي لِمُؤْمِنٍ فَلَمْ أُوَفِّرْهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ عَيْبِ مُؤْمِنٍ ظَهَرَ لِي فَلَمْ أَسْتُرْهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ كُلِّ إِثْمٍ عَرَضَ لِي فَلَمْ أَهْجُرْهُ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 2. أَعْتَذِرُ إِلَيْكَ يَا إِلَهِي

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مِنْهُنَّ وَ مِنْ نَظَائِرِهِنَّ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اعْتِذَارَ نَدَامَةٍ يَكُونُ وَاعِظاً
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لِمَا بَيْنَ يَدَيَّ مِنْ أَشْبَاهِهِنَّ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 3. فَصَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اجْعَلْ نَدَامَتِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَلَى مَا وَقَعْتُ فِيهِ مِنَ الزَّلَّاتِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ عَزْمِي عَلَى تَرْكِ مَا يَعْرِضُ لِي مِنَ السَّيِّئَاتِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
تَوْبَةً تُوجِبُ لِي مَحَبَّتَكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَا مُحِبَّ التَّوَّابِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

[^1]: Allusion to 2:222: Truly God loves those who repent.
