Surah Al-A’ala, n. 87
=====================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
سَبِّحِ اسْمَ رَبِّكَ الْأَعْلَى 
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Glorify the name of your Lord, the Most High (87:1)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الَّذِي خَلَقَ فَسَوَّىٰ 
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Who creates, then makes complete (87:2)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَالَّذِي قَدَّرَ فَهَدَىٰ 
  </p>
</blockquote>

***And Who makes (things) according to a measure, then guides (them to
their goal) (87:3)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَالَّذِي أَخْرَجَ الْمَرْعَىٰ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***And Who brings forth herbage (87:4)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَجَعَلَهُ غُثَاءً أَحْوَىٰ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Then makes it dried up, dust-colored (87:5)***
