Ziyarah of Lady Fatima
======================

*Translated by: Badr Shahin***([1])**

While standing in the area between the Holy Prophet’s tomb and minbar
(i.e. Garden of Paradise – *Rawdah*), you may visit the tomb of Lady
Fātimah al-Zahrā' (a.s) though there is disagreement about the place of
her tomb. Some say that she was buried in the *Rawdah*. Others say that
she was buried at her house. Others say that she was buried in the
Baqī\` Cemetery.

However, the majority of our scholars agree that she must be visited
from the *Rawdah*, although to visit her at these three places is more
preferable.

When you stand for the *ziyārah* of Lady Fātimah al-Zahrā'’s tomb, you
may say the following:

O the carefully examined one:

yā mumtahanatu

يَا مُمْتَحَنَةُ

Allah Who created you had tried you before He created you (for this
worldly life),

imtahanaki allāhu alladhy khalaqaki qabla an yakhluqaki

اِمْتَحَنَكِ اللّهُ الّذِي خَلَقَكِ قَبْلَ أَنْ يَخْلُقَكِ

and thus He found you successfully enduring in that trial.

fawajadaki limā imtahanaki sābiratan,

فَوَجَدَكِ لِمَا امْتَحَنَكِ صَابِرَةً،
