Seeking Justice
===============

**Seeking justice-الاِنتصاف**

1. The virtuous one does not seek justice from the vicious.

> 1ـ لا يَنْتَصِفُ الْبَرُّ مِنَ الفاجِرِ.

2. A scholar does not seek justice from an ignorant person.

> 2ـ لا يَنْـتَصِفُ عالِمٌ مِنْ جاهِل.

3. The noble person does not seek justice from the wicked.

> 3ـ لا يَنْـتَصِفُ الكَريمُ مِنَ اللَّئِيمِ.

4. Justice can never be sought from the foolish one except by being
forbearing with him.

> 4ـ لا يُنْـتَصَفُ مِنْ سَفِيه قَطُّ إلاّ بِالحِلْمِ عَنْهُ.
