40. Prayers and Supplications  
===============================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَنْ أَبِي عَبْدِ اللٌّهِ (ع) قَالَ: إِنَّ اللٌّهَ فَرَضَ عَلَيْكُمُ
الصَّلَوَاتِ الْخَمْسَ فِي أَفْضَلِ السَّاعَاتِ فَعَلَيْكُمْ
بِالدُّعَاءِ فِي إِدْبَارِ الصَّلَوَاتِ.
  </p>

Imam As-Sadiq (peace be upon him) has said: “Verily, Allah has made
obligatory upon you the five prayers at timings that are the best of
times, so it is important that you supplicate to Him at the end of these
prayers.”** **  
 Al-Khisal, Volume 1, Page 278
