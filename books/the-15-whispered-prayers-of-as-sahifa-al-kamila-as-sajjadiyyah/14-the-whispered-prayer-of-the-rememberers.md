The Whispered Prayer of the Rememberers
=======================================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم
  </p>
</blockquote>

*In the Name of ALLAH, the All-compassionate*

*the All-merciful*

BIS-MIL-LAAHIR-RAH'-MAANIR-RAH'EEM

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللّهُمّ صَلّ عَلَى مُحَمّدٍ وَآلِ مُحَمّد
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Allah, send blessings on Muhammad and on the family of Muhammad,*
ALLAHUMMA S'ALE A'LAA MUHAMMAD WA AALE MUHAMMAD

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إلهِي لَوْلا الْواجِبُ مِنْ قَبُولِ أمْرِكَ لَنَزَّهْتُكَ مِنْ ذِكْرِي
إيَّاكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God, were it not incumbent for me to obey your command, I would have
declared you far too grand for me to remember you,*

ILAAHEE LAW-LAAL-WAAJIBU MIN QABOOLI AM-RIKA LANAZ-ZAH-TUKA MIN
D'IK-REEE EE-YAAKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَلى أَنَّ ذِكْرِي لَكَ بِقَدْرِي، لا بِقَدْرِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*For when I remember you I do so in my (small) measure, not in the
(glorious)measure that you* *deserve*

A'LAAA AN-NA D'IK-REE LAKA BIQAD-REE LAA BIQAD-RIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَما عَسى أَنْ يَبْلُغَ مِقْدارِي، حَتّى أُجْعَلَ مَحَلاًّ
لِتَقْدِيسِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and my limited capacity makes it difficult for me to comprehend the
depth of your dignified and holy names*

WA MAA A'SAAA AY-YAB-LUGHA MIQ-DAAREE H'AT-TAAA AJ-A'LA
MAH'AL-LAL-LITAQ-DEESIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمِنْ أَعْظَمِ النِّعَمِ عَلَيْنا
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And amongst your greatest favors on me is-*

WA MIN AA'-Z'AMIN-NIA'MI A'LAY-NAA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
جَرَيانُ ذِكْرِكَ عَلى أَلْسِنَتِنَا،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Giving me the ability and permission to recite your holy remembrance
across my tongue*

JARAYAANU D'IK-RIKA A'LAAA AL-SINATINA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَإذْنُكَ لَنا بِدُعآئِكَ، وَتَنْزِيهِكَ وَتَسْبِيحِكَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and giving me permission to supplicate to you, and to glorify you and
to call you holy!*

WA ID'-NUKA LANAA BIDUA'AA-IKA WA TANZEEHIKA WA TAS-BEEH'IK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إلهِي فَأَلْهِمْنا ذِكْرَكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God, inspire me with your remembrance*

ILAAHEE FAAL-HIM-NAA D'IK-RAK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فِي الْخَلاءِ وَالْمَلاءِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*When I am alone and also when I am in public,*

FEEL-KHALAAA-I WAL-MALAAA*-*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَاللَّيْلِ وَالنَّهارِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And by night and day,*

WAL-LAY-LI WAN-NAHAAR

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَالإِعْلانِ وَالإِسْرارِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And in public and in secret*

WAL-IA'-LAANI WAL-IS-RAAR

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَفِي السَّرَّآءِ وَالضَّرَّآءِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And in prosperity and adversity!*

WA FIS-SAR-RAAA-I WAZ''-Z''AR-RAAA-I

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَآنِسْنا بِالذِّكْرِ الْخَفِيِّ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*permit me to have intimate moments with you, in silent remembrance,*

WA AAANIS-NAA BID'-D'IK-RIL-KHAFEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَاسْتَعْمِلْنا بِالْعَمَلِ الزَّكِيِّ، وَالسَّعْي الْمَرْضِيِّ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Occupy me in doing pure work and efforts which are pleasing to you*

WAS-TAA'-MIL-NAA BIL-A'MALIZ-ZAKEE-YI WAS-SAA'-YIL-MAR-Z''EE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَجازِنا بِالْمِيزانِ الَوَفِيِّ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*and reward me with the full weight of your scale*

WA JAAZINAA BIL-MEEZAANIL-WAFEE

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِكَ هامَتِ الْقُلُوبُ الْوالِهَةُ، إلهِي
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God, hearts that have love are delighted beyond measure by you*

ILAAHEE BIKA HAAMATIL-QULOOBUL-WAALIHAH

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَعَلى مَعْرِفَتِكَ جُمِعَتِ الْعُقُولُ الْمُتَبايِنَةُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And minds that differ are brought together by knowing you,*

WA A'LAA MAA'-RIFATIKA JUMIA'TIL-U'QOOLUL-MUTABAAYINAH

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَلا تَطْمَئِنُّ الْقُلُوبُ إلاَّ بِذِكْراكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*hearts find no tranquility except in your rememberence*

FALAA TAT'-MA-IN-NUL-QULOOBU IL-LAA BID'IK-RAAKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَلا تَسْكُنُ النُّفُوسُ إلاّ عِنْدَ رُؤْياكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*souls are not at rest except when they see you.*

WA LAA TAS-KUNUN-NUFOOSU IL-LAA I'NDA ROO-YAAKA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَنْتَ الْمُسَبَّحُ فِي كُلِّ مَكان،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*You are the one glorified in every place,*

ANTAL-MUSAB-BAHOO FEE KUL-LI MAKAAN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَالْمَعْبُودُ فِي كُلِّ زَمان،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And the one worshiped at every time,*

WAL-MAA'-BOODU FEE KUL-LI ZAMAANIN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَالْمَوْجُودُ فِي كُلِّ أَوان،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And the one found at every moment,*

WAL-MAW-JOODU FEE KUL-LI AWAAN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَدْعُوُّ بِكُلِّ لِسان،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And the one called by every tongue,*

WAL-MAD-U'W-WU BIKUL-LI LISAAN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَالْمُعَظَّمُ فِي كُلِّ جَنان،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And the one magnified and respected in every heart!*

WAL-MUA'Z'-Z'AMU FEE KUL-LI JANAAN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَسْتَغْفِرُكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And so i ask forgiveness from you,*

WA AS-TAGH-FIRUK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مِنْ كُلِّ لَذَّة بِغَيْرِ ذِكْرِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*For every act of pleasure that I experienced in which I did not
remember you,*

*MIN KUL-LI LAD'-D'ATIM-BIGHAY-RI D'IK-RIK*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمِنْ كُلِّ راحَة بِغَيْرِ أُنْسِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And for every moment of ease that I had, which was not as a result of
being close to you,*

WA MIN KUL-LI RAAH'ATIM-BIGHAY-RI UNSIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمِنْ كُلِّ سُرُور بِغَيْرِ قُرْبِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And for every time of happiness that I enjoyed which was not as a
result of being near to you*

WA MIN KUL-LI SUROORIM-BIGHAYRI QUR-BIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمِنْ كُلِّ شُغْل بِغَيْرِ طاعَتِكَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And every moment that occupied me which was a result of not obeying
you!*

WA MIN KUL-LI SHUGH-LIM-BIGHAY-RI T'AAA'TIK

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إلهِي أَنْتَ قُلْتَ وَقَوْلُكَ الْحَقُّ:
  </p>
</blockquote>

*My God, you have said—and your word is true—*

ILAAHEEE ANTA QUL-TA WA QAW-LUKAL-H'AQ-QU:

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا اذْكُرُوا اللهَ ذِكْراً كَثِيراً
وَسَبِّحُوهُ بُكْرَةً وَأَصِيلاً
  </p>
</blockquote>

***O you who believe, remember ALLAH with much remembrance and glorify
Him morning and in the evening!*** **(33:42-43)**

YA AY-YUHAAL-LAD'EENA AMANOOD'-KUROOL-LAHA D'IK-RANA KATHEERAW-WA
SAB-BIH'OOHOO BUK-RATAW-WA AS'EELA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَقُلْتَ وَقَوْلُكَ الْحَقُّ:
  </p>
</blockquote>

*And you have said—and your word is true-*

WA QUL-TA WA QAW-LUKAL-H'AQ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَاذْكُرُونِي أَذْكُرْكُمْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

*Remember Me, and I will remember you!*

FAD'-KUROONEEE AD'-KUR-KUM (2:152)

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَأَمَرْتَنا بِذِكْرِكَ، وَوَعَدْتَنا عَلَيْهِ أَنْ تَذْكُرَنا
تَشْرِيفاً لَنا وَتَفْخِيمَاً وَإعْظامَاً،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*You have commanded us to remember you, and you have promised us that
you will remember us- by which you will bestow us with dignity, respect,
and honor*

FAAMAR-TANAA BIDIK-RIKA WA WAAT-TANA A'LAY-HEEE AN TAD'-KURANA
TASH-REEFAL-LANAA WATAF-KHEEMAW-WA IA'-Z'AAMAA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَها نَحْنُ ذاكِرُوكَ كَما أَمَرْتَنا،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*So here we are, remembering you as You have commanded us!*

WAHAA NAH'-NU D'AAKIROOKA KAMAAA AMAR-TANAA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَأَنْجِزْ لَنا مَا وَعَدْتَنا
  </p>
</blockquote>

*So fulfill your promise to us*

FA ANJIZ LANAA MAA WAA'T-TANAA

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يا ذاكِرَ الذَّاكِرِينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Rememberer of those who rememberers!*

YAA D'AAKIRAD'-D'AAKIREEN

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَيا أَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِينَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

*O Most Merciful of the merciful!*

WA YAAA AR-H'AMAR-RAAH'IMEEN
