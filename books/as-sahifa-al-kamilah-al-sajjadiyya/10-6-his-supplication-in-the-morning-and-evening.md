6) His Supplication in the Morning and Evening
==============================================

> (6) وَ كَانَ مِنْ دُعَائِهِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلَامُ عِنْدَ الصَّبَاحِ وَ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْمَسَاءِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 1. الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي خَلَقَ اللَّيْلَ وَ النَّهَارَ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِقُوَّتِهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 2. وَ مَيَّزَ بَيْنَهُمَا بِقُدْرَتِهِ

> 3. وَ جَعَلَ لِكُلِّ وَاحِدٍ مِنْهُمَا حَدّاً مَحْدُوداً ، وَ أَمَداً
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَمْدُوداً
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 4. يُولِجُ كُلَّ وَاحِدٍ مِنْهُمَا فِي صَاحِبِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يُولِجُ صَاحِبَهُ فِيهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بِتَقْدِيرٍ مِنْهُ لِلْعِبَادِ فِيمَا يَغْذُوهُمْ بِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يُنْشِئُهُمْ عَلَيْهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 5. فَخَلَقَ لَهُمُ اللَّيْلَ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لِيَسْكُنُوا فِيهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مِنْ حَرَكَاتِ التَّعَبِ وَ نَهَضَاتِ النَّصَبِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ جَعَلَهُ لِبَاساً لِيَلْبَسُوا مِنْ رَاحَتِهِ وَ مَنَامِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَيَكُونَ ذَلِكَ لَهُمْ جَمَاماً وَ قُوَّةً ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لِيَنَالُوا بِهِ لَذَّةً وَ شَهْوَةً
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 6. وَ خَلَقَ لَهُمُ النَّهَارَ مُبْصِراً

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لِيَبْتَغُوا فِيهِ مِنْ فَضْلِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لِيَتَسَبَّبُوا إِلَى رِزْقِهِ ، وَ يَسْرَحُوا فِي أَرْضِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
طَلَباً لِمَا فِيهِ نَيْلُ الْعَاجِلِ مِنْ دُنْيَاهُمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ دَرَكُ الْآجِلِ فِي أُخْرَاهُمْ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 7. بِكُلِّ ذَلِكَ يُصْلِحُ شَأْنَهُمْ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يَبْلُو أَخْبَارَهُمْ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يَنْظُرُ كَيْفَ هُمْ فِي أَوْقَاتِ طَاعَتِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مَنَازِلِ فُرُوضِهِ ، وَ مَوَاقِعِ أَحْكَامِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لِيَجْزِيَ الَّذِينَ أَسَاءُوا بِمَا عَمِلُوا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يَجْزِيَ الَّذِينَ أَحْسَنُوا بِالْحُسْنَى .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 8. اللَّهُمَّ فَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ عَلَى مَا فَلَقْتَ لَنَا مِنَ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْإِصْبَاحِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مَتَّعْتَنَا بِهِ مِنْ ضَوْءِ النَّهَارِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ بَصَّرْتَنَا مِنْ مَطَالِبِ الْأَقْوَاتِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ وَقَيْتَنَا فِيهِ مِنْ طَوَارِقِ الْآفَاتِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 9. أَصْبَحْنَا وَ أَصْبَحَتِ الْأَشْيَاءُ كُلُّهَا بِجُمْلَتِهَا لَكَ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
سَمَاؤُهَا وَ أَرْضُهَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مَا بَثَثْتَ فِي كُلِّ وَاحِدٍ مِنْهُمَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
سَاكِنُهُ وَ مُتَحَرِّكُهُ ، وَ مُقِيمُهُ وَ شَاخِصُهُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مَا عَلَا فِي الْهَوَاءِ ، وَ مَا كَنَّ تَحْتَ الثَّرَى
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 10. أَصْبَحْنَا فِي قَبْضَتِكَ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَحْوِينَا مُلْكُكَ وَ سُلْطَانُكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تَضُمُّنَا مَشِيَّتُكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ نَتَصَرَّفُ عَنْ أَمْرِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ نَتَقَلَّبُ فِي تَدْبِيرِكَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 11. لَيْسَ لَنَا مِنَ الْأَمْرِ إِلَّا مَا قَضَيْتَ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَا مِنَ الْخَيْرِ إِلَّا مَا أَعْطَيْتَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 12. وَ هَذَا يَوْمٌ حَادِثٌ جَدِيدٌ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ هُوَ عَلَيْنَا شَاهِدٌ عَتِيدٌ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِنْ أَحْسَنَّا وَدَّعَنَا بِحَمْدٍ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ إِنْ أَسَأْنَا فَارَقَنَا بِذَمٍّ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 13. اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ ارْزُقْنَا حُسْنَ مُصَاحَبَتِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اعْصِمْنَا مِنْ سُوءِ مُفَارَقَتِهِ بِارْتِكَابِ جَرِيرَةٍ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَوِ اقْتِرَافِ صَغِيرَةٍ أَوْ كَبِيرَةٍ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 14. وَ أَجْزِلْ لَنَا فِيهِ مِنَ الْحَسَنَاتِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَخْلِنَا فِيهِ مِنَ السَّيِّئَاتِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ امْلَأْ لَنَا مَا بَيْنَ طَرَفَيْهِ حَمْداً وَ شُكْراً
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَجْراً وَ ذُخْراً وَ فَضْلًا وَ إِحْسَاناً .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 15. اللَّهُمَّ يَسِّرْ عَلَى الْكِرَامِ الْكَاتِبِينَ مَئُونَتَنَا ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ امْلَأْ لَنَا مِنْ حَسَنَاتِنَا صَحَائِفَنَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَا تُخْزِنَا عِنْدَهُمْ بِسُوءِ أَعْمَالِنَا .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 16. اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ لَنَا فِي كُلِّ سَاعَةٍ مِنْ سَاعَاتِهِ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
حَظّاً مِنْ عِبَادِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ نَصِيباً مِنْ شُكْرِكَ وَ شَاهِدَ صِدْقٍ مِنْ مَلَائِكَتِكَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 17. اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ احْفَظْنَا مِنْ بَيْنِ أَيْدِينَا وَ مِنْ خَلْفِنَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ عَنْ أَيْمَانِنَا وَ عَنْ شَمَائِلِنَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ جَمِيعِ نَوَاحِينَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
حِفْظاً عَاصِماً مِنْ مَعْصِيَتِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
هَادِياً إِلَى طَاعَتِكَ ، مُسْتَعْمِلًا لِمَحَبَّتِكَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 18. اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ وَفِّقْنَا فِي يَوْمِنَا هَذَا وَ لَيْلَتِنَا هَذِهِ وَ فِي جَمِيعِ
أَيَّامِنَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لِاسْتِعْمَالِ الْخَيْرِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ هِجْرَانِ الشَّرِّ ، وَ شُكْرِ النِّعَمِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اتِّبَاعِ السُّنَنِ ، وَ مُجَانَبَةِ الْبِدَعِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ الْأَمْرِ بِالْمَعْرُوفِ ، وَ النَّهْيِ عَنِ الْمُنْكَرِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ حِيَاطَةِ الْإِسْلَامِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ انْتِقَاصِ الْبَاطِلِ وَ إِذْلَالِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ نُصْرَةِ الْحَقِّ وَ إِعْزَازِهِ ، وَ إِرْشَادِ الضَّالِّ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مُعَاوَنَةِ الضَّعِيفِ ، وَ إِدْرَاكِ اللَّهِيفِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 19. اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اجْعَلْهُ أَيْمَنَ يَوْمٍ عَهِدْنَاهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَفْضَلَ صَاحِبٍ صَحِبْنَاهُ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ خَيْرَ وَقْتٍ ظَلِلْنَا فِيهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 20. وَ اجْعَلْنَا مِنْ أَرْضَى مَنْ مَرَّ عَلَيْهِ اللَّيْلُ وَ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
النَّهَارُ مِنْ جُمْلَةِ خَلْقِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَشْكَرَهُمْ لِمَا أَوْلَيْتَ مِنْ نِعَمِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَقْوَمَهُمْ بِمَا شَرَعْتَ مِنْ شرَائِعِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَوْقَفَهُمْ عَمَّا حَذَّرْتَ مِنْ نَهْيِكَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 21. اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أُشْهِدُكَ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ كَفَى بِكَ شَهِيداً ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أُشْهِدُ سَمَاءَكَ وَ أَرْضَكَ وَ مَنْ أَسْكَنْتَهُمَا مِنْ
مَلائِكَتِكَ وَ سَائِرِ خَلْقِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فِي يَوْمِي هَذَا وَ سَاعَتِي هَذِهِ وَ لَيْلَتِي هَذِهِ وَ
مُسْتَقَرِّي هَذَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَنِّي أَشْهَدُ أَنَّكَ أَنْتَ اللَّهُ الَّذِي لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا
أَنْتَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قَائِمٌ بِالْقِسْطِ ، عَدْلٌ فِي الْحُكْمِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
رَءُوفٌ بِالْعِبَادِ ، مَالِكُ الْمُلْكِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
رَحِيمٌ بِالْخَلْقِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 22. وَ أَنَّ مُحَمَّداً عَبْدُكَ وَ رَسُولُكَ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
و خِيَرَتُكَ مِنْ خَلْقِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
حَمَّلْتَهُ رِسَالَتَكَ فَأَدَّاهَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَمَرْتَهُ بِالنُّصْحِ لِأُمَّتِهِ فَنَصَحَ لَهَا .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 23. اللَّهُمَّ فَصَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَكْثَرَ مَا صَلَّيْتَ عَلَى أَحَدٍ مِنْ خَلْقِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ آتِهِ عَنَّا أَفْضَلَ مَا آتَيْتَ أَحَداً مِنْ عِبَادِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اجْزِهِ عَنَّا أَفْضَلَ وَ أَكْرَمَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَا جَزَيْتَ أَحَداً مِنْ أَنْبِيَائِكَ عَنْ أُمَّتِهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 24. إِنَّكَ أَنْتَ الْمَنَّانُ بِالْجَسِيمِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الْغَافِرُ لِلْعَظِيمِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَنْتَ أَرْحَمُ مِنْ كُلِّ رَحِيمٍ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَصَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الطَّيِّبِينَ الطَّاهِرِينَ الْأَخْيَارِ الْأَنْجَبِينَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

1- Praise belongs to God, who created night and day through His
strength,

2- set them apart through His power,

3- and appointed for each  
 a determined limit  
 and a drawn-out period.

4- He makes each of the two enter into its companion,  
 and makes its companion enter into it,  
 as an ordainment from Him for His servants  
 in that through which He feeds them  
 and with which He makes them grow.

5- He created for them the night,  
*that they might rest in it*[^1]  
 from tiring movements and wearisome exertions  
 and He made it a garment for them that they might be clothed in its
ease and its sleep,  
 that it might be for them refreshment and strength,  
 that they might reach therein pleasure and passion.

6- He created for them the *daytime, giving sight,*  
*that they might seek* within it of His *bounty*,[^2]  
 find the means to His provision,  
 and roam freely in His earth,  
 searching for that through which  
 to attain the immediate in their life in this world  
 and to achieve the deferred in their life to come.

7-Through all of this He sets right their situation,  
 tries their records,[^3]  
 and watches their state in the times for obeying Him,  
 the waystations of His obligations,  
 and the places of His ordinances,  
*that He may repay those who do evil with what they have done*  
*and repay those who do good with goodness*.[^4]

8- O God, to Thee belongs praise for the sky Thou hast split into dawn
for us,[^5]  
 giving us to enjoy thereby the brightness of daytime,  
 showing us sought-after nourishments,  
 and protecting us from the striking of blights.

9- In the morning we and all things, every one, rise for Thee,  
 the heaven and the earth  
 and what Thou hast scattered in each,  
 the still and the moving, the resident and the journeying,  
 what towers up in the air and what hides under the ground.

10- We rise in the morning in Thy grasp:  
 Thy kingdom and authority contain us  
 and Thy will embraces us.  
 We move about by Thy command  
 and turn this way and that through Thy governing.

11- We own nothing of the affair  
 except what Thou hast decreed  
 and nothing of the good  
 except what Thou hast given.

12- This is a fresh, new day,  
 over us a ready witness.  
 If we do good,  
 it will take leave from us with praise,  
 and if we do evil,  
 it will part from us in blame.

13- O God,  
 bless Muhammad and his Household,  
 provide us with the day's good companionship  
 and preserve us against parting from it badly  
 by doing a misdeed  
 or committing a sin, whether small or great!

14- Make our good deeds within it plentiful  
 empty us therein of evil deeds,  
 and fill what lies between its two sides for us  
 with praise and thanksgiving,  
 wages and stores,  
 bounty and beneficence!

15- O God, ease our burden on the Noble Writers,[^6]  
 fill our pages for us with our good deeds,  
 and degrade us not before them  
 with our evil works!

16- O God, appoint for us in each of the day's hours  
 a share from Thy servants,  
 a portion of giving thanks to Thee,  
 and a truthful witness among Thy angels!

17- O God, bless Muhammad and his Household  
 and safeguard us from before us and behind us,  
 from our right hands and our left hands  
 and from all our directions,[^7]  
 a safeguarding that will preserve from disobeying Thee,  
 guide to obeying Thee,  
 and be employed for Thy love!

18- O God,  
 bless Muhammad and his Household  
 and give us success in this day of ours,  
 this night of ours,  
 and in all our days,  
 to employ the good,  
 stay away from the evil, give thanks for favours,  
 follow the Sunna's norms, avoid innovations,  
 enjoin good behaviour, forbid the disapproved,  
 defend Islam,  
 diminish falsehood and abase it,  
 help the truth and exalt it,  
 guide the misguided,  
 assist the weak,  
 and reach out to the troubled!

19- O God,  
 bless Muhammad and his Household  
 and make this  
 the most fortunate day we have known,  
 the most excellent companion we have accompanied,  
 and the best time in which we have lingered!

20- Place us among the most satisfied of all Thy creatures whom night
and day have passed by,  
 the most thankful of them  
 for the favours Thou hast done,  
 the firmest of them in the laws Thou hast set down in the Shari'a,  
 and the most unyielding of them toward the prohibited acts  
 against which Thou hast cautioned!

21- O God, I call Thee to witness  
 - and Thou art sufficient witness -  
 and I call Thy heaven and Thy earth to witness  
 and Thy angels and Thy other creatures who inhabit them  
 in this my day, this my hour, this my night, and this my resting
place,  
 that I bear witness that Thou art God,  
 other than whom there is no god,  
 Upholding justice,  
 Equitable in judgement,  
*Clement to the servants*,[^8]  
*Master of the kingdom*,[^9]  
 Compassionate to the creatures,

22- and that Muhammad is Thy servant and Thy messenger,  
 Thy chosen from among Thy creatures.  
 Thou didst charge him with Thy message  
 and he delivered it;  
 Thou didst command him to counsel his community and he counselled it.

23- O God, so bless Muhammad and his Household more than Thou hast
blessed any of Thy creatures!  
 Give him for our sake the best Thou hast given any of Thy servants,  
 and repay him on our behalf better and more generously than Thou hast
repaid any of Thy prophets on behalf of his community!

24- Thou art All-kind with immensity,  
 the Forgiver of the great,  
 and Thou art more merciful than every possessor of mercy!  
 So bless Muhammad and his Household,  
 the good, the pure, the chosen, the most distinguished!
