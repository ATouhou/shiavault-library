Surah Yunus, n.10
=================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قُلْ مَنْ يَرْزُقُكُمْ مِنَ السَّمَاءِ وَالْأَرْضِ أَمَّنْ يَمْلِكُ
السَّمْعَ وَالْأَبْصَارَ وَمَنْ يُخْرِجُ الْحَيَّ مِنَ الْمَيِّتِ
وَيُخْرِجُ الْمَيِّتَ مِنَ الْحَيِّ وَمَنْ يُدَبِّرُ الْأَمْرَ ۚ
فَسَيَقُولُونَ اللَّهُ ۚ فَقُلْ أَفَلَا تَتَّقُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Say: Who gives you sustenance from the heaven and the earth? Or Who
controls the hearing and the sight? And Who brings forth the living from
the dead, and brings forth the dead from the living? And Who regulates
the affairs? Then they will say: Allah. Say then: Will you not then
guard (against evil)?(10:31)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَذَٰلِكُمُ اللَّهُ رَبُّكُمُ الْحَقُّ ۖ فَمَاذَا بَعْدَ الْحَقِّ
إِلَّا الضَّلَالُ ۖ فَأَنَّىٰ تُصْرَفُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***This then is Allah, your true Lord; and what is there after the truth
but error; how are you then turned back? (10:32)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
كَذَٰلِكَ حَقَّتْ كَلِمَتُ رَبِّكَ عَلَى الَّذِينَ فَسَقُوا أَنَّهُمْ
لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Thus does the word of your Lord prove true against those who
transgress that they do not believe*** ***(10:33)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قُلْ هَلْ مِنْ شُرَكَائِكُمْ مَنْ يَبْدَأُ الْخَلْقَ ثُمَّ يُعِيدُهُ ۚ
قُلِ اللَّهُ يَبْدَأُ الْخَلْقَ ثُمَّ يُعِيدُهُ ۖ فَأَنَّىٰ
تُؤْفَكُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Say: Is there any one among your associates***[^1] ***who can bring
into existence the creation in the first instance, then reproduce it?
Say: Allah brings the creation into existence, then He reproduces it;
how are you then turned away***[^2]***? (10:34)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قُلْ هَلْ مِنْ شُرَكَائِكُمْ مَنْ يَهْدِي إِلَى الْحَقِّ ۚ قُلِ
اللَّهُ يَهْدِي لِلْحَقِّ ۗ أَفَمَنْ يَهْدِي إِلَى الْحَقِّ أَحَقُّ
أَنْ يُتَّبَعَ أَمَّنْ لَا يَهِدِّي إِلَّا أَنْ يُهْدَىٰ ۖ فَمَا
لَكُمْ كَيْفَ تَحْكُمُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Say: Is there any of your associates who guides to the truth? Say:
Allah guides to the truth. Is He then Who guides to the truth more
worthy to be followed, or he who himself does not go aright unless he is
guided? What then is the matter with you; how do you judge?***[^3]
***(10:35)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَمَا يَتَّبِعُ أَكْثَرُهُمْ إِلَّا ظَنًّا ۚ إِنَّ الظَّنَّ لَا
يُغْنِي مِنَ الْحَقِّ شَيْئًا ۚ إِنَّ اللَّهَ عَلِيمٌ بِمَا
يَفْعَلُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***And most of them do not follow (anything) but conjecture; surely
conjecture will not avail aught against the truth; surely Allah is
cognizant of what they do (10:36)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَلَا إِنَّ لِلَّهِ مَنْ فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَنْ فِي الْأَرْضِ ۗ
وَمَا يَتَّبِعُ الَّذِينَ يَدْعُونَ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ شُرَكَاءَ ۚ
إِنْ يَتَّبِعُونَ إِلَّا الظَّنَّ وَإِنْ هُمْ إِلَّا يَخْرُصُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Now, surely, whatever is in the heavens and whatever is in the earth
is Allah's; and they do not (really) follow any associates, who call on
others besides Allah; they do not follow (anything) but
conjectures***[^4]***, and they only lie (10:66)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قَالُوا اتَّخَذَ اللَّهُ وَلَدًا ۗ سُبْحَانَهُ ۖ هُوَ الْغَنِيُّ ۖ
لَهُ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ ۚ إِنْ عِنْدَكُمْ مِنْ
سُلْطَانٍ بِهَٰذَا ۚ أَتَقُولُونَ عَلَى اللَّهِ مَا لَا تَعْلَمُونََ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***They say: Allah has taken a son (to Himself)! Glory be to Him: He is
the Self-sufficient***[^5]***: His is what is in the heavens and what is
in the earth; you have no authority for***[^6] ***this; do you say
against Allah what you do not know? (10:68)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قُلْ إِنَّ الَّذِينَ يَفْتَرُونَ عَلَى اللَّهِ الْكَذِبَ لَا
يُفْلِحُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Say: Those who forge a lie against Allah shall not be successful
(10:69)***

[^1]: False gods and deities

[^2]: From the truth

[^3]: This is an open of the validity of the majority’s opinion for they
follow their own conjectures instead of following the most learned one
‘The A’lim’

[^4]: Baseless conjecture

[^5]: Al-Ghani

[^6]: What they forged
