33) His Supplication in Asking for the Best
===========================================

> (33) وَ كَانَ ، مِنْ دُعَائِهِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلَامُ فِي الِاسْتِخَارَةِ

> 1. اللَّهُمَّ إِنِيِّ أَسْتَخِيرُكَ بِعِلْمِكَ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَصَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اقْضِ لِي بِالْخِيَرَةِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 2. وَ أَلْهِمْنَا مَعْرِفَةَ الِاخْتِيَارِ ، وَ اجْعَلْ ذَلِكَ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ذَرِيعَةً إِلَى
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
الرِّضَا بِمَا قَضَيْتَ لَنَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ التَّسْلِيمِ لِمَا حَكَمْتَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَأَزِحْ عَنَّا رَيْبَ الِارْتِيَابِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَيِّدْنَا بِيَقِينِ الْمُخْلِصِينَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 3. وَ لَا تَسُمْنَا عَجْزَ الْمَعْرِفَةِ عَمَّا تَخَيَّرْتَ

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَنَغْمِطَ قَدْرَكَ ، وَ نَكْرَهَ مَوْضِعَ رِضَاكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ نَجْنَحَ إِلَى الَّتِي هِيَ أَبْعَدُ مِنْ حُسْنِ الْعَاقِبَةِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَقْرَبُ إِلَى ضِدِّ الْعَافِيَةِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 4. حَبِّبْ إِلَيْنَا مَا نَكْرَهُ مِنْ قَضَائِكَ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ سَهِّلْ عَلَيْنَا مَا نَسْتَصْعِبُ مِنْ حُكْمِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 5. وَ أَلْهِمْنَا الِانْقِيَادَ لِمَا أَوْرَدْتَ عَلَيْنَا مِنْ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَشِيَّتِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
حَتَّى لَا نُحِبَّ تَأْخِيرَ مَا عَجَّلْتَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَا تَعْجِيلَ مَا أَخَّرْتَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَا نَكْرَهَ مَا أَحْبَبْتَ ، وَ لَا نَتَخَيَّرَ مَا كَرِهْتَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 6. وَ اخْتِمْ لَنَا بِالَّتِي هِيَ أَحْمَدُ عَاقِبَةً ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَكْرَمُ مَصِيراً ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِنَّكَ تُفِيدُ الْكَرِيمَةَ ، وَ تُعْطِي الْجَسِيمَةَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ تَفْعَلُ مَا تُرِيدُ ، وَ أَنْتَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْ‏ءٍ قَدِيرٌ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

1- O God,  
 I ask from Thee the best in Thy knowledge,  
 so bless Muhammad and his Household  
 and decree for me the best!

2- Inspire us with knowledge to chose the best  
 and make that a means to  
 being pleased with what Thou hast decreed for us  
 and submitting to what Thou hast decided!  
 Banish from us the doubt of misgiving  
 and confirm us with the certainty of the sincere!

3- Visit us not with incapacity to know what Thou hast chosen,  
 lest we despise Thy measuring out, dislike the place of Thy good
pleasure,  
 and incline toward that which is further from good outcome  
 and nearer to the opposite of well-being!

4- Make us love what we dislike in Thy decree  
 and make easy for us what we find difficult  
 in Thy decision!

5- Inspire us to yield to that which Thou bringest upon us by Thy
will,  
 lest we love the delay of what Thou hast hastened  
 and the hastening of what Thou hast delayed,  
 dislike what Thou lovest, and choose what Thou dislikest!

6- Seal us with that which is most praised in outcome  
 and most generous in issue!  
 Surely Thou givest generous gain, bestowest the immense,  
 dost what Thou wilt,  
 and *Thou art powerful over everything*.[^1]

[^1]: 3:26
