Ziyarah Of Imam Al-Jawad (a.s)
==============================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ يَا أَبَا جَعْفَر مُحَمَّدَ بْنَ عَلِيٍّ الْبَرَّ
ٱلتَّقِيَّ ٱلإِمَامَ الْوَفِيَّ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O Abu -Ja‘far Muhammad, son of ‘Ali; the pious, the
devout, the guide, and the loyal (to Almighty Allah)

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ أَيُّهَا ٱلرَّضِيُّ ٱلزَّكِيُّ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the Pleased, the Pure

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ يَا وَلِيَّ اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the Intimate Servant of Allah

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ يَا نَجِيَّ اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the confidant of Allah

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ يَا سَفِيرَ اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the Envoy of Allah

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ يَا سِرَّ اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the secret of Allah

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ يَا ضِيَاءَ اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the Light of Allah

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ يَا سَنَاء اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the Brilliance of Allah

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ يَا كَلِمَةَ اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the Word of Allah

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ يَا رَحْمَةَ اللهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the Mercy of Allah

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ أَيُّهَا ٱلنُّوُرُ ٱلسَّاطِعُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the dazzling Light

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ أَيُّهَا الْبَدْرُ ٱلطَّالِعُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the rising full moon

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ أَيُّهَا ٱلطَّيِّبُ مِنَ ٱلطَّيِّبِينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the pure and the offspring of the Pure Ones

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ أَيُّهَا ٱلطَّاهِرُ مِنَ الْمُطَهَّرِينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the immaculate and the offspring of the Immaculate
Ones

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ أَيُّهَا ٱلآيَةُ الْعُظْمَىٰ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the Grand Sign (of Allah)

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ أَيُّهَا الْحُجَّةُ الْكُبْرَىٰ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the Greatest Argument

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ أَيُّهَا الْمُطَهَّرُ مِنَ ٱلزَّلاَّتِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the Purified from slips

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ أَيُّهَا الْمُنَزَّهُ عَنِ الْمُعْضِلاَتِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the cleaned from filths

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ أَيُّهَا الْعَلِىُّ عَنْ نَقْصِ ٱلأَوْصَافِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the elevated against imperfect attributes

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ أَيُّهَا ٱلرَّضِيُّ عِنْدَ ٱلأَشْرَافِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the pleased in the view of the notables

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ يَا عَمُودَ ٱلدِّينَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the Pillar of the Religion

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَشْهَدُ أَنَّكَ وَلِيَّ اللهِ وَحُجَّتُهُ فِي أَرْضِهِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

I bear witness that you are verily the Representative and the Argument
of Allah on His lands

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَنَّكَ جَنْبُ اللهِ وَخَيَرَةُ اللهِ وَمُسْتَوْدَعُ
عِلْمِ اللهِ وَعِلْمِ ٱلأَنْبِيَاءِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

And that you are verily the Duty and the Choice of Allah And you are the
trust of the knowledge of Allah and the Prophets

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَرُكْنُ ٱلإِيمَانِ وَتَرْجُمَانُ الْقُرْآنِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

And the support of faith and the traducer of the Qur’an

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَشْهَدُ أَنَّ مَنِ ٱتَّبَعَكَ عَلَىٰ الْحَقِّ وَالْهُدَىٰ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

And I bear witness that he who follows you is on the truth and true
guidance

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَأَنَّ مَنْ أَنْكَرَكَ وَنَصَبَ لَكَ الْعَدَاوَةَ عَلَىٰ ٱلضَّلاَلَةِ
وَٱلرَّدَىٰ أَبْرَأُ إِلَىٰ اللهِ وَإِلَيْكَ مِنْهُمْ فِي ٱلدُّنْيَا
وَٱلآخِرَةِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَٱلسَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكَ مَا بَقِيتُ وَبَقِيَ ٱللَّيْلُ وَٱلنَّهَارُ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

While he who denies and antagonizes you is on the wrong and perdition
and Peace be upon you as long as I am alive and as long as there are day
and night

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وقل في الصّلاة عليه :
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَللّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَىٰ مُحَمَّدٍ وَأَهْلِ بَيْتِهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

O Allah: (please do) send blessings upon Muhammad and his Household

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَصَلِّ عَلَىٰ مُحَمَّدِ بْنِ عَلِيِّ ٱلزَّكِيِّ ٱلتَّقِيِّ وَالْبَرِّ
الْوَفِيِّ وَالْمُهَذَّبِ ٱلتَّقِيِّ هَادِي ٱلأُمَّةِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

And send blessings upon Muhammad ibn ‘Ali. The pure, the pious, the
devout, the loyal, the respectful, the immaculate,

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَوَارِثِ ٱلأَئِمَّةِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

The inheritor of the Imams

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَخَازِنِ ٱلرَّحْمَةِ، وَيَنْبُوعِ الْحِكْمَةِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

The trust of mercy, the spring of wisdom

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَقَائِدِ الْبَرَكَةِ، وَعَدِيلِ الْقُرْآنِ فِي ٱلطَّاعَةِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

The guide to blessings, the match of the Qur’an in respect with
(obligatory) obedience

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَوَاحِدِ ٱلأَوْصِيَاءِ فِي ٱلإِخْلاَصِ وَالْعِبَادَةِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

The equal of the Successors in sincerity and worshipfulness

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَحُجَّتِكَ الْعُلْيَا وَمَثَلِكَ ٱلأَعْلَىٰ، وَكَلِمَتِكَ الْحُسْنَى
ٱلدَّاعِي إِلَيْكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Your supreme Argument, Your ultimate example, Your Excellent Word

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَٱلدَّالِّ عَلَيْكَ، ٱلَّذِي نَصَبْتَهُ عَلَماً لِعِبَادِكَ،
وَمُتَرْجِماً لِكِتَابِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

The caller to you, the guide to you, whom you have assigned as symbol to
your servants, as interpreter of Your Book

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَصَادِعاً بِأَمْرِكَ، وَنَاصِراً لِدِينِكَ، وَحُجَّةً عَلَىٰ
خَلْقِكَ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

As declarer of Your commands, as supporter of Your religion as argument
against Your creatures,

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَنُوراً تَخْرُقُ بِهِ ٱلظُّلَمَ، وَقُدْوَةً تُدْرَكُ بِهَا
الْهِدَايَةُ، وَشَفِيعاً تُنَالُ بِهِ الْجَنَّةُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

As light by whom You penetrate murk as pattern through whom true
guidance is attained, and as interceder by whom Paradise is gained.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَللّهُمَّ وَكَمَا أَخَذَ فِي خُشُوعِهِ لَكَ حَظَّهُ، وَٱسْتَوْفَىٰ
مِنْ خَشْيَتِكَ نَصِيبَهُ، فَصَلِّ عَلَيْهِ أَضْعَافَ مَا صَلَّيْتَ
عَلَىٰ وَلِيٍّ ٱرْتَضَيْتَ طَاعَتَهُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

O Allah: Because he showed piety to You at the furthest and took his due
of fear of You, (please do) bless him many times as much as You have
blessed any of Your servants whose obedience (to You) has been admitted
by You

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَقَبِلْتَ خِدْمَتَهُ، وَبَلِّغْهُ مِنَّا تَحِيَّةً وَسَلاَماً،
وَآتِنَا فِي مُوَالاَتِهِ مِنْ لَدُنْكَ فَضْلاً وَإِحْسَاناً
وَمَغْفِرَةً وَرِضْوَاناً، إِنَّكَ ذُو الْمَنِّ الْقَدِيمِ وَٱلصَّفْحِ
الْجَمِيلِ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

And whose servitude to You has been accepted by you. And (please do)
convey to him our greetings and salutations And confer upon us, through
our loyalty to him, with favors, benevolence, forgiveness, and
satisfaction of You are verily the Lord of Eternal Favors and Benevolent
Pardon.

Another form of Ziyarah of Imam Al-Jawad
----------------------------------------

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلى مُحَمَّدٍ ابْنَ عَلِيٍّ الإمام التَّقِيِّ
النَّقِيِّ الرَّضِيِّ المَرْضِيِّ وَحُجَّتِكَ عَلى مَنْ فَوْقَ
الاَرْضِ وَمَنْ تَحْتَ الثَّرى
  </p>
</blockquote>

O Allah: (please do) send blessings upon Muhammad ibn ‘Ali, the Imam,
the pious, the pure, the pleasing, the pleased, Your argument against
all of those who live on earth and all those who are under it.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
صَلاةً كَثِيرَةً نامِيَةً زاكِيَةً مُبارَكَةً مُتَواصِلَةً
مُتَرادِفَةً مُتَواتِرَةً، كَأَفْضَلِ ماصَلَّيْتَ عَلى أَحَدٍ مِنْ
أَوْلِيائِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

Blessings that are innumerable, growing, increasing, sacred, incessant,
consecutive, and uninterrupted in the best way as You have ever blessed
any of Your Intimate Servants

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَالسَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ ياإِمامَ المُؤْمِنِينَ وَوارِثَ عِلْمِ
النَّبِيِّينَ وَسُلالَةَ الوَصِيِّينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the guide of the believers, the heir of the
Prophets, knowledge, and the descendant of the Prophets Successors

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
السَّلامُ عَلَيْكَ يانُورَ الله فِي ظُلُماتِ الأرْضِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the Light of Allah in the murk of the earth

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَتَيْتُكَ زائِراً عارِفاً بِحَقِّكَ مُعادِياً لاَعْدائِكَ مُوالِياً
لاَوْلِيائِكَ فَاشْفَعْ لِي عِنْدَ رَبِّكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

I am visiting you, recognizing your Right (i.e. standing) Declaring my
enmity to your enemies and my affability to your followers; therefore,
intercede for me before your Lord.

Common Ziyarah of Imam Al-Kadhim and Imam Al-Jawad
--------------------------------------------------

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
السَّلامُ عَلَيْكُما ياوَلِيَّي الله
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the (two) Intimate Servants of Allah

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
 السَّلامُ عَلَيْكُما ياحُجَّتَي الله
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the (two) Arguments of Allah

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
 السَّلامُ عَلَيْكُما يا نُورَي الله فِي ظُلُماتِ الاَرْضِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the (two) Lights of Allah in the murk of the earth

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَشْهَدُ أَنَّكُما قَدْ بَلَّغْتُما عَنِ الله ماحَمَّلَكُما
وَحَفِظْتُما مااسْتُودِعْتُما
  </p>
</blockquote>

I bear witness that you (both) have conveyed the message that Allah
ordered you to convey And you kept that which has been entrusted with
you

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَحَلَّلْتُما حَلالَ الله وَحَرَّمْتُما حَرامَ الله
  </p>
</blockquote>

And you deemed lawful that which Allah has deemed lawful And you deemed
unlawful that which Allah has deemed unlawful

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
 وَأَقَمْتُما حُدُودَ الله وَتَلَوْتُما كِتابَ الله
  </p>
</blockquote>

And you undertook the directions of Allah And you recited the Book of
Allah

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
 وَصَبَرْتُما عَلى الاَذىْ فِي جَنْبِ الله مُحْتَسِبينَ حَتّى أَتاكما
اليَّقِينُ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

And you endured harm for the sake of Allah and depended upon Him until
death came upon you.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَبْرأُ إِلى الله مِنْ أَعدائِكُما وَأَتَقَرَّبُ
إِلى الله بِوِلايَتِكُما،
  </p>
</blockquote>

I declare my disavowal of your enemies before Allah And I seek His
nearness through my loyalty to you

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَتَيْتُكُما زائِراً عارِفاً بِحَقِّكُما مُوالِياً لاَوْلِيائِكُما
مُعادِياً لاَعْدائِكُما
  </p>
</blockquote>

I am visiting you, recognizing your Right (i.e. standing) Declaring my
affability to your followers and my enmity to your enemies

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مُسْتَبْصِراً بِالهُدى الَّذِي أَنْتُما عَلَيْهِ عارِفاً بِضَلالَةِ
مَنْ خالَفَكُما ؛
  </p>
</blockquote>

Committing to the guidance that you bear Being sure about the deviation
of those who defy you

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَاشْفَعا لِي عِنْدَ رَبِّكُما فَإِنَّ لَكُما عِنْدَ الله جاهاً
عَظِيماً وَمَقاماً مَحْمُوداً
  </p>
</blockquote>

So, (please do) intercede for me before your Lord For you, verily, enjoy
a great standing and a praiseworthy position with Allah

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
السّلامُ عَلَيْكُمَا يَا حُجّتَيِ اللّهِ فِي أَرْضِهِ وَسَمَائِهِ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

Peace be upon you; O the (two) Arguments of Allah on His lands and in
His heavens

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
عَبْدُكُمَا وَوَلِيّكُمَا زَائِرُكُمَا مُتَقَرّبًا إِلَى اللّهِ
بِزِيَارَتِكُمَا.
  </p>
</blockquote>

I, your servant and your loyalist, am visiting you and seeking Allah.s
nearness through my visiting you.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
اللّهُمّ اجْعَلْ لِي لِسَانَ صِدْقٍ فِي أَوْلِيَائِكَ الْمُصْطَفَيْنَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

O Allah: (please do) ordain for me a goodly mention among Your Choicest
Servants

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَحَبّبْ إِلَيّ مَشَاهِدَهُمْ، وَاجْعَلْنِي مَعَهُمْ فِي الدّنْيَا
وَالآخِرَةِ يَا أَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِينَ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

And make me long for their mausoleums, and include me with their group
in the worldly life and the Next Life; O the most Merciful of all those
who show mercy.
