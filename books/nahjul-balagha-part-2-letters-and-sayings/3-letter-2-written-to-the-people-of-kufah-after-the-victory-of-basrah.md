Letter 2: Written to the people of Kufah after the victory of Basrah
====================================================================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ومن كتاب له (عليه السلام) إليهم،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
بعد فتح البصرة
  </p>
</blockquote>

May Allah reward you, townsmen (of Kufah), on behalf of a member of your
Prophet's family, with the best reward that He bestows on those who act
in obedience to Him, and on those who thank Him for His bounties.
Surely, you heard (me) and obeyed, and when you were called you promptly
responded.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَجَزَاكُمُ اللهُ مِنْ أَهْلِ مِصْر عَنْ أَهْلِ بَيْتِ نَبِيِّكُمْ
أَحْسَنَ مَا يَجْزِي الْعَامِلِينَ بِطَاعَتِهِ، وَالشَّاكِرِينَ
لِنِعْمَتِهِ، فَقَدْ سَمِعْتُمْ وَأَطَعْتُمْ، وَدُعِيتُمْ
فَأَجَبْتُمْ.
  </p>
</blockquote>
