Surah At-Taghabun, n.64
=======================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يُسَبِّحُ لِلَّهِ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ ۖ لَهُ
الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ ۖ وَهُوَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Whatever is in the heavens and whatever is in the earth declares the
glory of Allah; to Him belongs the kingdom, and to Him is due (all)
praise, and He has power over all things (64:1)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
هُوَ الَّذِي خَلَقَكُمْ فَمِنْكُمْ كَافِرٌ وَمِنْكُمْ مُؤْمِنٌ ۚ
وَاللَّهُ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ بَصِيرٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***He it is Who created you, but one of you is an unbeliever and another
of you is a believer; and Allah sees what you do (64:2)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
خَلَقَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ بِالْحَقِّ وَصَوَّرَكُمْ فَأَحْسَنَ
صُوَرَكُمْ ۖ وَإِلَيْهِ الْمَصِيرُ 
  </p>
</blockquote>

***He created the heavens and the earth with truth, and He formed you,
then made goodly your forms, and to Him is the ultimate resort (64:3)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يَعْلَمُ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ وَيَعْلَمُ مَا تُسِرُّونَ
وَمَا تُعْلِنُونَ ۚ وَاللَّهُ عَلِيمٌ بِذَاتِ الصُّدُورِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***He knows what is in the heavens and the earth, and He knows what you
hide and what you manifest; and Allah is cognizant of what is in the
hearts (64:4)***
