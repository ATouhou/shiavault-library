9) His Supplication in Yearning
===============================

> (9) وَ كَانَ مِنْ دُعَائِهِ عَلَيْهِ السَّلَامُ فِي الِاشْتِيَاقِ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِلَى طَلَبِ الْمَغْفِرَةِ مِنَ اللَّهِ جَلَّ جَلَالُهُ
  </p>
</blockquote>

His Supplication in Yearning to Ask Forgiveness from God

1- O God, bless Muhammad and his Household,  
 make us go to the repentance that Thou lovest  
 and make us leave the persistence that Thou hatest!

2- O God, when we halt before two decreases,  
 in religion or in this world,  
 let the decrease fall upon that which passes quickly  
 and relent in that which lasts the longer!

3- When we set out after two concerns,  
 one of which makes Thee pleased with us  
 and the other of which displeases Thee,  
 incline us toward that which makes Thee pleased and weaken our strength
in that which displeases Thee!

4- Leave not our souls alone to choose in that,  
 for they choose falsehood  
 except inasmuch as Thou givest success,  
 and they command to evil  
 except inasmuch as Thou hast mercy![^1]

5- O God,  
 thou created us from frailty,[^2]  
 built us up from feebleness,  
 and began us from *a mean water*;[^3]  
 we have no force except through Thy strength  
 and no strength except through Thy help.

6- So confirm us by giving us success,  
 point us the right way by Thy pointing,  
 blind the eyes of our hearts  
 toward everything opposed to Thy love,  
 and set not in any of our limbs  
 passage to disobeying Thee!

7- O God, bless Muhammad and his Household  
 and assign  
 the whisperings of our hearts,  
 the movements of our members,  
 the glances of our eyes,  
 and the idioms of our tongues,  
 to that which makes incumbent Thy reward,  
 lest a good deed slip by us, through which we might deserve Thy
repayment,  
 or an evil deed remain with us, by which we might merit Thy punishment!

> 1. اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ صَيِّرْنَا إِلَى مَحْبُوبِكَ مِنَ التَّوْبَةِ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
و أَزِلْنَا عَنْ مَكْرُوهِكَ مِنَ الْإِصْرَارِ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 2. اللَّهُمَّ وَ مَتَى وَقَفْنَا بَيْنَ نَقْصَيْنِ فِي دِينٍ أَوْ
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
دُنْيَا،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَأَوْقِعِ النَّقْصَ بِأَسْرَعِهِمَا فَنَاءً ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اجْعَلِ التَّوْبَةَ فِي أَطْوَلِهِمَا بَقَاءً
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 3. وَ إِذَا هَمَمْنَا بِهَمَّيْنِ يُرْضِيكَ أَحَدُهُمَا عَنَّا ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ يُسْخِطُكَ الْآخَرُ عَلَيْنَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَمِلْ بِنَا إِلَى مَا يُرْضِيكَ عَنَّا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَوْهِنْ قُوَّتَنَا عَمَّا يُسْخِطُكَ عَلَيْنَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 4. وَ لَا تُخَلِّ فِي ذَلِكَ بَيْنَ نُفُوسِنَا وَ اخْتِيَارِهَا ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَإِنَّهَا مُخْتَارَةٌ لِلْبَاطِلِ إِلَّا مَا وَفَّقْتَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَمَّارَةٌ بِالسُّوءِ إِلَّا مَا رَحِمْتَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 5. اللَّهُمَّ وَ إِنَّكَ مِنَ الضُّعْفِ خَلَقْتَنَا ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ عَلَى الْوَهْنِ بَنَيْتَنَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ مِنْ مَاءٍ مَهِينٍ ابْتَدَأْتَنَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَلَا حَوْلَ لَنَا إِلَّا بِقُوَّتِكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَا قُوَّةَ لَنَا إِلَّا بِعَوْنِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 6. فَأَيِّدْنَا بِتَوْفِيقِكَ ، وَ سَدِّدْنَا بِتَسْدِيدِكَ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ أَعْمِ أَبْصَارَ قُلُوبِنَا عَمَّا خَالَفَ مَحَبَّتَكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَا تَجْعَلْ لِشَيْ‏ءٍ مِنْ جَوَارِحِنَا نُفُوذاً فِي مَعْصِيَتِكَ
.
  </p>
</blockquote>

> 7. اللَّهُمَّ فَصَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَ آلِهِ ،

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ اجْعَلْ هَمَسَاتِ قُلُوبِنَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ حَرَكَاتِ أَعْضَائِنَا وَ لَمَحَاتِ أَعْيُنِنَا ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَهَجَاتِ أَلْسِنَتِنَا
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فِي مُوجِبَاتِ ثَوَابِكَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
حَتَّى لَا تَفُوتَنَا حَسَنَةٌ نَسْتَحِقُّ بِهَا جَزَاءَكَ ،
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَ لَا تَبْقَى لَنَا سَيِّئَةٌ نَسْتَوْجِبُ بِهَا عِقَابَكَ .
  </p>
</blockquote>

[^1]: Reference to 12:53: Surely the soul of man commands to evil,
except inasmuch as my Lord has mercy.

[^2]: Allusion to 30:54: God is He who created you of frailty.

[^3]: 77:20
