Letter 76: Instructions to Abdullah b. Abbas when he sent him as his representative to Basra
============================================================================================

*Given to \`Abdullah ibn al-\`Abbas at the time of his appoint ment as
his Governor of Basrah*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ومن وصية له (عليه السلام)
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لعبد الله بن العباس عند استخلافه إياه على البصرة
  </p>
</blockquote>

Meet people with a broad face, allow them free audience and pass
generous orders. Avoid anger because it is a augury of Satan. Remember
that whatever takes you near Allah takes you away from the Fire (of
Hell), and whatever takes you away from Allah takes you near the Fire.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
سَعِ النَّاسَ بِوَجْهِكَ وَمَجْلِسِكَ وَحُكْمِكَ، وإِيَّاكَ
وَالْغَضَبَ فَإِنَّهُ طَيْرَةٌ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ. وَاعْلَمْ أَنَّ مَا
قَرَّبَكَ مِنَ اللهِ يُبَاعِدُكَ مِنْ النَّارِ، وَمَا بَاعَدَكَ مِنَ
اللهِ يُقَرِّبُكَ مِنَ النَّارِ.
  </p>
</blockquote>
