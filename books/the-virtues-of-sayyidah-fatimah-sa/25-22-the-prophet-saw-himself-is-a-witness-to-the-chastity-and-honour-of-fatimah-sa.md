22) The Prophet (saw) himself is a witness to the chastity and honour of Fatimah (sa)
=====================================================================================

> 61. عن عبد الله قال: قال رسول الله صلى الله عليه وسلم: إن فاطمة أحصنت
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فرجها، فحرم الله ذريتها على النار.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Abdullah bin Masud (ra) narrates that the Holy Prophet (saw) said,
“Fatimah has protected her honour and purity in such a way that Allah
(SWT) has forbidden the Fire for her children.”

> 62. عن عبد الله رضى الله عنه، قال: قال رسول الله صلى الله عليه وسلم:
<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إن فاطمة حصنت فرجها و إن الله عزوجل أدخلها بإحصان فرجها و ذريتها
الجنة.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Abdullah (ra) narrates that the Holy Prophet (saw) said, “Fatimah has
protected her honour and chastity in such a way that Allah (SWT) has
entered her and her family into Paradise because of her protecting her
honour and chastity.”
