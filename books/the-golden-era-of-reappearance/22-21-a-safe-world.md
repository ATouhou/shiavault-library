21.  A Safe World
=================

• In the government of Imam az-Zamaan (a.t.f.s.), peace and security
will permeate to all corners of the globe.[^1]

• Mischief mongers and evil doers will be annihilated.[^2]

• Corruption, waywardness and mischief will be completely routed.[^3]

• Roads will be safe even during the nights.[^4]

• Man will not be afraid for his life or wealth from man- eating, wild
and carnivorous beasts.[^5]

• Calamities will be removed from the earth and the servants of
Allah.[^6]

[^4]: (Surah Sabaa: Verse 18; Behaar al-Anwaar, vol. 52,p.338 & 345;

[^6]: (Al-Kharaaej, vol. 2, p. 931)
