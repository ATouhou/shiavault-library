Sural Al-Hajj, n.22
===================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَلَمْ تَرَ أَنَّ اللَّهَ يَسْجُدُ لَهُ مَنْ فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَنْ
فِي الْأَرْضِ وَالشَّمْسُ وَالْقَمَرُ وَالنُّجُومُ وَالْجِبَالُ
وَالشَّجَرُ وَالدَّوَابُّ وَكَثِيرٌ مِنَ النَّاسِ ۖ وَكَثِيرٌ حَقَّ
عَلَيْهِ الْعَذَابُ ۗ وَمَنْ يُهِنِ اللَّهُ فَمَا لَهُ مِنْ مُكْرِمٍ ۚ
إِنَّ اللَّهَ يَفْعَلُ مَا يَشَاءُ ۩
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Do you not see that Allah is He, Whom obeys***[^1] ***whoever is in
the heavens and whoever is in the earth, and the sun and the moon and
the stars, and the mountains and the trees, and the animals and many of
the people; and many there are against whom chastisement has become
necessary; and whomsoever Allah abases, there is none who can make him
honorable; surely Allah does what He pleases. (22:18)***

[^1]: ‘Sijdah’ i.e. Prostration here means obedience – subservience;
pays obeisance - Adores
