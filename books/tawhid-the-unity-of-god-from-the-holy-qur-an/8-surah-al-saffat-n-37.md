Surah Al-Saffat, n. 37
======================

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِنَّ إِلَٰهَكُمْ لَوَاحِدٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Most surely your Allah is One (37:4)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
رَبُّ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا وَرَبُّ الْمَشَارِقِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***The Lord of the heavens and the earth and what is between them, and
Lord of the easts***[^1] ***(37:5)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَاسْتَفْتِهِمْ أَلِرَبِّكَ الْبَنَاتُ وَلَهُمُ الْبَنُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Then ask them whether your Lord has daughters***[^2] ***and they have
sons (37:149)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَمْ خَلَقْنَا الْمَلَائِكَةَ إِنَاثًا وَهُمْ شَاهِدُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Or did We create the angels females while they were witnesses?
(37:150)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَلَا إِنَّهُمْ مِنْ إِفْكِهِمْ لَيَقُولُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Now surely it is of their own lie that they say (37:151)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَلَدَ اللَّهُ وَإِنَّهُمْ لَكَاذِبُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Allah has begotten; and most surely they are liars (37:152)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَصْطَفَى الْبَنَاتِ عَلَى الْبَنِين
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Has He chosen daughters in preference to sons? (37:153)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَا لَكُمْ كَيْفَ تَحْكُمُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***What is the matter with you, how is it that you judge? (37:154)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَفَلَا تَذَكَّرُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Will you not then reflect? (37:155)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَمْ لَكُمْ سُلْطَانٌ مُبِينٌ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Or have you a clear authority? (37:156)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَأْتُوا بِكِتَابِكُمْ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ صَادِقِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Then bring your book, if you are truthful (37:157)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَجَعَلُوا بَيْنَهُ وَبَيْنَ الْجِنَّةِ نَسَبًا ۚ وَلَقَدْ عَلِمَتِ
الْجِنَّةُ إِنَّهُمْ لَمُحْضَرُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***And they assert a relationship between Him and the jinn; and
certainly the jinn do know that they shall surely be brought up
(37:158)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ عَمَّا يَصِفُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Glory be to Allah (for freedom) from what they***[^3] ***describe
(37:159)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِلَّا عِبَادَ اللَّهِ الْمُخْلَصِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***But not so the servants of Allah, the purified ones (37:160)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
فَإِنَّكُمْ وَمَا تَعْبُدُونَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***So surely you and what you worship (37:161)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
مَا أَنْتُمْ عَلَيْهِ بِفَاتِنِينَ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Not against Him can you cause (any) to fall into trial (37:162)***

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
إِلَّا مَنْ هُوَ صَالِ الْجَحِيمِ
  </p>
</blockquote>

***Save him who will go to hell (37:163)***

[^1]: Imagined as gods or equal to God or imagined to exist

[^2]: The angels worshipped by the heathens as the daughters of God

[^3]: People in general
