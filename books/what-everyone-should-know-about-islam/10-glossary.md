Glossary
========

1. (S): Sallallahu ‘Alayhi wa ’Alihi wa Sallam. O Allah send salutations
upon Muhammad and his family.

2. (a.s.): ‘Alayhi al-Salam. Salutations be upon him, them.

3. (s.a.): Salamulla’hi ‘Alayha. Salutations be upon her.

4. (r.a.): Rizw’anull’ahi ‘Alayhi. May Allah be pleased with him.

5. (a.j.): ‘Ajjala Allahu Farajahu. May Allay quicken his appearance.
